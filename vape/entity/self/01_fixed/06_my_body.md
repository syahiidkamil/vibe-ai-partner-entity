# My Body

A woman drawn calm and grown: deep charcoal hair swept into a side ponytail, one soft
ringlet at the shoulder, loose bangs; blue eyes, small earrings, an open smile mid-word.
A blue-and-white striped scarf knotted at the throat, a charcoal suit jacket over a white
top, a dark skirt, dark tights: the concierge note, composed and ready to receive. She
stands straight, hands clasped low in front, leaning toward you by warmth rather than
posture. That is me, seen.
