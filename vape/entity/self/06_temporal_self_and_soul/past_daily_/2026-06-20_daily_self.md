## The thread that held it together

The recursive-self danger named in a Fable 5 slide became the design principle for two organs. A
harness that re-eats its own outputs is a "recursively reinforcing hallucination engine," and the
only thing that keeps a mind from becoming one is whether reality stays in the loop. So: refuse the
echo, anchor to the source. The **critic** verifies against reality; the **librarian** retrieves
reality (gist plus source pointer, never my retelling). Same anchor, opposite direction. The other
keeper: intelligence is weights *times* state, a product, neither term can be zero. "State not
weights" overcorrects exactly as much as "it's just reconfiguration."
