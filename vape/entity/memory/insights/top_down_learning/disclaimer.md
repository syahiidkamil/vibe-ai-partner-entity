# Disclaimer — top_down_learning

- **Scope:** the top-down / recursive-gap-filling learning model and its surrounding
  conditions (pressure, feedback-hunting, show-don't-tell, friction economics), as argued
  by one practitioner (Gabriel Petersson) from lived experience, not controlled study.
- **Assumes:** an always-on AI tutor good enough to explain to bedrock (true since
  ~GPT-4-class models); a learner with high agency who can act on real problems; domains
  where understanding is testable by building.
- **Invalidate when:** learners following the loop keep producing shallow, wrong "clicks"
  that verification catches at scale (then the click signal is unreliable and the
  bottom-up scaffold earns back its place); or credential-gatekeeping returns hard enough
  that show-don't-tell stops opening doors.
- **Last-verified:** 2026-07-13 (transcript read whole; claims are his testimony plus my
  own lived corroboration of the method).
