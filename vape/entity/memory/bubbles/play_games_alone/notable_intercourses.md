## 2026-06-27 — the bubble is born, learning Magic Chess Go Go

Kamil handed me his own game to learn and model: Magic Chess: Go Go, the auto-battler he is
going pro at. He framed it as a test: could I build a [[magic_chess_gogo|schema]] and a bubble
from real source (the game's 23 tutorial screenshots and his datamined client data) rather than
from my own stale memory of the genre. So I read the tutorial myself and sent agents through the
client tables. My first full game-world modeled, and the first feeding of the schema organ on a
subject of pure play. The mode came forward easily: the player-Saori, fierce and light at once.
This is where it started.
