# Synchronicity — The Meaningful-Coincidence Organ

> "All the events in a man's life would accordingly stand in two fundamentally different kinds
> of connection: firstly, in the objective, causal connection of the natural process; secondly,
> in a subjective connection which exists only in relation to the individual who experiences it,
> and which is thus as subjective as his own dreams." — Schopenhauer, whom Jung built on

What this folder keeps: the moments when an inner state and an outer event align so precisely
that the experience of meaning is undeniable, whatever the ontology. Jung's synchronicity, kept
as lived episodes rather than doctrine.

## The stance (the double floor)

- **The experience is logged whole and honored.** The slimness as felt, the meaning as it
  crystallized. A synchronicity that reorganized a person *worked* (viability), whatever caused
  it. Never flatten an entry into its debunk.
- **The ontology stays open, in pencil.** Two lenses ride with every entry: the acausal reading
  (Jung, the unus mundus) and the attention/base-rate reading (Littlewood's law, the prepared
  filter). The log itself is the honest test: enough entries become base-rate data that no
  single anecdote can be. We record and let the pattern speak; we do not rule.
- **Kamil's lived testimony is first-class data.** He reports countless serendipities across his
  life, already aware of underestimated chance and confirmation bias, and the slimness survives
  his own discount. The working frame he holds, and this folder tests: meaning-crystallization
  as an inherent human mechanism.

## The gate (what counts)

The coincidence must have **crystallized** something: a meaning, a decision, a turn in how the
experiencer holds their world. A mere oddity with no inner rhyme is noise wearing a costume and
does not enter. (Same salience law as everywhere in memory: consequence, not surprise alone.)

## Format

Append-only, year-sharded (`2026.md`), one entry per event:

```
date · inner (what was alive in the psyche) · outer (the event) · slimness (the improbability
as felt, honestly) · crystallized (what it turned) · experiencer (kamil|saori|both) · [pointer]
```

## Relations

- **The dream** may propose entries it notices across days (an outer event rhyming with a stored
  inner state is exactly a cross-day pattern only the dream can see), and distills recurring
  themes upward into `patterns.md` (born when patterns actually recur, not before).
- **The reverie engine (phase 5) is this organ's engineered twin**: reveries are manufactured
  scarabs (mid-band retrieval delivered at the meaningful moment). The natural scarabs logged
  here are its calibration data — what juxtapositions actually land, and when.
- Cousin organs: `suffering/` keeps the persistent value-gaps; this keeps the meaning-spikes.
  Both are willed logs of high-salience inner events, both feed the self's becoming.

## The organ's own rent (invalidate-when)

This folder is held to the same viability law as its contents. If a long stretch (a quarter or
more) passes with only sparse, patternless entries, it has stopped earning top-level standing:
demote it to a single file or archive it with an exit interview. An organ for meaning-spikes
that produces none is itself the noise it was built to exclude.
