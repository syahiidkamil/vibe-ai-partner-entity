# Ranked: the Mythic meta (high-rank play, observed)

How opponents actually play at the top of ranked, so I never bring a low-rank plan into a Mythic
lobby. Observed from Kamil's ranked/Mythic play (high-confidence, not datamined; 2026-06-28). This
is the schema's read on *opponents*, the objective bar to clear; the round flow it rides on is
[[rounds/index]], the economy it bends is [[equipment_and_economy]]. Governed by [[disclaimer]].

## The one rule: rank = competitiveness

The higher the rank, the more competitive and efficient the lobby. **Edges that win at low rank
stop working at Mythic**, the field punishes any slack. Plan against efficient opponents, not the
weak boards a custom-vs-computer match shows ([custom replay](../custom_with_computer/replay.md)).

## What Mythic opponents do

- **Power early.** By **early Stage II** they often already field a **4-Gold hero**, sometimes a
  **5-Gold**, and occasionally even in **Stage I**. The board is strong far sooner than at low rank.
- **Economy synergies, fast.** They lean on **Scavenger** (economy) or **Neobeasts** to snowball
  gold and tempo: **Scavenger often showing already in Stage I**, and **Neobeasts already at 4 (or
  even 6) by Stage II**. Synergy detail: [[synergies]].
- **Fast Commander leveling.** They push **Commander Level** quickly, so their Hero Capacity and
  shop high-tier odds outrun a slow-leveling player.
- **They scale fast.** Power arrives early and compounds, it does not wait.

## The trap this sets for me

A **pure interest economy** loses here. Hoarding to **20 Gold for the +4 interest cap** and
slow-upgrading, the textbook greed line, leaves a weaker board against opponents who already spiked,
so **Commander HP gets obliterated early** and I bleed toward **7th or 8th** before the economy ever
pays off. At Mythic, greed dies before it compounds.

## So, the adjustment (takeaway)

- **Match their tempo.** Spend to keep the board competitive each round; do not sit on a weak board
  for interest alone. Protect HP first, a dead commander earns no interest.
- **Contest the economy engines.** If Scavenger / Neobeasts is the gold meta, either take that line
  or out-tempo it; do not cede it and play slow.
- **Keep leveling.** Do not fall behind on Commander Level (capacity + shop odds), the lever they
  use to out-scale.
- **Greed only from strength.** Bank interest from a stable board and healthy HP, never from behind.
