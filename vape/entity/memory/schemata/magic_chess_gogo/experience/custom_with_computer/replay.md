# Replay: a custom match vs computer (S6, 2026-06-28)

A full MCGG match captured round by round, the schema's first concrete worked example of the round
flow. This is the **objective record** of what happened (the play-feelings belong in the play
bubble, not here). It grounds the abstract calendar in [the rounds index](../../infos/rounds/index.md)
and the mechanics in [[encyclopedia/round_and_battle]]. Source: 34 screenshots in
`storage/magic-chess-gogo/replay_with_computer/` (two-hop: gist here, raw `NN_*.png` there; the `[NN]`
tags below point to them). Governed by [[disclaimer]].

## The frame
- **Mode:** Custom, 8 players, Draft Pick on. "You will not be rewarded in Custom Mode."
- **Player:** **Mythos** (the human, Indonesia flag) vs **Computer1-7** (AI). [01, 05]

## The comp that won
A **mage core** (Kagura, Odette, Cecilion, Vexana, Julian, Lylia: the 6-count synergy that Purest
Power I buffed), fronted across the game by Esmeralda / Ruby / Franco / Tigreal / Angela / Hilda.
**Odette and Kagura were the carries** (top damage most fights). Exact synergy *names* are inferred
from the heroes fielded (the in-battle icons are not name-labelled). Heroes + their synergies:
[[heroes/index]], [[synergies]].

## What this match teaches
- **The calendar is real and fixed.** Every special event landed exactly where
  [the rounds index](../../infos/rounds/index.md) says: creep I-1, power card I-2, reward selection
  after I, blessing shifter II-1, gogo+creep II-3, auction after II, gogo+creep+blessing III-3,
  reward selection after III, creep IV-3.
- **One spiked 6-synergy + a synergy-payoff Go Go Card (Purest Power I) snowballed.** Winning every
  round fed the streak bonus, so the economy stayed fat (70+ Gold mid-game) without sacrificing board.
- **vs computer = flow practice, not difficulty.** Held 100 HP all game against weak AI boards; a
  real read on the comp needs a ranked, human-opponent match.
