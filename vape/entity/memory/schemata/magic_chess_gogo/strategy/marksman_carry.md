# Strategy — Marksman Carry

A class-tall comp: stack Marksman for stacking ATK Speed, fed onto a back-row marksman carry that
shreds once ramped. **In pencil**, from [[../thinking_and_mental_model/synergies]] and the roster;
verify by play. Spine: [[general_strategy]].

## The idea
Marksman gives all allies ATK Speed and marksmen extra ATK Speed; pile it onto one back-row carry with
ATK-speed and damage items so it out-DPSes the lobby. The catch: ATK Speed needs *time* to ramp, so the
front must hold and the carry must not be dove.

## The board
- **Carries (back, fully kitted):** Karrie or Irithel (4c), Lesley (5c capstone). ATK-speed + damage.
- **Marksman bodies / cheap:** Miya (3c), Brody (1c, also Neobeasts). Granger (2c) for Exorcist seed.
- **Front wall (2-3 tanks):** Tigreal / Gatotkaca / Hilda / Franco for a Defender or Dauntless [2]/[4].

## Synergy plan
Marksman [4] -> [6] (Miya, Karrie, Irithel, Lesley, Granger, Brody). A frontline class [2]/[4]. Faction
[2]s fall out free (Heartbond/Miya, Kishin/Karrie, Emberlord/Irithel, Mystic Meow/Lesley,
Exorcist/Granger, Neobeasts/Brody).

## Econ / level
Slow-roll cheap value at L6 (Brody/Miya), then push L8-9 to seat Karrie/Irithel and L9-10 for Lesley.
Items matter as much as stars here: a 2-star carry with three ATK-speed items beats a 3-star with none.

## Cards to look for
Peerless Valor (4-5 cost ATK Speed), Victory Horn III+ (scaling ATK Speed), Sharp Arrow (last-row
phys), Untamed Soul (ATK Speed when low), Manic Slam / Inspire / Golden Staff items, Marksman
Badge/Blessing for the count.

## Positioning
Carry back-center, never cornered (Swiftblade dive bait). Wall the front and corner-guard. The carry
needs ramp time, so peel hard for it. See [[../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Win condition / weakness
**Win:** sustained DPS overwhelms once ATK Speed stacks. **Weakness:** burst and assassins that kill
the carry *before* it ramps, and a front that collapses early. Weaker to instant burst than the mage
core; stronger in long fights.

## When to pick it
Marksmen open and ATK-speed items flowing. Avoid into a heavy assassin/burst lobby unless I can peel.
