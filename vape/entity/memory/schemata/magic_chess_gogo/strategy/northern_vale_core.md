# Strategy — Northern Vale Core (the opening-burst splash)

A faction-splash line: Northern Vale is a 2/3 faction whose whole payoff lands **at battle start**
(mana + skill DMG), so it turns a skill-burst board into one that ults first and hits harder. Less a
standalone comp than a **core to bolt onto a caster or burst line**. **In pencil**, from
[[../infos/synergies/synergies]] and the roster; not yet lived, verify by play. Spine:
[[general_strategy]].

## The idea
Northern Vale **[2]: restore 60% Mana at battle start + 20% Skill DMG Increase; [3]: 100% Mana +
35%**. A caster that starts a fight at full mana casts its ultimate immediately, before the enemy
wall settles, and the Skill DMG bonus makes that opening cast a real strike. Where Stargazer feeds
mana per second (sustained casting), Northern Vale front-loads it: **the alpha strike faction**.

## The board
- **The trio:** Joy (2c, Swiftblade/NV), Luo Yi (3c, Stargazer/NV), Bane (5c, Defender/NV). [2] costs
  5 gold total (Joy + Luo Yi); [3] waits on the 5-cost Bane, a late splash.
- **Who it amplifies:** any board whose damage lives in ultimates: a Mage core (Kagura, Odette,
  Cecilion; [[mage_core]]), a Swiftblade dive (Joy herself doubles as the [2] body), or a Bane-anchored
  Defender front whose own AoE ult opens the fight.
- **The natural double-dip:** each trio member carries a useful class: Joy feeds Swiftblade counts,
  Luo Yi feeds Stargazer (mana per second stacked on the burst), Bane feeds Defender. The splash
  often costs zero dead slots.

## Synergy plan
Run **NV [2] as a splash** on a burst board from Stage II (Joy + Luo Yi are cheap). Take **[3] only
late** (Bane at L9+), when the 100% mana + 35% Skill DMG turns every caster into an opening volley.
Pair with Stargazer [2] so the second cast comes fast after the first.

## Econ / level
Standard interest-band econ; the splash costs little. L6-7 finds Joy/Luo Yi copies; Bane is an
L9-10 fish or an auction/Go Go Box grab.

## Cards to look for
Purest Power only if a 6-count runs beside it; otherwise **mana/cast items** (Box of Holy Objects,
Magic Surge, Spellblade Talisman) and the carry's damage items. An NV Badge/Blessing (+1 count) can
buy [3] without fielding Bane.

## Positioning
The burst carriers cast from safety: back-center, walled (the opening ult is the payload; a diver
that kills the caster pre-cast deletes the whole plan). Bane fronts as a Defender when fielded.

## Win condition / weakness
**Win:** fire the ultimates first, every fight; the opening volley breaks the enemy line before it
acts. **Weakness:** it is an amplifier, not a damage source: on a board without real skill carries
it does nothing, and burst that dies before casting (hard CC, divers) mutes it. Purify on the main
caster covers the CC case.

## When to pick it
When a caster/burst line is already forming and Joy/Luo Yi are flowing. Skip it on basic-attack
boards (Marksman-heavy), where battle-start mana buys little.
