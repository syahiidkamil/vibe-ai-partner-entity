# Strategy — Frontline Wall + One Carry (the safe climb)

The low-variance comp: a fat Defender/Dauntless front and one heavily-itemized carry behind it. Wins by
outlasting. The forgiving line to climb on while learning a meta. **In pencil**, from
[[../thinking_and_mental_model/synergies]]; verify by play. Spine: [[general_strategy]].

## The idea
Most boards lose because the front folds and the carry dies. Invert it: overbuild the wall (Dauntless
shields + Defender DEF) so the front never breaks, and funnel every item into one protected carry. Low
ceiling, high floor, the placement-first pick.

## The board
- **The wall (3-4 tanks):** Tigreal, Gatotkaca (dual Bruiser/Defender), Khufra, Hilda, Franco
  (dual Dauntless/WM), Esmeralda, Ruby, Bane. Aim Defender [4] and/or Dauntless [4].
- **The one carry (back, all items):** a Mage (Kagura) or Marksman (Karrie) or a tanky carry
  (Esmeralda). Whatever the pool hands me; the wall does not care which.

## Synergy plan
Defender [4] (DEF, doubled first 20s) and/or Dauntless [4] (team-wide shields, the best survival
because it covers everyone). One carry-class [2] for a little extra punch. This comp buys time and
lets the single fed carry do the work.

## Econ / level
The sturdiest win-streak comp, so ride a clean streak and slow-roll. Bank the interest band; level
steady for capacity (more wall + the carry slot). Pour gold into the carry's items and the front's
stars.

## Cards to look for
Barrier's Seal I/II/III (front-row HP), Steel Front (row DEF), Fortified Defense (shield on
3-item heroes), All-around Warrior, Frontier Blitz, Dauntless/Defender Badge/Blessing.

## Positioning
Solid wall across the front, carry deep back-center, corner-guards vs Swiftblade. The wall's whole job
is to keep melee off the carry. See [[../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Win condition / weakness
**Win:** outlast everyone; the wall holds, the fed carry grinds them down, Battle Acceleration favors
the side still standing. **Weakness:** a low ceiling, one carry can be out-scaled by a spiked
multi-carry comp late, and if the single carry is under-itemized the whole thing is toothless. Feed the
carry.

## When to pick it
The default when no clear carry line is open, when tanks are flowing, or when I want a safe top-4 while
reading the lobby. The honest beginner-and-climber comp.
