# Strategy — Mage Core

A class-tall comp: stack Mage to [6] and spike it with Purest Power. It won the custom-vs-computer
replay ([[../experience/custom_with_computer/replay]]), but that was against weak AI (flow practice,
not a difficulty test), so treat it as a *sound structure*, not a battle-tested winner. **In pencil**,
grounded in [[../thinking_and_mental_model/synergies]] and the roster; verify against real opponents.
Spine: [[general_strategy]].

## The idea
Stack the Mage class so the carries hit on magic power, feed them mana with Stargazer bodies so they
cast often, wall them with a cheap frontline, and spike the 6-count with a Purest Power card. Mana is
the multiplier: the skill is the damage, so more casts = more damage.

## The board
- **Carries (back, fully kitted):** Kagura (4c) and Odette (5c capstone). Magic power + mana items.
- **Mage body / cheap vertical:** Cecilion (1c Mage) is the cheap-3-star carry; Lylia (2c), Vexana
  (4c), Julian (3c, dual Mage/Phasewarper) fill the class to [6].
- **Mana enablers:** Stargazer bodies (Aurora, Pharsa, Lunox) for the [2]/[4] mana regen.
- **Front wall (2-cost glue):** Tigreal / Gatotkaca / Hilda / Esmeralda / Franco for a Defender or
  Dauntless [2], just enough to buy cast time.

## Synergy plan
Mage [4] by mid Stage II -> [6] by Stage III. Stargazer [2] for mana. A frontline class [2] (Defender
or Dauntless). The Mage stack seeds faction [2]s for free (Exorcist via Kagura, Astro via Odette,
Emberlord via Cecilion, Enchanted Tales via Vexana, Mystic Meow via Julian) but do not chase those;
the class is the axis.

## Econ / level
Standard interest-band econ. Sit L6 to flood cheap mage copies (Cecilion 3-star early for a cheap
spike), then push L8-9 to fish the 4-cost mages (Kagura/Vexana) and seat Odette at L9-10. Win-streak
if the early board holds; otherwise bank and spike in III.

## Cards to look for
- **Purest Power I/II/III** — the 6-Mage spike, the signature payoff. Top priority once Mage hits 6.
- **Magic / mana items:** Box of Holy Objects (Glowing Wand, Ice Queen Wand), Spellblade Talisman,
  Magic Surge, Total Domination (Dominance Ice). Anything that makes casters cast.
- **Mage Badge / Blessing** for a +1 Mage count if one short.

## Positioning
Carries deep back, spread so one AoE cannot catch both. Wall across the front; protect Kagura/Odette
from divers (Swiftblade assassins) by walling the back corners.

## Win condition / weakness
**Win:** spiked 6-Mage + Purest Power deletes the enemy back line before Battle Acceleration.
**Weakness:** squishy front (needs the wall and protection), and mana-starved without Stargazer.
Hard-CC and assassins that reach the carries are the counter; position against them.

## When to pick it
When mage heroes are flowing and uncontested, and ideally on a Mage-friendly commander (Kagura's
shield umbrella, or a value/synergy commander). Avoid if two rivals are already on mages (the pool
cannot feed three).
