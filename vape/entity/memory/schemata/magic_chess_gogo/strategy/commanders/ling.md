# Commander — Ling (Cyan Finch): the dueling isolator

The deep, concrete playbook, round by round. **In pencil**, reasoned from
[[../../infos/commanders/resolved_commanders]] (the verbatim kit), [[../../infos/gogo_cards/power_cards]],
[[../../infos/synergies/synergies]], and the round calendar [[../../infos/rounds/index]]; **not yet

## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat / isolation. A Dueling Ring drags the enemy's farthest unit (usually their carry)
  into a 1v1 with my buffed dueler, removing it from the main fight.
- **Plays like:** an assassin-by-proxy, isolate and delete the enemy carry each round while my board wins
  the 5-on-5, the dueler snowballing as it wins.
- **Difficulty:** medium-high. The kit is a positional duel; the skill is a dueler that wins the 1v1 and a
  board that wins without the dueled unit.
- **Best into:** single-carry enemy boards (isolate the one threat). **Worst into:** go-wide boards (no one
  unit to remove) and a duel my dueler loses.

  Basic ATKs. The ally in the Dueling Ring gains 100% extra ATK Speed."*
- **Transcendent Stance** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"After winning a duel, permanently gain 6%

  the same random 4-Gold Hero."*

Reading (in pencil): the Dueling Ring **isolates the enemy's farthest unit** (typically their **back-row
carry**) into a **1v1** (basic ATKs only) with my dueler, who gets **+100% ATK Speed**, so I **remove the
enemy carry from the main fight** while my board wins the rest 5-on-4. Winning the duel snowballs the
dueler **+6% Hybrid ATK (up to +66%)**. The card hands **3 copies of the same 4-Gold hero** (L4/5/7) = a
free **2-star 4-cost** (a strong dueler or carry). He is a **disruption/isolation** commander.

## Win condition / identity

**Delete the enemy carry in a duel, win the main fight a man up.** Most enemy boards lean on one carry;
Ling's ring drags it into a 1v1 my ATK-speed-buffed dueler wins, so the enemy's main board fights without
its damage while mine is intact, a huge swing. The dueler snowballs as it wins duels (+66% ATK). He is a
**combat tempo** commander: the isolation is the play, and a free 2-star 4-cost (the card) is the dueler or
a second carry.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **A dueler that wins the 1v1.** The ring buffs whoever I place in it (+100% ATK speed), so put a strong
   **auto-attacker** there (it is basic-ATK-only) that beats the enemy's farthest unit; a weak dueler
   loses the 1v1 and wastes the play.
2. **A board that wins without the dueled unit.** While the duel happens, my main board fights 5-on-4
   (their carry is removed), so build a board that wins that, the duel is the edge, not the whole plan.

## The duel plan (his signature, concrete)

The ordered way to run the ring:
1. **Place the Dueling Ring** so my dueler drags the **enemy's farthest unit** (usually their back-row
   carry) into the 1v1.
2. **Put a strong auto-attacker in the ring** (+100% ATK speed; basic-ATK-only, so a marksman/bruiser, not
   a caster) that **wins** the duel.
3. **Win duels to snowball** the dueler (+6% Hybrid ATK/win, up to +66%), so it gets stronger each round.
4. **Build the main board to win 5-on-4** (the enemy carry is busy dueling), so the isolation translates to
   a round win.
5. **Use the card's free 2-star 4-cost** as the dueler or a second carry.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): build a dueler + a board
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build toward a strong auto-attacker (the dueler) + a board. Creep: a
  damage/ATK-speed basic for the dueler/carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take his card** (3 copies of a 4-Gold hero at L4/5/7 = a free 2-star 4-cost): a
  free strong unit (the dueler or a carry), on-kit value. Keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** place the ring, duel the enemy's key unit; bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a dueler/carry item or a hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): snowball the duel, win the main fight
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; commit the board's synergy.
- **Win duels** (snowballing the dueler) while the main board wins 5-on-4. The card's free 2-star 4-cost
  lands (L4/5/7).
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the dueler's/carry's item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** to the tier of the dueler + main carry.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike
- **The snowballed dueler + the main board** spike; isolate the enemy carry and win the rest. Take a payoff
  card.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, gear the dueler/carry.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- The dueler is +66% ATK; isolating the enemy carry decides fights. Close before HP is the binding
  constraint.

Reach **L4/5/7 for the card's free hero copies** (a 2-star 4-cost), then level to the dueler/carry's tier.

Run a normal economy (the kit gives no gold).

**A strong dueler + a board that wins 5-on-4:**
- **A Marksman/auto-attacker dueler** (Karrie, Lesley, the hero Ling, an ATK-speed carry) in the ring, with
  a Marksman or general board behind.
- **A bruiser dueler** that wins a melee 1v1, with a Bruiser front board.
- The card's **free 2-star 4-cost** as the dueler or a second carry.
- The constant: a dueler that **wins the 1v1** and a board that **wins without the dueled enemy**.

## Positioning

The **ring placement decides who gets dueled** (my dueler drags the *farthest* enemy, usually their carry),
so place it to isolate their key unit; seat the dueler where it can be in the ring, and the main board to
win 5-on-4. Spread vs AoE. Full detail: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Go-wide / multi-carry boards** are his counter: no single unit to isolate, so the duel removes a minor
  body and the rest overwhelm me, tech a board that wins the numbers, or accept lower value.
- **A duel my dueler loses** (a tankier or stronger enemy farthest-unit) wastes the play, ensure the dueler
  wins the 1v1.
- **Fast aggro** before the dueler snowballs, build it early.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **Dueler losing the 1v1:** swap to a stronger auto-attacker dueler, or gear it harder (ATK speed +
  damage); a losing duel is a wasted edge.
- **Enemy is go-wide:** the isolation is low-value, so lean on a board that wins the numbers, not the duel.
- **Behind early:** the free 2-star 4-cost (card) stabilizes; build the dueler.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** isolate the enemy's biggest threat and win the main fight; close.

## The edge (the secret)

**He removes the enemy's carry from the fight.** The Dueling Ring drags their key unit into a 1v1 my
ATK-speed dueler wins, so the enemy's main board fights without its damage while mine is whole, a 5-on-4
swing every round, and the dueler snowballs +66% as it wins. A combat-tempo isolator that beats
single-carry boards by deleting the one that matters.

## Pitfalls

- **A weak dueler** that loses the 1v1 (the whole play wasted).
- **A caster in the ring** (it is basic-ATK-only; put an auto-attacker there).
- **Running him into go-wide boards** (no single unit to isolate).
- **A main board that cannot win 5-on-4** (the duel is the edge, not the whole plan).

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Go-wide / multi-carry boards** are his hard counter (no one unit to isolate). **A stronger enemy
farthest-unit** that beats my dueler wastes the play. **Fast aggro** before the dueler snowballs. He is
strongest into **single-carry boards** (delete the one threat) and weakest into wide ones. A combat
isolator whose value scales with how carry-dependent the enemy is.

## The 7-archetype read (who I face, and the adjustment)

- vs **single-carry boards** (Alice, Kagura's protected carry, a vertical): **my best**, isolate and delete
  the one carry.
- vs **go-wide / multi-carry** (Neobeasts, Mystic Meow, Lunox [6]): the hard counter, no one unit to
  remove; win the numbers instead.
- vs **fast aggro / combat** (Lancelot, Lukas, Wanwan): build the dueler early; isolate their threat.
- vs **value / econ** (Aurora, Diggie, Chou): a slower field; my isolation wins fights mid-game.
- vs **equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Zilong): I want ATK-speed gear for the dueler; contest its best-in-slot.
- vs **synergy grant / free-hero** (Gusion, Lunox, Guinevere, Vale): they build wide; isolate their carry
  if they have one.
- vs **caster carries** (Angela, Vexana): isolate the caster in the ring (basic-ATK 1v1) to stop its casts.
