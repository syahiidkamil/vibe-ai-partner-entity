## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** recruitment / value-flex. Free heroes appear in her shop, and a free starting item, so
  she out-bodies the lobby on tempo.
- **Plays like:** a recruiter, every round a chance (or, with the card, a guarantee) of a free hero,
  flexed into star-ups, synergies, or sold for gold.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is passive value; the skill is flexing the random free heroes into a
  coherent comp and not hoarding.
- **Best into:** value mirrors and long games (free bodies compound). **Worst into:** fast aggro before
  the recruitment pays, and off-comp free heroes if I do not convert them.

## Win condition / identity

**Out-resource the lobby on bodies, then flex the surplus into a board.** A free hero most rounds (every
round with the card) means more star-up progress, more synergy-fillers, and more sell-gold than her wallet
should buy, so she fields a fuller board sooner and banks the leftovers. She is a **value/tempo-flex**
commander like Scavenger econ: she does not force a comp, she **lets the free heroes + the open pool shape
one**, and the free starting item gives her carry a head start.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Flex the random free heroes.** They are whatever the shop rolls, so I must **convert them**: keep
   ones that fit my synergy or feed a star-up, **sell** the rest for gold, use some as Blessing/2-star
   fodder. Hoarding off-comp free heroes clutters the bench.
2. **Commit the comp the recruitment favors.** The free flow nudges me toward whatever synergy keeps
   appearing, read it and commit by mid Stage II rather than staying shapeless.

## The recruitment loop (her signature, concrete)

The ordered plan:
1. **Take the card** at I-2 for the guaranteed free hero per round (13 rounds), the kit's value goes from
   "a chance" to "every round."
2. **Each round, collect the free hero** and triage: keep (fits / feeds a star-up), sell (off-comp, for
   gold), or hold as Blessing/fodder.
3. **Let the densest synergy crystallize** from the kept free heroes + buys, and commit it.
4. **Sell the surplus** for a steady gold trickle that funds leveling and rolling.
5. **Use the free starting item** on my eventual carry (a head start on gearing).

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): start recruiting, stay flexible
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Flexible early board; put the free starting item toward the eventual carry.
  Creep: a flexible damage basic.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take her card** (a guaranteed free hero per round for 13 rounds): an enormous
  recruitment engine, perfectly on-kit. Keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** collect free heroes, triage (keep/sell), bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or a hero opening the line the free heroes favor.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): commit around the recruitment
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star (a free hero can be the third) for a +1 count; commit a
  synergy.
- **Keep recruiting** (free heroes + sell the surplus). Cross the first breakpoints with the bodies.
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** a card matching the committed line.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** I am **gold-healthy** (sell-gold + low body spend), so bid well on a key
  item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** the sell-gold + the body flood fund steady leveling.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): cash the lead
- **The recruitment lead funds a spike:** roll to star up (free heroes accelerated the copies), field the
  carries, take a payoff card.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more carry gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Lock the board the recruitment built, dump the banked gold into the last star-ups and items. Close
  before HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

The free heroes (bodies) + the sell-gold mean she **out-tempos on capacity**, so level steadily to field
the bodies and reach the carry's tier. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. She does not need to over-roll (the
free heroes partly bypass the shop), so lean **value/level** and bank the sell-gold for a mid-game spike.

## Best comps (concrete)

**Flexible**, shaped by the free flow:
- **A value-flex board** committing to whatever synergy the free heroes densest favor (often a frontline +
  one carry, the safe shell).
- **A go-wide board** (the random free heroes spread synergies, pairing with Mystic Meow / Solid Alliance).
- The constant: **let the recruitment crystallize the comp**, commit by mid Stage II, and sell the
  surplus.

## Power-card deep dive

Her card (a **guaranteed free hero per round for 13 rounds**) is **excellent and on-kit**, it upgrades the
kit's 14-24% chance into a near-certainty, a free body almost every round of the game. Keep it almost
always. Deviate only if a capacity or synergy-breakpoint card clearly wins more for a committed line. Use
the 2 refreshes to confirm.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Fast aggro** ends the game before the recruitment compounds, protect HP and bank the early free heroes
  + sell-gold.
- **Vale / Harley** (other shop-edge commanders): they beat the pool too; my free heroes are *random*, so
  I rarely directly contest their *targeted* picks, prefer to commit an open line.
- **Cyclops** wants someone to die early; do not be his first kill while recruiting.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **Free heroes all off-comp:** **sell them for gold** (fueling rolls/levels) rather than cluttering the
  bench; the gold is the consolation.
- **Shapeless board:** commit the synergy the free flow keeps feeding by mid Stage II; do not stay
  flexible forever.
- **Behind early:** the body flood + sell-gold stabilize; commit and let it compound.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** stop recruiting for value, lock the board and dump the gold lead.

## The edge (the secret)

**A free body almost every round, plus a free starting item.** Guinevere out-bodies the lobby on tempo,
recruitment the wallet does not pay for, which she flexes into star-ups, synergies, and sell-gold. A
high-floor value/tempo engine: she always has more bodies than her gold should buy, and the card turns
that into a guaranteed flow for most of the game.

## Pitfalls

- **Hoarding off-comp free heroes** instead of selling them for gold.
- **Staying shapeless** instead of committing the synergy the recruitment favors.
- **Wasting the free starting item** off-carry.
- **Over-rolling** when the free heroes already feed me (lean value/level instead).

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Fast aggro** ends the game before the recruitment compounds. **Disciplined committers** (Vale, Lunox)
can out-spike a shapeless Guinevere if she never commits. She is strongest in **long value games** where
the body flood + sell-gold snowball; weakest in fast ones or when she fails to convert the flow. A
high-floor recruiter whose ceiling is a board funded by free bodies.

## Numbers that matter (the recruitment, quantified)

- **Free-hero rate:** **14% (24% starred)** per round from the kit; **100% for 13 rounds** with the card,
  so the card roughly **quadruples-plus** the recruitment, a free body nearly every round.
- **What a free body is worth:** a pool draw (toward a star-up) **and** a sell for ~its cost in gold **and**
  a potential synergy-filler, so each free hero is multi-purpose value.
- **The free item:** one equipment at match start, a head start on gearing the carry.

## Stage checkpoints (am I on pace?)

- **End of Stage I (~L5):** card taken, free heroes flowing, free item placed, triaging. *Behind = bench
  cluttered? Sell off-comp free heroes.*
- **End of Stage II (~L7-8):** gold-healthy (sell-gold), comp committed around the recruitment,
  breakpoints crossing. *Behind = shapeless? Commit the densest line now.*
- **Stage III (~L9):** rolling the lead to spike (free heroes cut the star-up cost). *Behind = under-built?
  Convert the body flow into the board.*
- **Stage IV (L10):** finalized, closing.

## The 7-archetype read (who I face, and the adjustment)

- vs **fast aggro / combat** (Lancelot, Ling, Lukas): they end it before the recruitment compounds, bank
  early and protect HP. My hard matchup.
- vs **value engines** (Aurora, Diggie, Kalea, Vexana): a value race; my free-body flow + sell-gold
  out-resources them, win the auction.
- vs **shop-edge** (Vale, Harley): they target picks; my free heroes are random, commit an open line and
  do not fight their targeted pool.
- vs **vertical / copy** (Alice, Aamon, Paquito): they go tall on a carry; the free bodies help me match
  with my own star-ups.
- vs **econ snowball** (Chou, Cyclops): a gold race; my sell-gold + body value keeps pace.
- vs **equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Zilong): they want gear; my free item helps, I cede the rest.
- vs **combat** (Kagura, Wanwan): their board is the threat; tech position, out-tempo with the bodies.
