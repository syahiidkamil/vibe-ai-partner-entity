## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** synergy commitment / econ snowball. One big synergy injection plus a gold windfall for
  hitting a 6-count.
- **Plays like:** pick an axis early, slam it with a free blessing + 3 free heroes + 6 gold, rush it to
  6, and cash a +24 gold exclusive that funds the spike.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a single high-impact cast plus an econ card; the skill is choosing a
  reachable 6-stackable axis and committing before the pool tightens.
- **Best into:** open, uncontested synergy lanes she can stack to 6 first. **Worst into:** a rival
  contesting her axis (a Vale, or another go-tall), and fast boards that end before her mid-game 6-count
  lands.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Lunox, "Twilight Goddess." Skins: Twilight Goddess, Fate Goddess. (Note: the **commander**
  Lunox is the player avatar, distinct from the **hero** Lunox, a 4-cost Stargazer/Kishin unit in
  [[../../infos/heroes/cost_4]]. This file is the commander.)
- **Power of Chaos** [Active] (verbatim): *"Usable after Stage I-2. Lunox selects a Hero without a
  Blessing and grants them a random Blessing. Can be used 1 time(s). Blessing: The Hero with the Blessing
  effect adds 1 count to their Role or Faction Synergy. Blessings granted by Lunox cannot be transferred
  using the Blessing Shifter."*
- **Power of Order** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"After Lunox casts her skill, gain 3 random
  Hero(es) from the Synergy it grants (affected by current Shop refresh probabilities) and 6 Gold."*
- **Power Cards:** exclusive *Lunox's Power* - *"Gain 4 Gold. When you first activate a Synergy to 6,
  gain 24 more Gold."* **(Exclusive)**; borrowable *Lunox's Power* - *"Gain 6 Gold. Select one of your
  Heroes without a Blessing to grant a random Blessing."*

Reading (in pencil): the active is **one** powerful synergy injection, a free **+1 synergy count** on a
chosen hero, and (with the passive) **3 free heroes of that synergy + 6 gold**. The 3 free heroes scale
with **current shop odds**, so casting at a higher level yields better bodies (a real timing lever). The
exclusive then pays **+24 gold for the first time any synergy hits 6**, a huge windfall *on a commander
built to rush a 6-count*. Net: she seeds and rushes one synergy faster and cheaper than anyone, and gets
paid for doing it.

## Win condition / identity

**Spike one synergy faster and cheaper than anyone, then cash the payouts.** The one-shot injection (a
+1 count + 3 free synergy heroes + 6 gold) gives a chosen axis a head start the pool cannot match, and
the +24g exclusive funds the next spike for *doing what she does anyway* (reaching a 6-count). Her game
is **picking a 6-stackable synergy axis early and committing hard**, then converting the gold windfall
into star-ups.

## The core tension (every round)

She is strongest **committed to one synergy line**, and her one Power of Chaos cast is a high-stakes,
single-use commit, so **which synergy I inject is a real decision**. Two sub-tensions:
1. **Which axis.** Because the exclusive pays +24g for a **first 6-count**, pick an axis I can
   realistically **stack to 6**, a class-tall line (Mage, Marksman) or a deep faction, not a shallow
   2/3-member synergy (Scavenger, Phasewarper, Mystic Meow) that caps before 6.
2. **When to cast.** The 3 free heroes scale with shop odds, so casting *later* (after a level or two)
   yields higher-cost free bodies, but casting *earlier* commits the axis sooner. Lean to casting when I
   have decided the axis and want the head start; do not sit on the cast so long the axis is contested.

## The one-shot injection (her signature, concrete)

Power of Chaos is used **once** (after I-2), so make it count:
1. **Decide the 6-stackable axis** from what the early shop and the field leave open.
2. **Cast Power of Chaos on a keeper hero of that axis** -> +1 count + **3 free heroes of it + 6 gold**.
   This is an instant down-payment on the synergy: a count and three bodies toward the breakpoint.
3. **If I draw the borrowable card** (the +6g blessing), that is my **second** blessing, point it at the
   same axis (another +1 count toward 6).
4. **Use the II-1 / III-3 Blessing Shifters** (the normal blessing system) to top the count, but note her
   *own* Power-of-Chaos blessing **cannot be moved by a Shifter**, so place it deliberately.
5. **Rush to 6, cash +24 gold, roll it into star-ups.**

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): read the axis, commit
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Read which synergy the early shop is feeding and **start committing** to a
  6-stackable one. Creep: a damage basic for the synergy's carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take the exclusive** (+4g now, +24g on the first 6-synergy). For a commander
  literally rushing a 6-count, this is one of the best econ cards in the game; the +24g is enormous.
  (Deviate only if I genuinely cannot reach a 6-count this game, then the borrowable +6g+blessing.)
- **After I-2, Power of Chaos:** cast on a keeper hero of the committed axis -> +1 count + **3 free
  heroes of it + 6 gold**, an instant head start. (If I want better free bodies, it can wait a round for
  a level, but committing the axis early usually wins.)
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** grab a Synergy Magic Crystal of my axis (+1 count toward 6) or a
  carry piece.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): drive the axis to [4]
- **II-1 (Blessing Shifter + Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a free +1 count; place it on the
  axis. Top the count with the Shifter.
- **Drive the axis to [4]** using the free heroes and the count boosts; it climbs cheaply. Keep
  everything on the one synergy.
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** a Synergy Badge/Blessing of my axis (Magic Crystal + hero = another
  count), or the borrowable Lunox blessing card.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for a **Synergy Magic Crystal** (another +1 toward 6) or my carry's
  gear.
- **Leveling:** level for **capacity** so I can field the [6] when it comes.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): hit 6, cash the windfall
- **Hit the 6-count** (cash the **+24 gold**) and take **Purest Power** (the 6-count payoff, +20/28% to
  those heroes) if offered, the card bias should favor my committed synergy.
- The +24g funds a **roll-down to star up the core**. This is the spike.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish the count or grab a synergy orb.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Finalize the spiked synergy board; the gold lead from the payouts funds the last star-ups. Close.

## Leveling cadence

Level for **capacity** (she needs the slots to field a [6]) more than for shop tiers, since her free
heroes partly bypass the shop, though leveling *before* casting Power of Chaos improves the 3 free
bodies. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3: `L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 ·
L10: 231`. Push to the level that fields her target breakpoint, funded by the kit's gold + the +24g; do
not under-level and strand the synergy below [6].

## Best comps (concrete)

**Any synergy stackable to [6]:**
- **Mage core** ([[../mage_core]]): inject Mage, get 3 free mages + 6 gold, rush Mage [6], cash +24g,
  take Purest Power. Kagura/Odette carries, Stargazer mana.
- **Marksman [6]** ([[../marksman_carry]]): inject Marksman, snowball the count.
- **A deep faction** (Astro Power, Emberlord, Exorcist) if the pool leaves it open, she makes going tall
  **cheaper** (free count + free heroes). Pairs with **Solid Alliance** if going wider.
- Pick the axis the pool leaves **open**; her free heroes make a one-time pivot cheaper than for most.

**A worked faction-tall alternative (Astro Power [6]):** inject Astro Power with Power of Chaos (the 3
free heroes pull from Minotaur/Hilda/Aurora/Odette/Helcurt/Martis), rush Astro to [6] for the +24g, and
funnel items onto the **Astro Sovereign** (the most-equipped hero, who gains +80% DMG Increase and 15%
Lifesteal at [6]). Odette is the natural Sovereign-carry. This trades the Mage core's burst for a
gear-funnel scaling line, and pairs with the **Astro Judgment** card if it appears. **Emberlord [6]** is
another deep-faction option (a death-trigger snowball) when that lane is open. The rule is the same: any
**6-stackable** axis the pool leaves open, never a shallow one.

## Power-card deep dive

The exclusive is a standout: **+24 gold for a first 6-count** is a top-tier econ payout *on a commander
built to hit 6-counts*. Keep it unless a 6-count is genuinely unreachable this game (then the borrowable
+6g+blessing for a second injection, or a generic econ card). Use the 2 refreshes only if forced off the
6-count plan. The exclusive's value is conditional on actually reaching 6, so the whole game is built to
deliver that condition.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Contention on my synergy:** if a rival shares my axis (a **Vale** hammering one line, or another
  go-tall), the pool tightens and the 6-count slips. My **free heroes** partly insulate me, but prefer
  an **uncontested** synergy so the 6-count is reachable.
- **Aggressive boards** that end the game before my mid-game 6-count comes online beat my slow spike;
  protect HP early.
- Watch who else is going tall on my line and **pivot axis early** if it is crowded (the one-shot free
  heroes make a pivot cheaper than for most, but a wasted Power of Chaos hurts).
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **Synergy contested / cannot reach 6:** pivot the axis early if I have not cast yet; if I already cast
  Power of Chaos into a now-dead axis, lean on the borrowable +6g blessing card and settle for a strong
  **[4]** board, the exclusive's +24g value drops, so re-weigh it.
- **Behind early:** the kit's free heroes + 6 gold are a built-in stabilizer; commit the axis and let the
  payouts compound.
- **Highroll the 6-count early:** press hard, the +24g plus an early Purest Power is a brutal spike.
- **Cannot decide the axis:** do not cast Power of Chaos blindly, it is one-shot; wait a round to read
  the open lane, then commit.

## The edge (the secret)

**The cheapest, fastest synergy head-start in the roster, with a gold windfall attached.** One Power of
Chaos cast hands a +1 count + 3 free heroes + 6 gold of a chosen synergy, an instant down-payment the
pool cannot match, and the +24g for a first 6-count pays her to do exactly the rush she is built for,
funding the star-up spike. No commander commits a single synergy harder, faster, or cheaper.

## Pitfalls

- **Not committing a synergy early:** her whole kit needs a target; a wishy-washy Lunox wastes the one
  Power of Chaos and the exclusive.
- **Casting Power of Chaos blindly** (it is one-shot, not a stream) into an axis that ends up contested
  or shallow.
- **Picking a non-6-stackable axis** (a 2/3-member synergy), forfeiting most of the exclusive's +24g.
- **Missing the +24g** by never reaching a 6-count, that is most of the exclusive's value gone.

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Contention on her chosen synergy** (a rival, or a **Vale** also hammering one line) can starve the axis
and deny the 6-count. **Aggressive boards** that end the game before her 6-count comes online beat her
slow spike. And the +24g is **one-time**: after the spike she is a normal synergy board, so a commander
that **out-scales late** (value engines, Neobeasts) can overtake her if the spike did not already win.
Her window is the mid-game 6-count; land it and snowball, miss it and she is ordinary.

## Numbers that matter (the snowball, quantified)

- **The injection (one Power of Chaos):** +1 synergy count + **3 free heroes of that synergy** (their
  cost tier scales with my shop odds at cast time) + **6 gold**. That is an instant ~3-body down-payment
  on the axis the pool cannot match.
- **The windfall (the exclusive):** **+4 gold** now, **+24 gold** the first time any synergy hits **6**.
  On a commander built to rush a 6-count, +24g is among the best econ payouts in the game, and it lands
  exactly when I want to roll down for star-ups.
- **The payoff it buys:** Mage **[6]** = +30% Magic ATK all / +120% mages; **Purest Power I/II** = +20% /
  +28% Hybrid ATK to the 6-count heroes. Reaching that breakpoint a round before the lobby, funded by the
  +24g, is the entire snowball.
- **Axis caps (why 6-stackable only):** the exclusive needs a **6-count**, so a 2/3-member synergy
  (Scavenger, Phasewarper, Mystic Meow, Northern Vale) **forfeits the +24g**, never inject those.

## The 7-archetype read (who I face, and the adjustment)

- vs **synergy contesters** (Vale on one line, another go-tall, a second Lunox): they starve my axis and
  deny the 6-count, prefer an **uncontested** synergy, and pivot early if crowded.
- vs **fast aggro / combat** (Lancelot, Ling, Lukas, Wanwan): they end the game before my mid-game
  6-count lands, protect HP early and rush the count.
- vs **late-scaling** (Neobeasts, value engines, Cyclops): the +24g is **one-time**, so after my spike I
  am a normal board, I must win *with* the 6-count spike, not coast on it.
- vs **vertical** (Alice, Aamon): different axis (I go wide-on-one-synergy, they go tall-on-one-hero),
  little direct contention unless we share a carry hero.
- vs **econ snowball** (Lancelot, Chou): I match their econ with the +24g windfall and the 6 free gold,
  then out-spike with the synergy.
- vs **equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Zilong): I want Synergy Magic Crystals (counts), they want gear, so
  we rarely contest the same lots.
- vs **free-hero / value** (Guinevere, Harley, Aurora, Diggie): they beat the wide pool, I beat one
  synergy; I commit my axis and ignore their lane.
