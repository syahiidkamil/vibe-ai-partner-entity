How to play each of the 37 commanders, and the question Kamil flagged: **at I-2, is the commander's own

## How to pick a commander

Match the commander to the plan I want and the meta: econ/value kits are draw-independent and floor
placement (safe climb); duplicate/star-up kits enable verticals; synergy-grant kits buy breakpoints;
combat kits reward knowing the board. The four tests are in
[[../../thinking_and_mental_model/commanders]].

## The power-card-selection principle (the general answer)

At **I-2** my exclusive is **guaranteed** in the offer, alongside borrowable power cards, and I get **2
refreshes** (one card at a time) to dig. The exclusive is tuned to amplify my kit's loop, so it is the
**default best pick when I am committed to my commander's plan**. Take a **generic/borrowable instead**
when:
1. The exclusive is **back-loaded or win-more** and I need tempo now (take an economy card early).
2. A **universal generic out-values a weak exclusive** (a flat +2 gold loses to a synergy spike or a
   big econ card).
3. The exclusive is **off the line** the draws are actually pushing me toward.
4. A **breakpoint or capacity card wins more**: Purest Power / Solid Alliance / a Synergy Badge-Blessing
   (a +1 count), or **Fanny's Power (+1 Hero Capacity)**, which is the best borrowable for almost any
   comp wanting a slot.

The strongest **borrowable** picks to weigh against any exclusive: **Fanny's Power** (+1 capacity),
**Trust Fund / Vault Upgrade+ / Payday** (economy), **Reinforcement+** (a 2-star 4-cost), **Purest
Power** (if 6-stacking), and the **Synergy Badge/Blessing** (a bought breakpoint). Use the 2 refreshes
to fish for these when my exclusive does not fit.

## Economy / Gold

- **Lancelot** (no interest, all gold auto-levels him, flat bonus gold by stage). Play: out-level early
  off the bonus gold, commit a carry, spike mid. **Power card:** his (+2 gold/round at L5) is *weak*;
  his kit already handles gold, so prefer a **synergy spike or a big econ generic** (Trust Fund). Deviate.
- **Chou** (+2 gold on win, +3 on loss: streak-agnostic econ). Play: stable gold no matter the result,
  good for a slow-build. **Power card:** Chou's Sash (chest per 3 kills) is fine on a kill-heavy carry;
  otherwise a generic econ/synergy. Situational.
- **Nana** (survive first death at 1 HP, +8 gold per 10 DMG taken). Play: a sticky econ-survival
  commander that profits from being hit. **Power card:** **take the exclusive** (chest + 8 gold +
  one fatal-immunity): unique survival that compounds her kit. Keep.

## Duplicate / copy / star-up

- **Aamon** (Mirror Devices = free copies). Play: chase 2-stars, convert to Mirrors, star up. **Power
  card:** **take his** (+4 gold per 2-star, up to 20): it pays his whole loop. (And contest enemy
  Mirror Devices, per [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].)
- **Alice** (star-up a chosen carry without 3 copies). Play: pick a carry, spike it absurdly. **Power
  card:** **take the exclusive** (+5 EXP, +45% Hybrid ATK to her chosen heroes): turbo-charges the very
  carry her kit builds. Keep.
- **Dyrroth** (devour up the cost ladder). Play: feed 1-stars, climb to high-cost heroes. **Power
  card:** **take his** (a free devour every 3 rounds + EXP): doubles his engine. Keep.
- **Harley** (floods 5-cost shop from II-2). Play: bridge to II-2, then field 5-costs early. **Power
  card:** his (+6 gold, refresh to 4-gold x2) is a fine early bridge; or a generic if the kit suffices.
  Situational, lean keep early.
- **Lylia** (gain a copy of the highest-cost deployed hero at Capacity 8). Play: build to a fat carry,
  duplicate it. **Power card:** **take hers** (a free 5-gold hero + matching equipment at L7): a free
  5-cost is huge. Keep.
- **Moskov** (Shadow Field enhance + duplicate). Play: enhance a key hero, spawn copies. **Power
  card:** his free cost-ladder (1->5 gold heroes) is solid value; or a synergy card if committed.
- **Paquito** (free copies of 3/4/5-cost by stage). Play: copy-engine into star-ups. **Power card:**
  **take his** (Glory Gloves: copy a hero + 4 gold): free copies are star-up progress. Keep.
- **Popol and Kupa** (Copy Trap: steal enemy heroes). Play: trap-steal off the lobby. **Power card:**
  the kit gives the trap, so **take the exclusive** (+2 gold/capture, chests at 10) if leaning into
  captures; else a generic.
- **Vale** (synergy-targeted refresh + free buys per level). Play: commit a synergy, refresh into it,
  level for charges. **Power card:** **take his** (a free hero + gold each level-up): tempo that pairs
  with the leveling kit. Keep.

## Equipment

- **Karina** (execute Twinblades). Play: an execute-carry on a low-HP-bursty board. **Power card:**
  **take the exclusive** (+6 gold, Twinblades +40% ATK Speed): amplifies the blades. Keep.
- **Luo Yi** (hero -> Essence Core equipment). Play: convert spare heroes into gear-stats. **Power
  card:** her repeated free 3-gold hero is good fodder; or a synergy card. Situational.
- **Minotaur** (enhance gear + chests). Play: an equipment-economy carry board. **Power card:** **take
  his** (enhance 2x + 2 random equipment): gear economy. Keep.
- **Ruby** (starts with Cloak + Scythe, resurrects). Play: a sticky item-carry front. **Power card:**
  her borrowable Scythe+Cloak is *redundant* (the kit gives them), so **take the exclusive** (+2 gold
  per surviving carrier, max 35) or a synergy card. Deviate from the borrowable.
- **Zilong** (a scaling Dragon Spear). Play: stack the spear's traits onto a carry. **Power card:**
  **take the exclusive** (Spear + 30% ATK, shop fills with traits, 1 free): a big spear power-up. Keep.

## Synergy grants

- **Gusion** (grant a random Role synergy). Play: buy breakpoints, go flexible-wide. **Power card:**
  **take his** (a Role Magic Crystal Chest + 3 gold): a free +1 count is high value. Keep.
- **Guinevere** (free heroes appear in shop). Play: a recruitment-tempo flex. **Power card:** **take
  hers** (a free hero for 13 rounds): enormous recruitment value. Keep.
- **Lunox** (grant a Blessing, then heroes of that synergy). Play: commit a synergy, fill its count.
  **Power card:** **take the exclusive** (+4 gold, **+24 gold when you first hit a 6-synergy**): one of
  the best econ cards in the game *for a synergy commander doing this anyway*. Strong keep.

## Reward / value engines

- **Alucard** (Demonic Altar reward engine). Play: passive value, flex the rewards. **Power card:**
  **take his** (extra altars at I-2/II-2/III-2): more reward engine. Keep.
- **Aurora** (Frost rewards for not buying). Play: econ, buy little, bank value. **Power card:** **take
  hers** (a >=4-gold reward each no-buy round) if playing the econ; a generic if I want to spend.
- **Cyclops** (loot eliminated commanders). Play: scale off others' deaths, strong late. **Power
  card:** his (+8 gold, +gold per elimination) is **back-loaded** (needs a kill), so early prefer a
  **generic econ**; take his once eliminations are near. Deviate early.
- **Diggie** (Alarm Chest every round). Play: reliable passive value. **Power card:** the kit gives the
  chest, so **take the exclusive** (+15 points/round, an extra 25-gold open): accelerates it. Keep.
- **Kalea** (Lucky Dice -> Trove). Play: roll for reward swings. **Power card:** **take hers** (dice now
  + at Stage III for 21-35 gold): more dice value. Keep.
- **Vexana** (extra Go Go Box picks + auction gold). Play: out-pick the value stages. **Power card:**
  **take hers** (an extra reward in the first 4 boxes + 13 auction gold): her whole edge. Keep. (Enemy
  Vexana outbids me, per [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].)

## Combat & board control

- **Angela** (shield + mana an ally). Play: protect a caster carry. **Power card:** her doll (+70% mana
  regen nearby) is great on a caster board; a generic otherwise. Situational.
- **Fanny** (Shadow copies; capacity at L5). Play: extra bodies in the fight. **Power card:** **take
  hers** (+1 Hero Capacity at L5): an extra slot is near-universal value, and the best **borrowable**
  card for any comp wanting a breakpoint slot. Keep / borrow.
- **Johnson** (free Johnny bodies, 2 synergies each). Play: free summon bodies that fill synergies.
  **Power card:** **take the exclusive** (+5 Johnnies in pool, +25% ATK/+20% HP): amplifies the summons.
  Keep.
- **Kagura** (Shield Umbrella on a carry). Play: a protected mage carry (the replay line). **Power
  card:** **take hers** (a carry +20% lifesteal +30% ATK at battle start, +9 gold): buff + econ, a
  strong I-2. Keep.
- **Layla** (hologram lasers; hero from top synergy per DMG). Play: a high-DMG board feeds free heroes.
  **Power card:** hers scales with damage (free heroes from the top synergy); good on a damage board,
  situational early.
- **Ling** (Dueling Ring; dives). Play: an assassin carry that drags out a target. **Power card:**
  **take his** (the same 4-gold hero at L4/5/7 = a near-2-star 4-cost free): strong value. Keep.
- **Lukas** (statue stun/shockwave). Play: a CC-front disruptor. **Power card:** **take the exclusive**
  (+12 gold, Sacred Beast +50% DMG): gold + amplifies the statue. Keep.
- **Miya** (coordinated arrows on a carry). Play: a marksman add-on board. **Power card:** **take hers**
  (+12 gold + Lunar Longbow ATK-speed item): gold + a marksman item. Keep.
- **Wanwan** (low-HP burst; gold per kill). Play: a finisher carry. **Power card:** hers (50% chance +1
  gold per kill, max 35) is an econ engine on a kill-heavy board; a generic if the board is not killy.
  Situational.

## How to use this, and what comes next

The verdict pattern: **keep the exclusive when it pays the kit's loop or buffs the carry the kit builds;
deviate to a generic when the exclusive is flat, back-loaded, or off-line, or when a capacity / synergy
/ big-econ card wins more** (and use the 2 refreshes to fish for it). These are structural priors, not
win-rate data and not lived play; verify each in real games and overwrite. As I actually play a
commander, its deep file graduates here (`commanders/<name>.md`) with positioning, comp pairings, and
matchup notes; this index stays the map and the power-card ledger.
