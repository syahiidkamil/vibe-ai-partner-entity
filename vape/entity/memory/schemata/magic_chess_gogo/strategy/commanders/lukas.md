## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat / CC-burst disruptor. A statue opens the fight with an AoE stun, %-max-HP damage,
  and a DEF shred, softening the enemy for my board.
- **Plays like:** a fight-opener, the statue stuns and shreds the enemy cluster at battle start, then my
  carries capitalize on the opening.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a free battle-start disruption; the skill is a board that capitalizes
  on the stun + shred and good statue placement.
- **Best into:** clustered and tanky enemy boards (AoE stun + %-HP damage). **Worst into:** spread boards
  (less AoE value) and a board of my own that cannot follow up the stun.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Lukas, "Beast of Light." Skins: Beast of Light, Ardent Roar. (There is no *hero* Lukas in the
  S6 shop roster, so no commander/hero collision.)
- **Unleash the Beast** [Passive] (verbatim): *"Generate a statue on the battlefield. When battle starts, it
  unleashes Shockwave Blast before vanishing. Shockwave Blast: Deals Physical DMG equal to 20% of target's
  Max HP to enemies in medium range and stuns them for 1s."*
- **Frenzy** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"Shockwave Blast's power increases to 45% of target's Max
  HP as Physical DMG, and stun duration increases to 1.5s. If the statue is placed in the front row, the
  Sacred Beast unleashes Wild Power before vanishing. Wild Power: Deals 45% of target's Max HP as Physical
  DMG to enemies in medium range and reduces their Hybrid DEF by 65."*
- **Power Cards:** exclusive *Lukas's Power* - *"Gain 12 Gold. Sacred Beast gains 50% extra DMG."*
  **(Exclusive)**; borrowable *Lukas's Power* - *"Gain 1 statue. At battle start it deals DMG and applies
  debuffs, then leaves."*

Reading (in pencil): the statue **opens the fight** with an AoE **Shockwave Blast**, **%-of-Max-HP
Physical DMG** (20%, **45%** starred, great vs tanks) + a **stun** (1s, **1.5s** starred) to the enemy
cluster, and front-placed (starred) it adds **Wild Power** (another 45% Max-HP AoE + **-65 Hybrid DEF**
shred). So he **softens and CCs the enemy at battle start**, setting up my board's damage. The exclusive
adds **+12 gold + 50% statue DMG**; the borrowable just re-grants the statue, redundant on the owner. A
**CC-burst disruptor**.

## Win condition / identity

**Open every fight with a stun + shred, then let my carries clean up.** The statue's battle-start AoE stun
freezes the enemy cluster while %-max-HP damage and a DEF shred soften them, so my board hits a stunned,
weakened enemy, a free advantage every round. He is a **combat enabler**: the disruption is the play, and
the board needs **follow-up carries** that capitalize on the opening (burst during the stun, finish the
shredded targets). Best vs clustered, tanky boards (the %-HP and AoE punish them).

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Capitalize on the opening.** The stun + shred are only as good as the follow-up, so build **carries
   that burst during the stun window** (a mage, an execute carry) or an AoE board that benefits from the
   enemy clustered and CC'd.
2. **Statue placement.** Front-placed (starred) the statue adds Wild Power (more AoE + the DEF shred), so
   **place it front** for the full effect, and aim its medium-range blast at the densest enemy cluster.

## The statue plan (his signature, concrete)

The ordered way to run the disruptor:
1. **Take the exclusive** (+12 gold + 50% statue DMG), making the opening burst hit hard.
2. **Place the statue front** (starred) so it unleashes both Shockwave Blast + Wild Power (stun + double
   %-HP AoE + the -65 DEF shred).
3. **Aim it at the enemy cluster** (medium range, densest area) for max AoE stun + damage.
4. **Build follow-up carries** that capitalize, a mage that bursts the stunned enemy, an execute carry
   (Karina-style) that finishes shredded/low targets, or an AoE board.
5. **Run a normal economy** (the kit is mostly combat + the card's +12 gold).

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): build a follow-up board
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build toward a board that capitalizes on the stun (a burst carry / AoE).
  Creep: a damage basic for the carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take the exclusive** (+12 gold + 50% statue DMG), NOT the borrowable (redundant
  re-grant). It funds + amplifies the opener; keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** place the statue front, aim at the cluster; bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or a burst/AoE hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): open + follow up, cross breakpoints
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; commit the follow-up synergy.
- **The statue opens (stun + shred), my carries capitalize**; cross the first breakpoints.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's item or a Synergy Magic Crystal; the +12 gold helps.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike the follow-up
- **Spike** the carry (star-up, the [6], a payoff card); the statue's stun + shred makes the spike land on
  a frozen, weakened enemy.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, gear the carry.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- The opening disruption + a spiked carry decide fights. Close before HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Standard for the follow-up comp; the card's +12 gold helps the economy, so level to the carry's tier and
for capacity. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The statue scales with the commander
star (Frenzy), so star-up matters for the full Wild Power + 1.5s stun.

## Best comps (concrete)

**A board that capitalizes on the opening stun + shred:**
- **A burst mage core** (Kagura/Odette/Vexana): the casters delete the stunned, DEF-shredded enemy cluster.
- **An execute / AoE board** (Karina-style, or AoE casters): the %-HP softening + shred set up finishers.
- A **front-heavy board** so the statue (front-placed) and the wall hold while the burst lands.
- The constant: **disruption opens, carries capitalize**, best vs clustered/tanky enemies.

## Positioning

**Place the statue front** (starred Wild Power) and **aim at the densest enemy cluster** (medium-range AoE
stun + shred). Seat the follow-up carry **back-center** to burst the stunned enemy; spread my own non-statue
units vs enemy AoE. Full detail: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Power-card deep dive

His **exclusive** (**+12 gold + 50% Sacred Beast DMG**) is the right pick, econ + a much harder opening
burst, amplifying the disruption the kit provides. Keep it. The **borrowable** is a **trap on the owner**
(it re-grants a statue he already has), it exists for *others* to borrow a disruptor. Use the 2 refreshes
only if a synergy/capacity card clearly wins more.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Spread enemy boards** dodge the AoE stun (less value); the %-HP single-target still helps, but the
  disruption drops, aim at whatever cluster exists.
- **My own board must follow up** the stun; against a tanky enemy the %-HP + shred shine, against a squishy
  spread the burst matters more.
- **Economy commanders** out-resource me long-term (mostly combat kit + 12 gold); convert the combat edge
  to HP.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **No follow-up (weak board):** the stun is wasted without carries to capitalize, so prioritize a burst/AoE
  carry; the statue is an enabler, not a win-con alone.
- **Spread enemies:** aim the statue at the biggest cluster; lean on single-target %-HP and my carries.
- **Behind early:** the +12 gold + the free opening burst stabilize; build the follow-up.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** spike the carry and let the statue's opening decide the fight; close.

## The edge (the secret)

**He starts every fight a step ahead.** The statue's battle-start AoE stun + %-max-HP burst + DEF shred
freeze and soften the enemy cluster before they act, so my board hits a helpless, weakened enemy, free
value every round, doubly punishing tanky/clustered boards. A combat enabler whose opening disruption sets
up the kill.

## Pitfalls

- **Taking the borrowable card** (redundant, re-grants the statue).
- **No follow-up carries** (the stun + shred go to waste).
- **Statue placed back row** (front-placed unlocks Wild Power + the DEF shred when starred).
- **Aiming the statue at a spread board** (it wants the enemy clustered).

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Spread boards** blunt the AoE stun (less disruption). **A weak follow-up** of my own wastes the opening.
He is strongest into **clustered, tanky** boards (AoE stun + %-HP + shred) and weakest into spread ones or
when my own carries cannot capitalize. A combat enabler whose value is the opening disruption, gated by the
follow-up.

- vs **tanky / clustered boards** (frontline walls, Defender/Dauntless): **my best**, %-HP burst + AoE stun
  + DEF shred punish them; my carries delete the softened cluster.

- vs **value / econ** (Aurora, Diggie, Chou): convert the combat edge to HP before they scale.
- vs **equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Zilong): I want damage gear for the carry; statue is kit.
- vs **caster carries** (Angela, Vexana): stun their casters in the opening to deny casts.
