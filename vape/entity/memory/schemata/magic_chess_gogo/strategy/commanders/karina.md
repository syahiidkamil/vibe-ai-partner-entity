## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** equipment / execute melee-carry. One free strong item turns a front-carry into a
  low-HP-target deleter that snowballs damage as it kills.
- **Plays like:** a sticky bruiser-carry that cleans up softened enemies, each execute making it hit
  harder, paired with AoE/chip that pushes targets into execute range.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is one free item + a placement decision (a melee carry adjacent to
  enemies); the snowball rewards enabling the executes with chip damage.
- **Best into:** tanky / chip / sustain lobbies (executes punish low-HP stragglers), long fights (the
  +DMG stacks). **Worst into:** pure burst that one-shots her carry, anti-heal, and boards she cannot
  push below the 18% threshold.

**Equipment the kit grants ([[../../infos/equipments/commander_exclusive]]):** the **Twinblades**, a

- **Shadow Twinblades** (1-Star): **+35% Hybrid ATK, +25% Max HP** (*Enh: +55% / +30%*). *Execute:*
  execute adjacent enemies below 18% HP.
- **Supreme Blades** (2-Star): **+35% Hybrid ATK, +25% Max HP** (*Enh: +55% / +30%*). *Gift:* Execute +
  each execution grants **+2% DMG, up to +36%** (the Evolution stacks live here).

Reading (in pencil): the Twinblades are **both a stat stick and a win-condition**, +35%/+55% Hybrid ATK
and +25%/+30% Max HP makes the carrier a **sticky bruiser-carry**, and the **execute** deletes any
adjacent enemy under 18% HP (ignoring their effective HP, so it punishes tanks and chip-softened units).
Each execute then **stacks +2% DMG up to +36%**, so she **snowballs within a fight**: kill one straggler,
hit harder, kill the next. The exclusive adds **+40% ATK Speed** (more attacks = faster executes and
faster stacking) plus +6 gold. The execute is **adjacent**, so she is a **melee / front-ish carry**, not
a back-liner.

Rethink (2026-07-10, first principles): the execute's real identity is **defense-ignoring damage**,
a unit at 17% HP dies regardless of armor, shields queued, or healing incoming, so she is the
structural counter to the sustain-and-shield economies (Dauntless walls, lifesteal stacks, Kagura
umbrellas, Ruby resurrect timing if the execute lands before the revive window, verify that
interaction live). Her currency is equipment (the Blades), fully trick-proof. The conditionality is
also structural: below-18% requires CHIP, so her value is a function of fight length, long
attrition fights = compounding executes, burst metas = the blades never trigger. A conditional
amplifier with a real tech niche: B in general, strongest exactly where the lobby builds sustain.

## Win condition / identity

**A snowballing melee execute-carry that cleans up the enemy board and gets stronger doing it.** The free
Twinblades make her gear-ahead and tanky-and-hitting at once; the execute turns chip damage into kills
and the kills into more damage. She wants a board that **softens enemies into execute range** (AoE,
chip, a Glowing Wand burn) so the Twinblades carrier can mop up and stack. Like the other equipment
commanders her floor is high (a free strong item, no bricks), with a real snowball ceiling when the
executes chain.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing decisions:
1. **Which carry holds the Twinblades.** It wants a **durable melee unit that fights adjacent to enemies**
   (a Bruiser like Yu Zhong/Masha, a front-carry like the hero Ruby, or Alucard), because the execute is
   *adjacent* and the +25% HP keeps it alive in melee. A back-row ranged carry rarely sits adjacent to
   low-HP enemies, so it under-uses the execute.
2. **Enabling the executes.** The execute needs targets under 18% HP, so the board should bring **chip /
   AoE** (a Mage, a Glowing-Wand burn, AoE casters) to push enemies into range; a board with no
   softening leaves the execute starved.

## The execute snowball (her signature, concrete)

The ordered plan:
1. **Equip the Twinblades on a melee bruiser-carry** turn 1 (free), one that will be in contact with the
   enemy line. Funnel ATK-Speed + damage items onto it.
2. **Build chip/AoE around it** so enemies drop below 18% for the execute to trigger (AoE casters,
   burn, a Bruiser front that whittles).
3. **Stack the +2% DMG (up to +36%):** the more executes per fight, the harder the carry hits, so
   position it to **reach multiple low-HP enemies** (a flank into the enemy back line of softened units,
   or the densest cluster).
4. **Chase the 2-Star Twinblades** (Supreme Blades, her commander star-up) where the +2%/execute stacking
   lives, and **Enhance** them (+55% ATK / +30% HP), the real power jump.
5. **Add the exclusive's +40% ATK Speed** so she attacks (and executes, and stacks) faster.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): equip the blades, find the melee carry
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Equip the free Twinblades on my best early melee bruiser. Buy toward a
  Bruiser front + a chip source. Creep: an **ATK-Speed (Inspire) or damage basic** for the carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take the exclusive** (+6 gold, Twinblades **+40% ATK Speed**): more attacks =
  more executes = faster snowball, plus the econ. Keep it. (The borrowable just re-grants the Twinblades I
  already have, redundant on Karina, never take it.)
- **I-3 / I-4:** build the front + a chip unit, hold gold toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** an ATK-Speed / damage **Enhanced** item for the Twinblades carry,
  or a chip-AoE hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): stack the carry, add chip, cross breakpoints
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a free +1 count (Bruiser or a frontline synergy).
- **Stack the carry** (ATK-Speed + damage) and field **AoE/chip** to enable executes; cross **Bruiser
  [4]** (double-hit basics + DMG, which both feeds attacks and softens) and a frontline [2].
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** an item bundle for the carry or a chip-enabling card.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's **best ATK-Speed / damage Enhanced items** and a
  Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier (L7-8).

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): 2-star blades, snowball
- **Chase the 2-Star Twinblades (Supreme Blades)** for the +2%/execute stacking, and Enhance them. The
  carry now snowballs hard within fights.
- **Spike** the comp (carry star-up, Bruiser [6] or the chip line, a payoff card).
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more carry gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Twinblades maxed (Enhanced Supreme + stacked items) + chip support. In the long Battle-Acceleration
  window the carry executes and stacks to a runaway state. Close before HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Standard for a single-carry comp: level to the carry's tier and for capacity (to fit both the carry and
the chip enablers), reroll for the carry's star-up and front. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The exclusive's +6 gold and her gear
lead let her level steadily on the interest band.

## Best comps (concrete)

Built around a **melee execute-carry + chip to feed it**:
- **Bruiser front-carry:** Yu Zhong (4c Bruiser/Exorcist) or Masha (3c Bruiser/Emberlord) holding the
  Twinblades, on a Bruiser [4] board (double-hit basics soften + the carry executes), fronted by a

  the enemy line so the Twinblades carry (a sturdy bruiser) mops up. The casters chip, Karina executes.
- **Dauntless front-carry:** the hero Ruby or Esmeralda holding the Twinblades for a tanky, sticky
  execute-carry that survives melee.

## Positioning

The Twinblades carry must be **adjacent to enemies** (the execute is adjacent), so it is a **front /
melee** seat, not back-row. Seat it to **contact multiple enemies** (the front line, or a flank that
reaches softened back-row units) so executes chain and the +DMG stacks. The +25%/+30% HP from the blades
lets it survive the front. Keep the chip/AoE enablers protected behind. Full detail:

## Power-card deep dive

Her **exclusive** (+6 gold, Twinblades **+40% ATK Speed**) is the right pick: more attacks directly mean
more executes and faster +DMG stacking, on top of useful early econ. Keep it. The **borrowable** Karina
card is a **trap on Karina herself** (it re-grants the Twinblades she already starts with), it exists for
*other* commanders to borrow her item. Use the 2 refreshes only if a Bruiser Badge/Blessing or capacity
card would win more.

## The edge (the secret)

**A free item that is a stat stick *and* a win-condition that snowballs.** The Twinblades make one melee
carry tanky-and-hitting from round 1, then the **execute turns chip into kills and kills into +DMG**, so
she runs away with attrition fights, and the exclusive's +40% ATK Speed accelerates the whole loop. She
punishes exactly the tanky/sustain boards that grind other carries down, and an enemy softened once is an
enemy deleted.

## Pitfalls

- **Twinblades on a back-row ranged carry** (the execute is *adjacent*, so a back-liner under-uses it).
- **No chip / AoE to enable executes** (the snowball never starts).
- **Taking the borrowable card** (redundant, re-grants the blades).
- **Over-extending the melee carry into burst** (it needs to survive to stack).
- **Spreading gear** instead of funneling onto the one Twinblades carry.

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Pure burst / assassins** that delete her melee carry before it stacks (she wants attrition, not burst).
**Boards she cannot push below 18%** (no chip on my side, high-sustain enemies) starve the execute.
**Other equipment commanders** contest her gear. She is strongest into **tanky / chip / sustain** lobbies
and **long fights** (the +36% DMG and the executes compound); weakest into fast burst and unsoftenable
boards. High floor (a free strong item), ceiling tied to enabling the executes.

## A note on the 18% threshold (the enabler math)

The execute fires on **adjacent enemies under 18% Max HP**, which is why *enabling* it matters more than
on most kits. Two ways the threshold gets reached, and both are mine to engineer:
- **My chip pushes them there.** AoE/burn (Glowing Wand's burn, a Mage's AoE, a Bruiser front's
  double-hit basics) shaves the enemy line; once a unit dips under 18%, the execute is an *instant* kill
  regardless of their remaining effective HP (shields, DEF, sustain do not save them). That is why she
  beats tanks: I do not have to chew through the last 18%, I delete it.
- **The fight naturally softens.** In a long Battle-Acceleration window, many enemies end up low, so a
  Twinblades carry positioned to reach the cluster chains executes and stacks +DMG fast. A board with *no*
  chip and *fast* fights (enemies die before dipping to 18% in my carry's reach) starves the execute, so
  never run Karina without a softening source.
