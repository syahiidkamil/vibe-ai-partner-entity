## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** duplicate / enhance via a cost-ladder ritual. Complete the Shadow Field for gold, then
  enhance one carry and spawn two free copies of it.
- **Plays like:** feed the ritual one hero of each cost (banking +20 gold), then buff a chosen carry and
  jump it toward a star-up with two free copies, a self-funding vertical.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a setup ritual plus an enhance-target choice; the skill is acquiring
  the ladder heroes and aiming the enhance + copies at a keeper carry.
- **Best into:** gold-fed mid-games where the ritual + copies complete a carry. **Worst into:** fast aggro
  that ends before the ritual pays, and a contested pool that competes for the same carry.

- **Dark Star** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"Generates two additional 1-Star copies of the

  in following rounds obtain a random 2-, 3-, 4-, and 5-Gold Hero in sequence."*

Reading (in pencil): the Shadow Field is a **cost-ladder ritual**, feed it one **1-Gold, 2-Gold, 3-Gold,
and 4-Gold** hero one by one, **+5 gold each (+20 total)**, to complete it. Then place a chosen carry in
it for **Power of Shadows** (+15% Hybrid ATK & +15% HP), and (with the star-up) **two free 1-Star copies
of that carry** appear, real progress toward a star-up the pool would charge for. The card conveniently
**feeds the ladder**: it hands a 1-Gold hero now, then 2/3/4/5-Gold in sequence, exactly the costs the
ritual needs (and a free 5-cost on top). So he is a **self-funding enhance-and-duplicate** commander.

## Win condition / identity

**Enhance one carry and duplicate it toward a star-up, paid for by the ritual gold.** Completing the
Shadow Field banks +20 gold *and* buffs a chosen carry (+15%/+15%) *and* spawns 2 free copies of it, so
he assembles a buffed, star-up-progressed carry for less than the pool would charge. He is a
**duplicate/value vertical**: the ritual funds the game, the enhance + copies build the carry. Flexible
on which carry, since the kit works on any hero placed in the field.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Feeding the ritual (acquiring the ladder).** It needs one each of **1/2/3/4-Gold** heroes, so I must
   acquire those costs to complete it, the card supplies them in sequence, but otherwise I shop for them.
   Completing the ritual is +20 gold and the enhance, so it is worth prioritizing.
2. **Which carry gets enhanced + copied.** The Power of Shadows (+15%/+15%) and the 2 free copies land on
   **the hero I place in the field**, so place the **keeper carry I want to star up** (a 3-4 cost that
   scales), not a throwaway. The 2 copies are a star-up jump, so a carry I can finish matters most.

## The ritual sequence (his signature, concrete)

The ordered plan for the Shadow Field:
1. **Take the card** for the cost-ladder feed (1g now, then 2/3/4/5g in sequence), it hands me the exact
   heroes the ritual eats *and* a free 5-cost.
2. **Feed the field one each of 1/2/3/4-Gold heroes** (cheap bodies, the card's early hands), banking
   **+5 gold per placement (+20 total)** to complete the ritual.
3. **Place my keeper carry in the field** for **Power of Shadows** (+15% ATK / +15% HP) and, with the
   star-up, **2 free 1-Star copies** of it, a big step toward a 2-star (or further with rolls).
4. **Finish the star-up** with the 2 free copies + a couple of rolls (the ritual gold funds it).
5. **Build the board around the buffed carry** (front + synergy) and press.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): start feeding the ladder
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build a normal early board and start acquiring the **1/2-Gold** ritual
  feed. Creep: a flexible damage basic for the eventual carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take his card** (the cost-ladder free heroes): it feeds the ritual *and* hands a
  free 5-cost over time, perfectly on-kit. Keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** feed the field the cheap ladder heroes as they come (+5 gold each), bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or a hero opening my line / feeding the ladder.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): complete the ritual, enhance the carry
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; commit a synergy the carry anchors.
- **Complete the ritual** (feed the 3/4-Gold rungs, +20 gold total), then **place the keeper carry** for
  Power of Shadows + the 2 free copies. Star up the carry with them + rolls.
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** a card matching the carry's synergy.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's **best item** or a Synergy Magic Crystal; the ritual
  gold helps fund it.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike the buffed carry
- **The enhanced, star-progressed carry spikes** (the +15%/+15% + the copies + items). Cross the [6] or
  the carry's breakpoint, take a payoff card.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more carry gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Lock the buffed carry board, dump the ritual-funded gold into the last star-ups and items. Close before
  HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

The ritual's +20 gold and the card's free heroes mean he is **resource-rich**, so he can level *and* roll
to feed the ladder and finish the carry. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. Level to the carry's tier and for
capacity; do not over-level past the tier where the carry and its copies live.

## Best comps (concrete)

Built around the **enhanced, duplicated carry**:
- **A 3-4 cost carry** worth enhancing and star-up jumping (a Mage like Kagura/Vexana, a Marksman like
  Karrie, a bruiser like Yu Zhong), placed in the field for +15%/+15% + 2 copies, on a synergy shell.
- **Flexible:** the kit buffs any placed hero, so commit to whatever the pool + the card's free heroes
  open, and enhance the carry of that line.
- The card's free 5-cost late can become a second threat or the enhance target if it fits.

## Positioning

Standard for the buffed carry (back-center for ranged/caster, never cornered vs Swiftblade divers; front
for a bruiser), wall front, spread vs AoE. The Power of Shadows carry is the investment, protect it. Full
detail: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Power-card deep dive

His card (a **cost-ladder of free heroes**: 1g now, then 2/3/4/5g in sequence) is **on-kit and strong**:
it feeds the ritual exactly the costs it needs *and* delivers a free 5-cost over time. Keep it. Deviate
only if a synergy-breakpoint card clearly wins more for a committed line. Use the 2 refreshes to confirm.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Fast aggro** ends the game before the ritual + copies complete the carry, protect HP and prioritize
  completing the ritual quickly.
- **Contested carry:** the 2 free copies help, but a rival buying my carry's copies still slows the
  star-up, prefer an uncontested carry, or use a Mirror to duplicate past it.
- **Cyclops** wants someone to die early; do not be his first kill while the ritual spins up.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **Missing a ladder cost (ritual stalled):** shop for the missing 1/2/3/4-Gold rung, or wait for the
  card's sequence to deliver it, completing the ritual is +20 gold + the enhance, so it is worth a buy.
- **Wrong enhance target:** the field enhances whatever I place, so place the right keeper carry; if I
  enhanced a fall-off unit, the copies are still bodies but the +15%/+15% is partly wasted.
- **Behind early:** the ritual gold + card heroes are a stabilizer; complete the ritual and let the
  resources compound.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** dump the ritual-funded gold into the buffed carry's star-up and items.

## The edge (the secret)

**A self-funding enhance-and-duplicate that buffs a carry, copies it twice, and pays +20 gold for the
setup.** Moskov assembles a buffed, star-up-progressed carry for less than the pool would charge, with
the card feeding the exact ladder the ritual needs and a free 5-cost on top. A resource-rich vertical
that turns a cost-ladder chore into a carry plus a wallet.

## Pitfalls

- **Not completing the ritual** (leaving +20 gold + the enhance on the table).
- **Enhancing a throwaway** instead of the keeper carry.
- **Hoarding the card's free heroes** instead of feeding them into the ritual (they are the ladder).
- **Over-extending while the ritual spins up** (its payoff is mid-game).

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Fast aggro** ends it before the ritual + copies mature. A **contested carry** slows the star-up the 2
copies were building. He is strongest in **gold-fed mid-games** where the ritual completes and the
enhanced carry spikes; weakest in fast ones. A resource-rich vertical whose power is mid-game, not early.

## Enhance-and-duplicate: the carry math (deep dive)

The whole kit converges on **one well-chosen carry**, so the math of *which* and *when* is worth spelling
out. In pencil:
- **The star-up jump is the real prize.** A normal star-up needs 3 of a kind; Dark Star hands **2 free
  copies** of the enhanced hero, so I need only **1 more** (mine or a roll) to hit 2-star, the kit pays
  two-thirds of a star-up. On a **3-cost** that is a strong, reachable mid-game spike (the third copy is
  cheap, 9 of 18 pool); on a **4-cost** it is a star the pool would usually deny (9 of 14).
- **The enhance stacks on top.** +15% Hybrid ATK and +15% Max HP is a flat carry buff *plus* the copies,
  so the enhanced carry is both starred-up *and* statted-up beyond a normal version, funnel items onto it
  to compound.
- **Enhance-target priority:** (1) a **3-4 cost keeper carry** I can finish to 2-star with the 2 copies +
  one more (the highest payoff); (2) a **scaling carry** that stays relevant late (not a cheap fall-off);
  (3) the **card's free 5-cost** if it is my best unit by the time the ritual is ready. Never a throwaway.
- **Ritual timing:** complete the field as soon as I have the 1/2/3/4-Gold rungs (the card supplies them
  in sequence), because the **+20 gold is most valuable early** (it compounds via interest) and the
  enhance + copies want to land in the mid-game spike window, not Stage IV.
- **Mirror synergy:** a Mirror Device adds a *third* copy to the 2 from Dark Star = an instant 2-star (or
  progress to 3-star), so a Mirror on the enhanced carry is his best non-kit lever.
