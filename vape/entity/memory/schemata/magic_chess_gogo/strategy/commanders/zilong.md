## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** equipment / single auto-attack carry. One scaling artifact (the Dragon Spear) plus up
  to three bought traits turns one basic-attacker into the lobby's DPS.
- **Plays like:** funnel everything into one auto-attacking carry, multiply its attacks with the spear,
  and bolt on traits for sustain, survival, or econ.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a free artifact + a trait-shopping decision; the skill is the right
  carry (a fast auto-attacker), the right traits, and protecting the one threat.
- **Best into:** lobbies that cannot reach the back-row carry, long fights (the spear + ATK speed
  compounds). **Worst into:** burst/assassins and hard CC that kill or lock the carry before it ramps,
  and other equipment commanders contesting gear.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Zilong, "Spear of the Dragon." Skins: Spear of the Dragon, Spear of Valor. (There is no
  *hero* Zilong in the S6 shop roster, so no commander/hero collision.)
- **Great Dragon Spear** [Passive] (verbatim): *"Gain Zilong's Great Dragon Spear at start of match,
  granting the carrier a 20% chance to activate 2 extra ATKs every Basic ATK. You can purchase up to 1
  trait(s) in the Shop to enhance the Great Dragon Spear. Traits include: Control Immunity & Lifesteal:
  Become immune to control for 25s and restore 16% HP based on DMG dealt. Gold Bandit: Each Basic ATK has
  a 5% chance to grant 1 Gold. Low-HP Shield: When HP drops below 30%, gain a Shield equal to 50% of Max
  HP. (If this hasn't been triggered and fatal DMG is taken, the Shield will be granted immediately)."*
- **Dragon's Roar** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"You can purchase 2 additional trait(s) to
  enhance the Great Dragon Spear."*
- **Power Cards:** exclusive *Zilong's Power* - *"Great Dragon Spear + 30% Hybrid ATK. The Shop
  auto-refreshes to fill all slots with its traits. Select 1 trait free."* **(Exclusive)**; borrowable
  *Zilong's Power* - *"Obtain Great Dragon Spear (20% chance for extra ATKs on each Basic ATK); get traits
  from the Shop to enhance it."*

**Equipment the kit grants ([[../../infos/equipments/commander_exclusive]]):** the **Great Dragon Spear**
("Enhanceable Heavenly Artifact"), no base stat at Basic (**Enh: +20% Hybrid ATK**). *Dragon Flurry:*
each Basic ATK has a **20% chance to add 2 extra ATKs**. He then buys **traits** in the shop (1 base +
2 from the star-up = up to **3**) to layer onto it:
- **Control Immunity & Lifesteal:** control-immune 25s + restore **16% HP** based on DMG dealt.
- **Gold Bandit:** each Basic ATK has a **5% chance to grant 1 Gold** (an econ engine on a fast attacker).
- **Low-HP Shield:** below 30% HP, gain a **shield = 50% Max HP** (triggers on fatal DMG if unused).

Reading (in pencil): the spear is a **DPS multiplier for an auto-attacker**, +20% chance of 2 extra ATKs
is on average **~+0.4 attacks per attack (~40% more hits)**, which compounds with ATK Speed (so a
Marksman board is the natural host). The traits make the carry **uninterruptible and self-sustaining**
(CC immunity + lifesteal), **survivable** (the 50% shield), or **gold-printing** (Gold Bandit). Up to 3
traits = a stacked artifact. The whole plan is **one auto-attack carry, hyper-scaled.**

## Win condition / identity

**One auto-attacking carry the lobby's DPS cannot match.** The free spear plus 3 traits plus stacked
damage items turns a single Marksman (or basic-attack bruiser) into an uninterruptible, self-healing,
attack-multiplying threat. He is an **equipment carry** commander: the artifact is the plan, the comp is
the vehicle that protects and feeds it. Like the other gear commanders his floor is high (the spear is
free and never bricks), and the traits let him tune the carry to the lobby.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing decisions:
1. **Which carry holds the spear.** It wants the **fastest, most consistent auto-attacker** (a Marksman:
   Karrie, Irithel, Lesley, Miya; or a basic-attack bruiser like Masha/Alucard), because the spear and
   the traits all key off Basic ATKs. A caster (skill-damage) carry wastes most of it.
2. **Which traits, in what order.** Up to 3, bought with gold. Default priority: **Control Immunity &
   Lifesteal** first (an uninterruptible, self-healing carry is the biggest power jump), then **Low-HP
   Shield** (survival vs burst), then **Gold Bandit** (econ, or first if I want to fund a fast economy
   off a high-attack-rate carry). Tune to the threat: vs CC, rush Control Immunity; vs burst, Low-HP
   Shield.

## The Dragon Spear loop (his signature, concrete)

The ordered plan for the artifact:
1. **Equip the spear on the keeper auto-attack carry** turn 1 (it is free); start funneling ATK-speed +
   damage items onto that same unit.
2. **Buy the first trait early** (Control Immunity & Lifesteal usually) so the carry can attack through
   CC and sustain.
3. **At the star-up (Dragon's Roar), buy 2 more traits** for the full 3-trait artifact; the exclusive
   card also hands a free trait and refreshes the shop to show them.
4. **Enhance the spear** (+20% Hybrid ATK) when offered, and stack ATK-Speed items (Golden Staff,
   Inspire) so the 20%-extra-ATK proc fires more often (it is per Basic ATK, so more attacks = more
   procs = compounding).
5. **Protect and position the carry** (it is the whole board's damage); the traits help (CC immunity +
   shield), but a dead carry is a dead board.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): equip the spear, find the carry
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Equip the free spear on my best early auto-attacker. Buy toward a Marksman
  line. Creep: an **ATK-Speed basic (Inspire)** or a damage basic for the spear carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take the exclusive** (spear + **30% Hybrid ATK**, shop fills with traits, **1
  free trait**): a big early spear power-up that also jump-starts the trait collection. Keep it. (The
  borrowable just re-grants the spear I already have, redundant on Zilong, so never take it.)
- **I-3 / I-4:** buy the **first trait** (Control Immunity & Lifesteal), build the front, hold gold toward
  the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** an ATK-Speed / damage **Enhanced** item for the spear carry.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): stack the carry, cross breakpoints
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a free +1 count (Marksman or a frontline synergy).
- **Stack the spear carry:** ATK-Speed + damage items, cross **Marksman [4]** (team + carry ATK speed,
  which multiplies the spear) and a frontline [2].
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** an ATK-speed / item bundle (Manic Slam = Golden Staff, The Hunt) for the
  carry, or a synergy payoff.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's **best ATK-Speed / damage Enhanced items** and a
  Synergy Magic Crystal. Gold Bandit (if bought) helps fund bids.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier (L8-9 for a 4-cost marksman).

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): full 3-trait spike
- **Buy the 2 extra traits** (Dragon's Roar): the full 3-trait spear (CC immunity + shield + Gold Bandit
  or a 2nd damage-relevant pick) makes the carry uninterruptible, survivable, and self-funding.
- **Spike** the carry (star-up, Marksman [6], a synergy payoff card). Enhance the spear.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more carry gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Spear maxed (Enhanced + 3 traits) + stacked items + Marksman [6]. The carry's compounding attacks shred
  in the long Battle-Acceleration window. Close before HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Standard for a single-carry comp, but he also spends gold on **traits** (up to 3), so budget for them on
top of leveling/rolling. Level to the carry's tier (L8-9 for a 4-cost marksman, L9-10 for Lesley), reroll
for the star-up and front. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The Gold Bandit trait can partly
self-fund the economy if the carry attacks a lot.

## Best comps (concrete)

Built around **one auto-attack carry holding the spear**:
- **Marksman carry** ([[../marksman_carry]]): Karrie (4c, dual-wield = many attacks, ideal spear host) or
  Irithel/Lesley/Miya, with Marksman [4]/[6] (ATK Speed multiplies the spear) and a Defender/Dauntless
  front. The premier Zilong line.
- **Basic-attack bruiser carry:** Masha (3c, ATK-speed steroid) or Alucard (1c, basic-ATK nuke) holding
  the spear on a Bruiser/Weapon Master board, a front-carry variant.
- The constant: an **auto-attacker** (not a caster), Marksman ATK-Speed to compound the spear, a wall to
  buy ramp time.

## Positioning

The spear carry is usually a **back-row Marksman**, seat it **back-center, never cornered** (Swiftblade
divers). The **Control Immunity trait** lets it attack through CC, and the **Low-HP Shield** trait buys a
death-window, so it tolerates a slightly greedier seat than a normal carry, but it still needs the wall
and a corner-guard. A front-carry (Masha/Alucard) variant sits front but still wants peel. Full detail:
[[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## A worked line (one ideal game)

I-1 L3: equip the free spear on an early Marksman (Brody), take Inspire off the creep. I-2: take the
exclusive (spear + 30% ATK, a free trait, shop full of traits), pick Control Immunity & Lifesteal. Stage
I-II: I commit a Marksman line, find Karrie (the dual-wield ideal host), move the spear onto her, stack
Golden Staff + ATK-speed items; the 20%-extra-ATK proc fires constantly with her attack rate. Auction: I
win an Enhanced Golden Staff for Karrie. III: Dragon's Roar lets me buy 2 more traits (Low-HP Shield +
Gold Bandit), Karrie is now uninterruptible, self-healing, shielded, and self-funding; I cross Marksman

long fights. 1st, unless someone deletes her before she ramps.

## Power-card deep dive

His **exclusive** is strong and on-kit (spear + **30% Hybrid ATK**, the shop refreshes to show traits,
and a **free trait**), a big early spike for the artifact and a head start on the 3-trait collection.
Keep it. The **borrowable** Zilong card is a **trap on Zilong himself** (it re-grants the spear he already
starts with), it exists for *other* commanders to borrow. Use the 2 refreshes only if a breakpoint card
(a Marksman Badge/Blessing) or capacity (Fanny's Power) would win more, rare on him.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Burst / assassins / hard CC** (Ling, Swiftblade, Lukas stun): they kill or lock the carry before it
  ramps, the spear needs time and attacks. Rush the **Control Immunity** and **Low-HP Shield** traits and
  position to survive the dive.
- **Other equipment commanders** (Minotaur, Ruby, Karina): contest gear lots; I need the pool a bit less
  (the spear is free) but I still want ATK-Speed items, so bid where the carry's BiS overlaps.
- **Anti-heal** boards blunt the Lifesteal trait; lean on the shield + raw damage instead.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **No good auto-attacker yet:** put the spear on the best basic-attacker I have (even a cheap one) until
  the keeper carry arrives; the spear works on anyone who auto-attacks.
- **Carry getting deleted before it ramps:** rush the survival traits (Control Immunity, Low-HP Shield),
  re-position (back-center, corner-guard, peel), and consider a sturdier carry.
- **Gold-starved (traits cost gold):** buy Gold Bandit first to self-fund, or delay the 2nd/3rd trait
  until the econ recovers; do not break the interest band for a trait early.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** double down on the carry's spear + items + traits; the artifact is a
  strong all-in anchor.

## The edge (the secret)

**A free, scaling artifact that multiplies one carry's attacks and tunes to the lobby.** The Dragon
Spear's ~40%-more-attacks compounds with ATK Speed, and the three traits make the carry uninterruptible,
self-healing, survivable, and even self-funding, all bolted onto a single auto-attacker that he gears
ahead of the field from round 1. A high-floor equipment carry whose artifact never bricks.

## Pitfalls

- **Spear on a caster** (skill-damage) carry, it keys off Basic ATKs, so a Mage wastes it.
- **Taking the borrowable card** (redundant, re-grants the spear).
- **Wrong trait order** (e.g. Gold Bandit when the carry is dying, buy survival first).
- **Under-stacking ATK Speed:** the spear proc is per Basic ATK, so without ATK-Speed items/synergy the
  multiplier underdelivers.
- **Spreading items** instead of funneling onto the one spear carry.

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Burst / assassin / CC** boards are his nightmare: kill or lock the carry before it ramps and the whole
board's damage is gone (mitigated by the Control Immunity + shield traits and good position, but real).
**Other equipment commanders** contest his gear. **Anti-heal** blunts the lifesteal trait. He is
strongest into **long fights** where the spear + ATK Speed compounds and boards that cannot reach his
carry; weakest into fast burst that deletes the single threat. High floor (free artifact), ceiling capped
by being a one-carry plan.

## Numbers that matter (the artifact, quantified)

- **Spear proc:** 20% chance per Basic ATK to add **2 extra ATKs** = on average **~+0.4 attacks per
  attack (~40% more hits)**, and it is *per Basic ATK*, so it **compounds with ATK Speed** (more attacks
  -> more procs). Enhanced spear adds **+20% Hybrid ATK**.
- **Traits:** Control Immunity 25s + **16%** Lifesteal; Gold Bandit **5%/ATK** for 1 gold; Low-HP Shield
  **50% Max HP** at <30% (or on fatal DMG). Up to **3** (1 base + 2 from the star-up).
- **Marksman synergy** (the host): **[4]** = +10% ATK Speed all, +45% marksmen; **[6]** = +20% / +90%.
  That ATK-Speed is what turns the spear from "nice" into the lobby's DPS.

## Stage checkpoints (am I on pace?)

- **End of Stage I (~L5):** spear equipped on an auto-attacker, the exclusive's free trait + 30% ATK
  banked, first bought trait coming. *Behind = no auto-attacker? Park the spear on any basic-attacker
  until the keeper carry shows.*
- **End of Stage II (~L7-8):** spear on the keeper Marksman carry, Marksman [4] + a front [2], 1-2 traits,
  Enhanced items won. *Behind = carry doing little? Stack ATK Speed; the proc needs attacks.*
- **Stage III (~L9):** full **3 traits** (Dragon's Roar), Enhanced spear, Marksman [6], pressing wins.
  *Behind = carry dying? Rush Control Immunity + Low-HP Shield, reposition.*
- **Stage IV (L10):** spear maxed + items, closing on the compounding DPS.

## The 7-archetype read (who I face, and the adjustment)

- vs **burst / assassin / CC** (Ling, Swiftblade, Lukas): they delete or lock the carry before it ramps,
  rush the survival traits + corner-guard + peel. My hardest matchup.
- vs **other equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Karina): contest gear, I need the pool a bit less (spear is
  free), so bid only on the carry's ATK-Speed best-in-slot.
- vs **anti-heal** boards: they blunt the Lifesteal trait, lean on the shield + raw damage.
- vs **go-wide / multi-carry** (Neobeasts, Mystic Meow): they out-body my single threat, I must out-DPS.
- vs **econ / value** (Lancelot, Aurora, Diggie): my gear lead wins the early-mid, close before they
  scale; Gold Bandit keeps my econ near theirs.
- vs **vertical** (Aamon, Alice): I match their carry with my hyper-scaled spear carry.
- vs **synergy grant / free-hero** (Gusion, Lunox, Guinevere, Vale): I commit a Marksman line and gear it;
  ignore their wide-pool lane.
