## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat / auto-attacker amplifier. She adds %-max-HP arrow volleys keyed to a chosen
  carry's attacks and speeds it up, anti-tank damage on a marksman.
- **Plays like:** a marksman booster, pick an auto-attack carry, give it +50% ATK speed, and rain %-HP
  arrows tied to its attacks, melting tanks and carries.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a one-target amplifier + a normal economy; the skill is the
  auto-attacker comp and protecting the carry.
- **Best into:** tanky/high-HP enemy boards (the %-HP arrows shred them). **Worst into:** non-auto-attacker
  boards (the proc keys off basic ATKs), burst on the carry, and economy commanders.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Miya, "Greenwood Archer." Skins: Greenwood Archer (other skin labels not legible in the
  source). (Note: the **commander** Miya is the player avatar, distinct from the **hero** Miya, a 3-cost
  Marksman/Heartbond unit in [[../../infos/heroes/cost_3]]. This file is the commander.)
- **Lunar Longbow** [Active] (verbatim): *"Choose an allied Hero for coordinated attacks. After every 6
  Basic ATK(s) of the chosen Hero, Miya rapidly fires 5 arrow(s) at enemies, each dealing Physical DMG
  equal to 7% of the target's Max HP."*
- **Moonshade Arrow** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"Miya fires additional 3 split arrow(s), each
  dealing Physical DMG equal to 4% of the enemy's Max HP. Additionally, the chosen Hero gains 50% ATK
  Speed."*
- **Power Card:** *Miya's Power* (the I-2 offer always includes it) - *"Gain 12 Gold. Obtain Lunar Longbow
  (+20% ATK Speed; on kill restores 20% Max HP to an ally)."*

Reading (in pencil): she **amplifies one auto-attack carry**, after every **6 Basic ATKs** of the chosen
hero she fires **5 arrows (7% target Max HP each)** + (starred) **3 split arrows (4% Max HP)** and gives
the carry **+50% ATK Speed** (so it attacks faster -> procs the volley faster). The damage is
**%-of-Max-HP**, so it **shreds tanks** (their big HP pools), a strong anti-frontline tool on a marksman.
The card adds **+12 gold + the Lunar Longbow item** (ATK speed + heal-on-kill). She is the
**auto-attacker's Angela**: mana-for-casters becomes ATK-speed-and-%-HP-arrows for marksmen.

Rethink (2026-07-10, first principles): two multiplications hide in the kit. The +50% ATK Speed is
**self-synergistic**, it raises the carry's own DPS AND the volley's proc rate at once, so the
starred kit is worth more than its two halves read separately; and the %-Max-HP arrows are the
**anti-tank inversion**, the enemy's Dauntless/Defender HP investment literally feeds her damage,
so she is strongest against exactly the walls that blank normal marksmen. The conditionality is the
same shape as Angela's: the whole kit keys off one auto-attacker's Basic ATKs, so a CC'd, dead, or
absent carry zeroes it, general-list B, tank-meta S. Trick era: combat-only kit, hosts the
shop-lock freely; the +12g card plus trick gold covers her missing econ.

## Win condition / identity

**A fast auto-attack carry that melts everything, especially tanks.** The +50% ATK speed makes the carry
both DPS faster and trigger Miya's %-HP arrow volleys more often, and %-max-HP damage ignores armor
stacking, so a tanky enemy front folds. She is a **combat amplifier** for a marksman/auto-attacker board:
pick the carry, speed it up, rain anti-tank arrows, and out-DPS the lobby. The %-HP angle makes her
especially strong into the heavy-frontline metas other carries grind against.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **One auto-attacker.** The coordinated attacks key off a chosen hero's **Basic ATKs**, so pick a
   **fast, consistent auto-attacker** (a marksman), not a caster (skill-damage wastes the proc). Stack ATK
   speed on it (the proc is per-6-attacks, so faster = more volleys).
2. **Combat-only kit, run the economy.** Beyond the card's +12 gold, the kit pays no gold, so run a normal
   economy alongside.

## The amplify plan (her signature, concrete)

The ordered way to boost the carry:
1. **Pick a fast auto-attack carry** (a marksman: Karrie, Lesley, the hero Miya, Irithel) for the
   coordinated attacks.
2. **Lunar Longbow on it every prep** (+50% ATK speed starred), so it attacks faster and procs the %-HP
   volley (5 + 3 arrows) more often.
3. **Stack ATK speed** (Golden Staff, Inspire, Marksman synergy) on the carry, the faster it attacks, the
   more arrow volleys, the proc scales with attack rate.
4. **Lean on the %-HP damage vs tanks** (the arrows shred high-HP fronts), the carry's own damage handles
   squishies.
5. **Protect and position** the carry (it is the whole plan); the card's heal-on-kill item adds sustain.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): build a marksman carry
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build toward a marksman/auto-attacker line. Creep: an **ATK-speed (Inspire)**
  basic for the carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take her card** (+12 gold + the Lunar Longbow item): econ + an ATK-speed/heal
  item for the carry, on-kit. Keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** Lunar Longbow on the carry; bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** an ATK-speed/damage item or a marksman hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): amplify, cross breakpoints
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; cross Marksman [4] + a frontline [2].
- **Lunar Longbow on the carry (+50% ATK speed starred)**, stack ATK speed; the %-HP arrows shred the
  enemy front. Bank.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's ATK-speed/damage item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike the carry
- **Spike** the carry (star-up, Marksman [6], a payoff card); with +50% ATK speed + the arrow volleys it
  out-DPSes the lobby and melts tanks. Press the round wins.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, gear the carry.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Items maxed, ATK speed stacked, %-HP arrows shredding. Close before HP is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Standard for the marksman comp: sit at the carry's tier (L8-9 for a 4-cost marksman), reroll for the
star-up and support, level for capacity. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The card's +12 gold helps; run a normal
economy (the kit gives no other gold).

## Power-card deep dive

Her card (**+12 gold + the Lunar Longbow item**: +20% ATK speed, heal-on-kill) is **on-kit**, econ + an
ATK-speed/sustain item for the carry, smoothing both economy and the carry's ramp. Keep it. Deviate only
if a synergy/capacity card clearly wins more for a committed line. Use the 2 refreshes to confirm.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Burst / assassins** on the carry (it needs ramp time), position + peel; the carry is the whole plan.
- **CC** that locks the carry stops its attacks (and the proc), tech Purify.
- **Non-tank squishy boards** reduce the %-HP arrows' edge (they shine vs HP); the carry's own damage still
  carries.
- **Economy commanders** out-resource me (combat kit + 12 gold); convert the combat edge to HP.

## Contingency and pivots

- **No auto-attacker:** her kit needs a basic-ATK carry, so prioritize a marksman; a caster wastes the
  proc.
- **Carry getting deleted before it ramps:** re-position (back-center, corner-guard), peel, gear sustain
  (the card's heal-on-kill item).
- **Behind on econ:** tighten the interest band (combat kit).
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** spike the carry with ATK speed + items and close.

## The edge (the secret)

**An auto-attack carry that melts tanks.** The +50% ATK speed makes the carry DPS faster *and* proc Miya's
%-max-HP arrow volleys more often, and %-HP damage ignores armor, so a tanky enemy front folds where other
carries would grind. She is the marksman's amplifier and a hard answer to the heavy-frontline metas.

## Pitfalls

- **Amplifying a caster** (the proc keys off basic ATKs; pick an auto-attacker).
- **Under-stacking ATK speed** (the proc is per-6-attacks; faster = more volleys).
- **Under-protecting the carry** (it is the whole plan; it needs ramp time).
- **Neglecting economy** (combat kit) or **forgetting Purify vs CC**.

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Burst / assassins / CC** that delete or lock the carry before it ramps (it needs attack time). **Economy
commanders** out-resource her over a long game. She is strongest into **tanky/high-HP** boards (the %-HP
arrows shred them) and **long fights** (ATK speed compounds); weakest into fast burst on the carry. A
marksman amplifier and anti-tank specialist.

## Numbers that matter (the amplify, quantified)

- **The volley:** after every **6 Basic ATKs** of the chosen carry, **5 arrows (7% target Max HP each)** +
  (starred) **3 split arrows (4% Max HP)** = a big **%-of-Max-HP** burst, anti-tank (ignores armor
