## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat / damage-into-recruitment. A laser hologram adds free DMG, and dealing damage
  feeds free heroes of my top synergy.
- **Plays like:** a high-damage snowball, build a hard-hitting synergy board, and let its damage print free
  heroes of that synergy, compounding it.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a free DMG source + a damage-scaled recruiter; the skill is a
  high-damage synergy that hits the thresholds fast.
- **Best into:** high-damage synergy comps (Marksman/Mage). **Worst into:** low-damage / slow boards (the
  recruitment starves) and fast aggro before it ramps.

Reading (in pencil): a **hologram** adds **free laser DMG** (340 Physical, scaling **+3% ATK speed/round**,
so it ramps), and the star-up turns **damage dealt into free heroes**: every **8000 DMG**, a free hero of
my **highest synergy** next round, plus the card's **1 hero per 50000 ally DMG**. So a **hard-hitting
board prints free synergy heroes**, compounding the very synergy doing the damage. She is a
**damage-into-recruitment** engine: the more I hit, the more free bodies of my line I get.

Rethink (2026-07-10, first principles): the recruited heroes are the good currency (denial-immune
synergy thickeners aimed at my own densest line, copies the pool cannot refuse me), but the
**causality runs the wrong way for a floor**: damage must exist before the engine pays, so she
amplifies strength and cannot rescue weakness, the same win-more shape as Diggie with a narrower
trigger. The honest pilot conclusion: treat the kit as a LATE multiplier and build the early-mid
like a kit-less commander (normal econ, normal tempo); count on the hologram's ramp (+3%/round
compounds quietly into a real free carry by Stage III) rather than the recruitment to carry the
early game. Trick era: shop-independent, hosts the lock freely, which matters since her kit pays no
gold at all.

## Win condition / identity

**A high-damage synergy that recruits itself.** Build a hard-hitting synergy board (Marksman/Mage); its
damage crosses the 8000/50000 thresholds and hands free heroes of that synergy, thickening it toward
[6]/[10] for free, while the hologram adds scaling laser DMG. She is a **combat snowball**: damage feeds
synergy feeds more damage. The skill is a board that *deals a lot* fast, so the recruitment engine spins.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Maximize damage (to feed the engine).** The free heroes scale with damage dealt, so a **high-DMG
   carry + synergy** (and the hologram's ramp) makes the recruitment pour; a low-damage board starves it.
2. **Commit one high synergy.** The free heroes come from my **highest activated synergy**, so **commit
   one line hard** (so the free bodies thicken *it*, not a random spread), ideally a damage synergy that
   both hits hard and benefits from more members.

## The recruitment plan (her signature, concrete)

The ordered way to run the engine:
1. **Build a high-damage synergy** (Marksman/Mage) with a real carry, so I cross the damage thresholds
   fast.
2. **Position the hologram** to laser through the enemy (it ramps +3% ATK speed/round) for free extra DMG.
3. **Let the damage print free heroes** of my top synergy (every 8000 / 50000 DMG), thickening the line
   toward [6]/[10] for free.
4. **Commit one synergy** so the free bodies all reinforce it (not a scatter).
5. **Snowball:** more members = more damage = more free members; bank the gold the free heroes save for
   carries/star-ups.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): build toward a damage line
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build toward a high-damage synergy (Marksman/Mage). Creep: a damage/ATK-speed
  basic for the carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take her card** (free hero per 50k ally DMG) *if* committing a damage board (it
  scales with damage); a generic synergy/econ card if my damage is low early. Situational, lean keep on a
  damage line.
- **I-3 / I-4:** build the damage core; the hologram adds laser DMG. Bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or a member of my damage synergy.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): ramp damage, recruit free heroes
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; commit the damage synergy.
- **The damage thresholds start paying** free heroes of my top synergy; thicken the line toward [6]. The
  hologram ramps.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's damage item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** to the carry's tier + capacity (to field the free heroes).

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): the snowball
- **More members = more damage = more free members**; the synergy reaches [6] (toward [10]) partly free.
  Spike, take a payoff card (Purest Power on the [6]).
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, gear the carry.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Lock the high-damage, self-recruited board, dump banked gold into star-ups and items. Close before HP is
  the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Level for **capacity** (to field the free heroes the damage prints) and the carry's tier. The upgrade
ladder, cumulative from L3: `L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The free
heroes save gold for star-ups; keep a healthy economy (the kit gives no gold directly).

## Best comps (concrete)

**High-damage synergy lines that self-recruit:**
- **Marksman** (Karrie/Irithel/Lesley/Miya): high sustained DMG crosses the thresholds fast, free marksmen
  thicken it.
- **Mage** (Kagura/Odette/Vexana): burst damage feeds free mages.
- A **deep faction** that both hits hard and wants many members (Astro, Emberlord), the free heroes push
  toward [10].
- The constant: a board that **deals a lot**, committed to **one** synergy the free heroes reinforce.

## Power-card deep dive

Her card (a **free hero per 50000 ally DMG** of my top synergy) is **strong on a damage board** (it
compounds the recruitment), but **scales with damage**, so it is weak if my board does not hit hard. Take
it on a committed damage line; **deviate to a generic** if my early damage is low. Situational, lean keep
on a damage comp. Use the 2 refreshes to weigh it.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Fast aggro** ends the game before the damage engine ramps, build damage early and protect HP.
- **Low-damage matchups / heavy defense** slow my thresholds (less recruitment); lean on raw carries.
- **Economy commanders** out-resource me (no gold kit), but the free heroes save gold; convert it to
  carries.

## Contingency and pivots

- **Low damage (engine starving):** prioritize a real carry + damage items so the thresholds hit; the kit
  is dead on a weak board.
- **Spread synergies (no clear highest):** commit one so the free heroes reinforce it.
- **Behind early:** the hologram adds free DMG to stabilize; build the damage core.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** field the self-fed synergy + carry and close.

## The edge (the secret)

**A board that recruits itself by dealing damage.** Layla's damage crosses thresholds that hand free heroes
of her top synergy, so a hard-hitting line thickens toward [6]/[10] for free while the ramping hologram
adds laser DMG, more damage buys more members buys more damage. A combat snowball that turns offense into
recruitment.

## Pitfalls

- **A low-damage board** (the recruitment engine starves; she needs to hit hard).
- **Scattering synergies** (the free heroes come from the *highest*, so commit one).
- **Taking her card on a weak board** (it scales with damage; deviate if low).
- **Wasting the hologram's angle** (position it to penetrate the enemy line).

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Fast aggro** ends the game before the engine ramps. **Heavy defense / low-damage matchups** slow the
recruitment thresholds. She is strongest in **high-damage synergy comps** that hit the thresholds fast
(self-recruiting toward [6]/[10]) and weakest in fast finishes or on a low-damage board. A combat snowball
gated by how hard her board hits.
