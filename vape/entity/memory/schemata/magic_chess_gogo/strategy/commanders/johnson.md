## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat / summon synergy-filler. Free Johnnies carry 2 synergies each and AoE-stun, so he
  stacks a synergy cheaply with disruption.
- **Plays like:** a body-printer, summon free 3-Gold Johnnies that fill a synergy and CC the enemy, then
  back them with real carries.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a free-body engine; the skill is committing the synergy the Johnnies
  roll and pairing them with real damage.
- **Best into:** synergy-greedy comps and clustered enemy boards (the AoE stun). **Worst into:** spread
  boards (less AoE value) and fast aggro before the Johnny board forms.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Johnson, "Wild Engine." Skins: Wild Engine, Swifting Heart. (There is no *hero* Johnson in the
  S6 shop roster; the Johnnies he summons are special units.)
- **Johnny, Go!** [Active] (verbatim): *"Tap the skill to open an exclusive shop. Choose 1 out of 5
  Johnnies (3-Gold) for free. Each Johnny has 2 different Synergies randomly selected from Johnson's
  Synergy Pool. After selection, the Shop will only refresh Johnnies with the same Synergies. The exclusive
  shop displays up to 2 different Faction Synergies. Johnny's skill \"Spanner Spin\": Fling a wrench at the
  area with the most enemies, dealing DMG and stunning them for 0.5 / 0.7 / 1 s. Johnnies obtained by other
  Commanders do not have Synergies."*
- **Transform!** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"When battle starts, Johnny charges toward the area
  with the most enemies, dealing Magic DMG equal to 10% of his Max HP to surrounding enemies. Johnny is
  immune to crowd control while transformed."*
- **Power Cards:** exclusive *Johnson's Power* - *"Increases Johnnies in the Hero Pool by 5. Johnny gains
  25% Hybrid ATK and 20% Max HP."* **(Exclusive)**; borrowable *Johnson's Power* - *"Open Johnson's Skill
  Shop to pick a Johnny (3-Gold) with 2 random Synergies; all future Johnnies share those Synergies."*

Reading (in pencil): he summons **free 3-Gold Johnnies**, each carrying **2 synergies** (and the shop then
refreshes Johnnies of those **same** synergies), so he **stacks a synergy with free bodies** that also
**AoE-stun** (Spanner Spin) and, starred, **charge for AoE magic DMG + are CC-immune** (Transform!). The
exclusive **buffs the Johnnies** (+25% ATK / +20% HP, +5 in pool, making them real threats); the
borrowable just re-opens the skill shop, redundant on the owner. He is a **free-body synergy filler with
disruption**.

Rethink (2026-07-10, first principles): "cheap synergy bodies" undersells two properties. (1) The
Johnnies are **denial-immune breakpoint currency**: they come from HIS exclusive pool (and lose
their synergies if stolen), so no bench-holding or contention touches his counts, the same immunity
class as Gusion's grants. (2) The Spanner Spin stun + the starred CC-immune charge is a **per-fight
tempo opener** in the Lukas family: an AoE stun on the enemy's densest tiles is 0.5-1s of free
damage every single fight, small but universal fight-flip value. What caps him at B is structural:
the kit supplies enablers only, no carry and no econ, so a real damage core must come from the shop
like anyone's. Trick era: shop-independent kit, hosts the shop-lock freely, which patches the
no-econ half.

## Win condition / identity

**Stack a synergy for free with Johnnies, add CC, and back it with real carries.** The free Johnnies fill
a faction/class cheaply (2 synergies each) and disrupt with AoE stuns, so I reach breakpoints without
spending the gold a normal board would, banking it for real carries. With the exclusive the Johnnies hit
hard enough to be a board on their own. He is a **combat synergy-engine**: cheap breakpoints + disruption,
topped with a couple of bought carries.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Commit the synergy the Johnnies roll.** Each Johnny has 2 random synergies and the shop then locks to
   those, so **the first Johnnies set my axis**, pick a Johnny whose synergies I want to stack, then the
   refresh feeds that line.
2. **Free bodies vs real carries.** Johnnies are cheap synergy-fillers but (without the exclusive) modest
   damage, so **pair them with real bought carries**, the Johnnies turn synergies on and CC, the carries
   do the damage.

## The Johnny plan (his signature, concrete)

The ordered way to run the engine:
1. **Take the exclusive** (+5 Johnnies in pool, +25% ATK / +20% HP), making the Johnnies real threats, not
   just chumps.
2. **Open the skill shop and pick a Johnny** whose 2 synergies I want to stack (the shop then refreshes
   that line), banking my gold.
3. **Field free Johnnies to stack the synergy** + the AoE stun (Spanner Spin), reaching breakpoints cheaply
   and disrupting clustered enemies.
4. **Spend the banked gold on real carries** that the Johnny-stacked synergy supports.
5. **Position the Johnnies to charge the enemy cluster** (Transform!, AoE magic + CC-immune) for max
   disruption.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): pick the Johnny line
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Open the skill shop, pick a Johnny whose synergies I want; build around them.
  Creep: a flexible damage basic for a real carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take the exclusive** (+5 pool Johnnies, +25%/+20%), NOT the borrowable
  (redundant re-open). It turns Johnnies into threats; keep it.
- **I-3 / I-4:** field free Johnnies to stack the synergy, bank gold for carries.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or a real carry hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): stack the synergy, add carries
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count (stacks with the Johnny synergy).
- **Field Johnnies to cross a [4]/[6]** of the locked synergy + AoE stun; spend banked gold on real
  carries.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for a carry item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** for capacity (more Johnnies + carries) and the carry's tier.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike with Johnnies + carries
- **The free-Johnny synergy + buffed Johnnies + real carries** spike together; take a payoff card (Purest
  Power on the Johnny synergy if [6]).
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, gear the carry.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Lock the Johnny-stacked board + carries, dump banked gold into star-ups and items. Close before HP is the
  binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Level for **capacity** (to field many Johnnies + carries) and the carry's tier; the free Johnnies save
gold, so he can level/roll on a healthy economy. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`.

## Best comps (concrete)

**A synergy stacked by Johnnies + real carries:**
- **Whatever faction/class the Johnnies roll** (the shop locks to it): stack it to [4]/[6] with free
  bodies, add 1-2 bought carries of that line for the damage.
- The AoE-stun Johnnies pair well with a **back-row carry** that benefits from the enemy being CC'd.
- The constant: **free Johnnies for the synergy + CC, bought heroes for the carry.**

## Positioning

Position the **Johnnies to charge the enemy cluster** (Transform! hits the densest area + CC-immune), so
they disrupt the enemy front/back; keep the real carry **back-center** behind the Johnny wall. Spread the
non-Johnny units vs AoE. Full detail: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## A worked line (one ideal game)

I-1 L3: open the skill shop, pick a Johnny with Bruiser + a faction I like; build around it. I-2: take the
exclusive (Johnnies +25%/+20%). II: I field free buffed Johnnies to stack the synergy to [4] + AoE-stun
the enemy cluster, banking gold; I buy a real carry of that line. III: the Johnny synergy [6] + Purest
Power + the carry spike together; the AoE stuns lock the enemy while my carry deletes them. IV: the
free-body board + carry close it. Top placement off a synergy I barely paid for.

## Power-card deep dive

His **exclusive** (+5 pool Johnnies, **+25% ATK / +20% HP**) is the right pick, it turns the free Johnnies
from chumps into a real board, amplifying the whole engine. Keep it. The **borrowable** is a **trap on the
owner** (it re-opens the skill shop he already has), it exists for *others* to borrow Johnnies. Use the 2
refreshes only if Purest Power / a capacity card clearly wins more.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Fast aggro** ends the game before the Johnny board + carries form, pick the line early and protect HP.
- **Spread boards** dodge the AoE stun (less Johnny value); the synergy-stack still holds, but the
  disruption drops.
- **Economy commanders** out-resource me long-term, but the free Johnnies keep my gold healthy; convert
  the saved gold into carries.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **Johnnies rolled a bad synergy:** the first picks set the axis, so pick a Johnny whose synergies I want;
  if stuck, commit the better of the 2 and supplement with bought heroes.
- **Johnnies too weak (no exclusive):** lean harder on bought carries; the Johnnies are then mainly
  synergy-fillers + CC, not damage.
- **Behind early:** the free bodies stabilize a board cheaply; stack the synergy and bank.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** field max Johnnies + carries and close.

## The edge (the secret)

**A synergy stacked for free, with disruption built in.** Free 2-synergy Johnnies let Johnson cross
breakpoints without spending the gold a normal board would (banking it for real carries), while their AoE
stuns lock clustered enemies, and the exclusive makes them real threats. A combat synergy-engine that
turns free bodies into a board plus CC.

## Pitfalls

- **Taking the borrowable card** (redundant, re-opens the skill shop).
- **No real carry** (Johnnies enable synergies + CC but, even buffed, want a damage carry alongside).
- **Letting the Johnnies roll a synergy I do not want** (pick the line with the first Johnny).
- **Spreading into a board the AoE stun cannot reach** (Johnnies want the enemy clustered).

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Fast aggro** ends the game before the Johnny board + carries form. **Spread boards** blunt the AoE stun.
He is strongest in **synergy-greedy, clustered-enemy** games (cheap breakpoints + disruption) and weakest
in fast finishes or vs spread boards. A combat synergy-engine whose power is free bodies + CC.

## Numbers that matter (the engine, quantified)

- **Free bodies:** Johnnies are **3-Gold value, free**, each with **2 synergies**, so a few Johnnies stack
  a [4]/[6] for ~zero gold (banked for carries), and the shop locks to refresh that synergy.
- **The exclusive buff:** **+25% Hybrid ATK, +20% Max HP** to Johnnies (+5 in pool), turning them from
  chumps into a real board.
- **The disruption:** Spanner Spin **stuns 0.5/0.7/1s** (AoE, densest area), and Transform! charges for
  **10% of Johnny's Max HP** as AoE magic + **CC-immune**, real combat value, best vs clustered enemies.

- vs **go-wide / synergy** (Neobeasts, Lunox, Vale): I stack a synergy for free with Johnnies, banking gold

- vs **vertical / copy** (Alice, Aamon): they spike a carry; my AoE stun locks it while my carry answers.

- vs **synergy grant / free-hero** (Gusion, Guinevere): we both generate bodies/counts; I commit the Johnny
  line.
- vs **caster carries** (Angela, Kagura): my AoE stun + CC-immune charge disrupt their casts.
