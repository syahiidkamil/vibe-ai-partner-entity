## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** combat support / caster enabler. She floods one caster carry with mana (more casts) and
  shields it.
- **Plays like:** a skill-carry amplifier, pick the caster, pour mana + a shield into it so it casts more
  and survives, and let the skill damage win.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a one-target protect plus a normal economy she must run herself; the
  skill is the caster comp and protecting the carry.
- **Best into:** caster/mage lobbies (mana = damage). **Worst into:** non-caster boards (her mana buff is
  wasted), CC that locks the carry, and economy commanders who out-resource her.

  with the highest Power. This protection prioritizes Heroes with Mana."*
- **Power Card:** *Angela's Power* (the I-2 offer always includes it) - *"Gain a movable doll. At battle
  start it increases Mana Regen by 70% for allies within 2 tiles to its left and right on the same row."*

Reading (in pencil): she **floods one chosen carry with mana** (Regen to **180%**, so it casts far more
often) and **shields it** (25% Max HP), and the star-up extends protection to the highest-Power hero
(prioritizing mana users). The card adds a **movable doll** that gives **+70% Mana Regen** to same-row
allies near it (a positional mana buff for a caster cluster). She is a **caster enabler**: for a
skill-damage carry, **mana is damage** (more casts), so she turbo-charges exactly that, plus a survival
shield.

Rethink (2026-07-10, first principles): mana on a caster is the same mechanism family as Northern
Vale's battle-start restore ([[../northern_vale_core]]), **cast-rate is the caster's fight-flip**,
but hers is continuous and single-target where NV's is a one-shot team opener, so on the right carry
her per-fight value rivals Kagura's umbrella (more ultimates delivered vs one death blanked). The
whole difference is conditionality: Kagura's %-HP shield pays on ANY body; Angela's mana pays only
where ultimates carry the fight, which is why she is a general-list C and a caster-meta S, the same
kit graded on two boards. Trick-era note: her kit is combat-only (no shop dependence), so she hosts
the shop-lock freely, and her econ weakness (no gold in kit) matters less now that guaranteed
Scavenger gold is a bolt-on.

## Win condition / identity

**A caster carry that casts twice as often and does not die.** Skill-damage carries (mages, Stargazer
casters) are gated by mana, so Angela's mana flood roughly doubles their cast rate while the shield keeps
them alive, turning one caster into a relentless skill machine. She is a **combat support** like Kagura
(protect a carry), but tuned to **mana/casters** rather than raw survival. The board is a caster core; she
is the amplifier.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **One target.** Love's Protection (mana + shield) goes on **one caster carry**, so pick the one whose
   **skill is the damage** (a mage/Stargazer), and stack the doll's row-mana + items on the same cluster.
2. **Combat-only kit, run the economy.** Her kit pays no gold, so I must **run a full economy** (interest
   band, streaks, leveling) alongside; a great mana buff on a broke board still loses.

## The enable plan (her signature, concrete)

The ordered way to amplify the caster:
1. **Build a caster core** (Mage [4]/[6] + Stargazer mana) and pick the primary caster carry.
2. **Love's Protection on the carry every prep** (mana to 180% + a 25% shield), so it casts more and
   survives the focus.
3. **Place the doll (card)** behind the caster cluster so its +70% row-mana hits multiple casters,
   compounding the mana theme.
4. **Stack mana items** (Spellblade Talisman, Dominance Ice, Magic Surge) on the casters, mana is
   damage, so it is nearly as good as raw power here.
5. **Protect and position** the casters (they are squishy); the shield buys one death-window, position
   buys the rest.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): build toward a caster core
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Build toward a mage/caster line. Creep: a magic/ATK-speed or mana basic for
  the eventual carry.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take her card** (the mana doll) *if* I am committing a caster board (it is great
  there); a generic synergy/econ card if my line is not caster-mana. Situational, lean keep on casters.
- **I-3 / I-4:** bank toward the band; her edge is combat, so do not fall behind on econ.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a caster item or a mage hero.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): lock the caster, enable it
- **II-1 (Blessing live):** combine a 2-star for a +1 count; cross Mage [4] + Stargazer [2].
- **Love's Protection on the caster carry**, place the doll behind the caster cluster. Bank, level to the
  carry's tier.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the caster's mana/magic item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): spike the caster
- **Spike** the caster (star-up, Mage [6], Purest Power); with mana flooded it casts relentlessly. Press
  the round wins.
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more caster gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): finalize and close
- Items maxed on the casters, mana + shield holding. The skill machine outpaces the lobby. Close before HP
  is the binding constraint.

## Leveling cadence

Standard for the caster comp: sit at the carry's tier (L8-9 for a 4-cost mage), reroll for the star-up and
casters, level for capacity. The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. No commander-specific pace; play the
caster comp's clock and keep the economy healthy (the kit gives no gold).

## Positioning

Cluster the **casters in a row near the doll** (so its +70% row-mana hits multiple), but **spread vs AoE**
(casters are squishy and clumping invites it), a tension to balance. Carry back-center, never cornered vs
Swiftblade divers; the 25% shield buys one death-window, position buys the rest. Full detail:
[[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **CC commanders** (Lukas stun, hard control): a CC'd caster casts nothing despite the mana/shield, **the
  hard counter**, tech Purify on the carry.
- **Economy commanders** (Lancelot, Aurora, Diggie, Neobeasts) out-resource me (no gold kit); convert the
  combat edge into HP before they scale.
- **Assassins / burst** on the caster: the shield buys one window, position + peel buy the rest.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **No caster carry:** her kit is half-wasted on a non-caster, so prioritize a caster line; if forced
  off-caster, the shield still helps a carry but the mana is dead, lean on a generic plan.
- **Carry CC'd:** tech Purify + control of my own; the mana/shield do not beat a stun-lock.
- **Behind on econ:** tighten the interest band + streaks (no gold kit).
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** spike the caster with items + mana and close.

## The edge (the secret)

**She turns mana into damage on a caster carry.** A skill-damage carry gated by mana casts roughly twice
as often under her 180% flood (plus the doll's row-mana), while the shield keeps it alive, so one caster
becomes a relentless skill machine. For a mage/Stargazer board, mana *is* the damage, and she is the best
amplifier of it.

## Pitfalls

- **Running her without a caster** (the mana flood is wasted).
- **Mana/shield on a non-caster** instead of the skill carry.
- **Clustering casters into an AoE** for the doll's row-mana (balance row-mana vs spread).
- **Neglecting economy** (combat-only kit) or **forgetting Purify vs CC**.

## Threat assessment (who beats her, and why)

**Hard CC** neutralizes the mana-flooded caster (cast nothing while stunned), the hard counter.
**Economy commanders** out-resource her over a long game. **Non-caster matchups** that ignore her mana
theme reduce her to a one-shield support. She is strongest into **caster mirrors / mage metas** (she
out-casts everyone) and weakest into control-heavy or economy-snowball lobbies.

## Numbers that matter (the enable, quantified)

- **Mana flood:** Regen to **180%** on the protected carry (+the doll's **+70%** to same-row allies), so
  casters fire **far more often**, on a skill carry, cast rate *is* DPS, so this is a damage multiplier.
- **The shield:** **25% Max HP** on the carry (a death-window buffer), plus the star-up's extra protection
  to the highest-Power mana user.
- **The catch:** mana does nothing if the carry is **stunned**, so Purify vs CC is the missing half (like
  Kagura's shield-vs-CC limit).
