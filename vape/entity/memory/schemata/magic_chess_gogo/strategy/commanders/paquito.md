## Snapshot

- **Archetype:** duplicate / scheduled vertical. Free Gloves at fixed rounds hand 2 copies of a 3-, then
  4-, then 5-cost hero, star-ups on a timetable.
- **Plays like:** plan the carry to match the schedule, have the right-cost hero ready when each Glove
  arrives, and cash 2 free copies the pool would never give.
- **Difficulty:** medium. The kit is a fixed timeline; the skill is having the matching-cost carry ready
  at II-1 / III-1 / IV-1 to use each Glove well.
- **Best into:** games that reach Stage III-IV (the 4-5 cost Gloves are the payoff). **Worst into:** fast
  aggro that ends before the late Gloves land, and not having a matching-cost carry when a Glove arrives.

## The kit (exact)

- **Title:** Paquito, "The Heavenly Fist." Skins: [not legible in the source screenshot, fill on a cleaner
  capture]. (There is no *hero* Paquito in the S6 shop roster, so no commander/hero collision.)
- **Fist of Glory** [Passive] (verbatim): *"In Round II-1 and Round III-1, obtain Boxing Gloves for 3-Gold
  and 4-Gold Heroes respectively. Equip the Glove to a Hero of the corresponding cost to obtain their
  1-Star copy ×2."*
- **Champ Stance** [Passive, the star-up] (verbatim): *"In Round IV-1, obtain a Boxing Glove for 5-Gold
  Heroes. Equip the Glove to a Hero of this cost to obtain their 1-Star copy ×2."*
- **Power Card:** *Paquito's Power* (the I-2 offer always includes it) - *"Obtain Glory Gloves. Equip to a
  3-Gold-or-lower Hero for their 1-Star copy ×2 and 4 Gold."*

Reading (in pencil): on a **fixed schedule** he gets a Glove that hands **2 free 1-Star copies** of a hero
of the matching cost: **a 3-cost at II-1, a 4-cost at III-1, a 5-cost at IV-1** (and the card adds an early
3-or-lower Glove + 4 gold). Two copies is a huge star-up jump, especially on a **5-cost at IV-1** (2 + my
1 = a **2-star 5-cost**, normally near-impossible: 9 of 9 copies). So he is a **scheduled duplicate
vertical**: the value is fixed in time, and the skill is **having the matching-cost carry ready** to wear
each Glove.

## Win condition / identity

**Star up the carry the pool would deny, on a timetable.** Each Glove turns a single matching-cost hero
into a 3-of-a-kind (2 copies + my 1) for free, so by IV-1 he can field a **2-star 5-cost** plus
star-jumped 3- and 4-costs from earlier Gloves, power the shared pool would never hand out. He is a
**predictable vertical**: I know exactly when the copies come (II-1, III-1, IV-1), so I plan the comp to
have the right carry ready for each.

## The core tension (every round)

Two standing tensions:
1. **Have the matching-cost carry ready when the Glove arrives.** A Glove is wasted if I have no
   3-cost (II-1), 4-cost (III-1), or 5-cost (IV-1) worth copying. So I must **acquire the matching-cost
   carry before each scheduled round**, the kit rewards planning to the timetable.
2. **Which hero of that cost.** The 2 copies go to the hero I equip the Glove on, so equip the **keeper
   carry** of that cost (one that scales and fits my synergy), not a filler. Especially the **5-cost at
   IV-1** (a 2-star 5-cost is the jackpot).

## The Glove schedule (his signature, concrete)

The kit is a fixed timeline, so plan to it:
- **I-2 (card):** Glory Gloves -> 2 copies of a **3-or-lower** hero + 4 gold. Use it on an early cheap
  carry (a 1-2 cost) to jump its star-up, or hold for a 3-cost.
- **II-1 (3-cost Glove):** have a **3-cost keeper carry** ready, equip the Glove -> 2 copies (a 2-star
  3-cost, or toward 3-star). A strong mid-game spike.
- **III-1 (4-cost Glove):** have a **4-cost carry** ready, equip -> 2 copies (a star-jumped 4-cost the
  pool rarely gives).
- **IV-1 (5-cost Glove):** have a **5-cost carry** ready, equip -> 2 copies = a **2-star 5-cost**, the
  jackpot, plan the whole game so I own one 5-cost by IV-1 to cash this.
The discipline: **acquire the matching-cost hero a round or two before each Glove**, so none is wasted.

## Stage-by-stage, round by round

Base DMG by stage: **I = 3 · II = 5-6 · III = 7-11 · IV = 13-15** ([[../../infos/encyclopedia/commander]]).

### Stage I (rounds 1-4): set up the first Glove
- **I-1 (creep):** L3, 2 gold. Normal early board; line up a cheap carry for the card's Glove. Creep: a
  flexible damage basic.
- **I-2 (power card):** **take his card** (Glory Gloves: 2 copies of a 3-or-lower hero + 4 gold): a free
  early star-up jump + gold, on-kit. Keep it. Use the Glove on a keeper cheap carry (or hold for a
  3-cost).
- **I-3 / I-4:** acquire a **3-cost carry** ahead of the II-1 Glove; bank toward the band.
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection:** a carry item or the 3-cost I want for II-1.

### Stage II (rounds 5-10): cash the 3-cost Glove
- **II-1 (3-cost Glove + Blessing):** equip the Glove on my **3-cost keeper carry** -> 2 copies (a 2-star
  3-cost spike). Combine a 2-star for a +1 count. Commit the synergy.
- **Acquire a 4-cost carry** ahead of the III-1 Glove.
- **II-3 (Go Go Card + creep):** a card matching my line.
- **End of Stage II, Auction:** bid for the carry's item or a Synergy Magic Crystal.
- **Leveling:** toward L8 so the 4-cost (III-1) and 5-cost (IV-1) carries are fieldable.

### Stage III (rounds 11-16): cash the 4-cost Glove
- **III-1 (4-cost Glove):** equip on my **4-cost carry** -> 2 copies (a star-jumped 4-cost). Spike the
  board; take a payoff card.
- **Acquire a 5-cost carry** ahead of IV-1 (the jackpot Glove).
- **III-3 / end-of-III Reward Selection:** finish breakpoints, more carry gear.

### Stage IV (rounds 17+): cash the 5-cost Glove, the jackpot
- **IV-1 (5-cost Glove):** equip on my **5-cost carry** -> 2 copies = a **2-star 5-cost**, a power nobody
  else can field. Finalize and close on it.

## Leveling cadence

He must **reach the tiers his Gloves target on schedule**: L6-7 for the 3-cost (II-1), L8 for the 4-cost
(III-1), L9-10 for the 5-cost (IV-1), so a steady level climb is part of the plan (the matching-cost
carry must be *fieldable* when the Glove lands). The upgrade ladder, cumulative from L3:
`L4: 3 · L5: 11 · L6: 31 · L7: 63 · L8: 103 · L9: 157 · L10: 231`. The card's +4 gold helps; level on pace
so no Glove arrives before I can field its cost.

## Best comps (concrete)

Built around a **carry that escalates with the Glove schedule**:
- **A 3-cost into a 4-cost into a 5-cost carry line:** e.g. a Marksman line (Miya 3c at II-1, Karrie 4c at
  III-1, Lesley 5c at IV-1) or a Mage line (a 3-cost mage, Kagura 4c, Odette 5c), so each Glove jumps the
  next carry up the line.
- **Flexible:** the Gloves work on any matching-cost hero, so commit to whatever the pool opens and have
  the right cost ready each scheduled round.

## Positioning

Standard for the carry (back-center for ranged/caster, never cornered vs Swiftblade divers; front for a
bruiser), wall front, spread vs AoE. The star-jumped carries are the investment, protect them. Full
detail: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Power-card deep dive

His card (**Glory Gloves**: 2 copies of a 3-or-lower hero + 4 gold) is **on-kit**, an early star-up jump
on a cheap carry plus gold, smoothing the timeline before the II-1 Glove. Keep it. Deviate only if a
synergy-breakpoint card clearly wins more and I have a strong cheap carry already. Use the 2 refreshes to
confirm.

## What to watch from other players (matchups)

- **Fast aggro** ends the game before the late (4-5 cost) Gloves land, the bulk of his value, so protect
  HP and lean on the early Gloves to stay competitive.
- **Contested carry:** the 2 copies help, but a rival buying my matching-cost carry can mean I lack the
  base hero when a Glove arrives, **secure the carry early**.
- **Cyclops** wants someone to die early; do not be his first kill before the Gloves pay.
- Reading the field: [[../../thinking_and_mental_model/reading_enemy_commanders]].

## Contingency and pivots

- **No matching-cost carry when a Glove arrives:** equip it on the **best hero of that cost I have** (even
  a sub-optimal one) rather than wasting it, or hold it briefly if the rules allow until I acquire one.
- **Behind early:** the early Glove + card gold stabilize; lean on them and reach the schedule.
- **5-cost not secured by IV-1:** buy *any* 5-cost I can to cash the jackpot Glove (2 copies of even an
  off-carry 5-cost is real power), the IV-1 Glove is too valuable to waste.
- **Stage IV, not finalized:** cash the 5-cost Glove and close on the 2-star 5-cost.

## The edge (the secret)

**Pool-denied star-ups on a predictable timetable, climbing the cost ladder.** Paquito knows exactly when
his copies arrive (II-1, III-1, IV-1) and what cost they target, so he plans the carry to each and ends
with star-jumped 3-, 4-, and a **2-star 5-cost** the shared pool would never allow. A scheduled vertical
whose power compounds up the cost tiers.

## Pitfalls

- **No matching-cost carry ready** when a Glove arrives (the Glove is wasted).
- **Gloving a filler** instead of the keeper carry of that cost.
- **Falling behind on level** so the 4-5 cost carries are not fieldable at III-1 / IV-1.
- **Over-extending while waiting** for the late Gloves (the big payoff is Stage III-IV).

## Threat assessment (who beats him, and why)

**Fast aggro** ends the game before the 4-5 cost Gloves land (his biggest value). **A contested carry**
can leave him without the base hero a Glove needs. He is strongest in **long games that reach IV-1**
(where the 2-star 5-cost lands) and weakest in fast ones. A scheduled vertical that rewards reaching the
late game with the right carries staged.

## Numbers that matter (the schedule, quantified)

- **The timetable:** Gloves at **II-1 (3-cost), III-1 (4-cost), IV-1 (5-cost)**, plus the card's early

## The 7-archetype read (who I face, and the adjustment)

- vs **fast aggro / combat** (Lancelot, Ling, Lukas): they end it before the late Gloves, lean on early
  Gloves and protect HP. My hard matchup.
- vs **vertical / copy** (Alice, Aamon, Dyrroth, Lylia): we both build pool-denied carries; mine is
  scheduled, contention only if we share the carry hero.
- vs **high-cost rushers** (Harley, Lancelot): they contest my 5-cost for the IV-1 Glove, secure it early.
- vs **econ / value** (Aurora, Diggie, Chou): slower, my scheduled spikes out-tempo them if I reach the
  late Gloves.
- vs **equipment** (Minotaur, Ruby, Zilong): they want gear, I want matching-cost carries, little overlap.
- vs **synergy grant / free-hero** (Gusion, Lunox, Guinevere, Vale): they go wide; I go tall via the
  Gloves.
- vs **combat** (Kagura, Wanwan): their board is the threat; tech position, reach my schedule.

## Per-Glove carry planning (the cost-ladder in detail)

The kit is a fixed timetable, so the planning is "have the right carry of the right cost ready for each
Glove." A concrete plan by line (in pencil; rosters [[../../infos/heroes/index]]):
- **The card Glove (I-2, 3-or-lower):** use on the **best cheap carry I have early** (a 1-2 cost worth a
  star jump), or hold for a 3-cost if one is close. Low stakes, smooths the start + 4 gold.
- **II-1 Glove (3-cost):** the keeper 3-cost should be a **carry, not glue**, Miya (Marksman/Heartbond),
  Saber/Helcurt (Swiftblade carries), Masha (Bruiser), or a 3-cost that anchors my synergy. 2 copies = a
  2-star 3-cost, a real Stage-II spike. Have it bought *before* II-1.
- **III-1 Glove (4-cost):** the keeper 4-cost, Kagura/Vexana (Mage), Karrie/Irithel (Marksman), Yu Zhong
  (Bruiser), whatever carries my line. 2 copies = a star-jumped 4-cost the pool rarely gives. Buy it (or
  the card may have delivered one) before III-1.
- **IV-1 Glove (5-cost), the jackpot:** own **any strong 5-cost** that fits, Odette (Mage/Astro), Lesley
  (Marksman), Ling, Ruby, by IV-1. 2 copies + my 1 = a **2-star 5-cost**, the single best outcome in the
  game, plan the whole midgame to have a 5-cost in hand for this.
- **The laddered ideal:** a single synergy with a carry at each tier (Marksman: Miya -> Karrie -> Lesley;
  Mage: a 3-cost -> Kagura -> Odette) so every Glove jumps the *next* carry up my own line, compounding
  one comp instead of scattering star-ups.
