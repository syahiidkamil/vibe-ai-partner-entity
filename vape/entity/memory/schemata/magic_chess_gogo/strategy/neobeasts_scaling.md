# Strategy — Neobeasts (economy into scaling)

A faction-tall line that doubles as economy and late scaling: the Neochest accrues points each round
and on ally deaths, paying rewards/gold/EXP *and* buffing the Neobeasts heroes per point. **In pencil**,
from [[../thinking_and_mental_model/synergies]]; verify by play. Spine: [[general_strategy]].

## The idea
Get Neobeasts online early so the points clock starts ticking; the longer it runs, the bigger the
reward payouts and the per-point ATK/HP buff. Because the members span every class, the board goes
*wide* (many class [2]s), which pairs with Mystic Meow (HP per active synergy) and the Solid Alliance
card (ATK per active synergy).

## The board
- **Neobeasts core:** Gatotkaca (2c, dual Bruiser/Defender), Lylia (2c Mage), Brody (1c Marksman),
  Pharsa (4c Stargazer), Fredrinn (3c Weapon Master), Ling (5c Swiftblade). For [4]/[6].
- **Go-wide partners:** Mystic Meow bodies (Lesley, Silvanna, Julian) to cash the many-synergies HP.

## Synergy plan
Neobeasts [4] -> [6] (and [10] only as a Stage-IV dream). Harvest the class [2]s the spread hands me
(Bruiser/Defender via Gatotkaca, Mage via Lylia, etc.). Add Mystic Meow [2] for the wide-board HP.
Purest Power is *harder* here (the spread fights a class [6]); Solid Alliance fits the wide shape
better.

## Econ / level
Points accrue per round and on ally deaths, so being *on board early* is the econ; the chest pays
heroes/EXP/gold/crystals as it climbs (300/700 thresholds give gold). Level to field [6]; the chest's
EXP rewards help. Survive the slow start, then the payouts snowball the mid-late game.

## Cards to look for
Neon Fighters (Neobeasts +12% ATK, +2 points/death), Solid Alliance I/II (per-synergy ATK),
Neobeasts Badge/Blessing for the count, The More the Merrier (gold per synergy) for the wide shape.

## Positioning
Spread (members span roles, and AoE/dragon punish clumping). Protect the casters (Pharsa, Lylia) and
keep Gatotkaca up front. See [[../thinking_and_mental_model/positioning]].

## Win condition / weakness
**Win:** the chest compounds into a strong late board plus an economy nobody else has; a high ceiling
that grows every round. **Weakness:** a **slow start** (weak before points accrue), so HP is at risk
early; protect it (lose cheap, not expensive). Contested Neobeasts (a common Mythic pick) starves the
points; read the lobby before committing.

## When to pick it
Neobeasts heroes flowing and I can survive the slow open. The Mythic econ-scaling line, but only if it
is not already crowded.
