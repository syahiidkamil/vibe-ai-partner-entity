# Middle-Game Tier List — Stage II (rounds 5-10), Commander Lv.6-8 (MCGG S6)

The **mid phase** valuation: which synergies and heroes win the *commit* window, when the 3-costs peak,
the first 4-cost carries arrive, and Blessings let you push a count past your member count. Part of a
three-file set with [[early_game_tier_list]] and [[end_game_tier_list]]; the **commander** layer is
[[commander_tier_list]], the piece-level baseline [[../strategy/tier_lists]]. **In pencil**, scored from
the kits/stats/odds, not yet lived ([[../disclaimer]]).

## What "mid" is (the phase frame)

- **Rounds 5-10 (Stage II)**, Commander Lv.6-8. Capacity has grown; you can field a real comp shape.
- **The shop tilts to 3-cost and cracks 4-cost open.** Draw odds ([[../infos/encyclopedia/hero]]): **Lv.6**
  25/30/**40**/5 (3-cost peaks), **Lv.7** 19/28/**42**/10/1 (5-cost first appears at 1%), **Lv.8**
  16/26/35/**18**/5. So mid = 2-star your 3-cost core, and fish the first 4-cost carry.
- **Blessing online (II-1):** a Blessing Shifter plus the combine-a-2-star +1, so you can push a synergy
  count **one above** your bodies (a [3]->[4], or seed toward a faction [6]). This is the lever that makes
  mid breakpoints reachable a round early.
- **II-3** a Go Go Card + creep equipment; **end of Stage II (II-6) the auction** (bid gold on lots,
  including 4-cost heroes and enhanced gear). Calendar: [[../infos/rounds/index]].

## How mid pieces are scored (the phase triad)

- **Breakpoint (B)** — does it hit a [4] (or a full [3]/[2]-cap) that *flips a fight* this phase?
- **Star-up reach (R)** — can I realistically 2-star it from the pool now (3-cost depth) or pull it
  (4-cost odds), so the power is attainable, not theoretical?
- **Commit-flex (C)** — does committing to it keep me adaptable (splashable, dual-purpose) rather than
  locking me into a brick?

High on all three = S **for the commit window**.

## The caveat (forms of life)

A tier is meaningless stripped of its line ([[../strategy/tier_lists]]). Mid is exactly where the
*commander's* particular list takes over: Vale/Gusion/Lunox bend the breakpoint math; a card-build
(Valir/Yu Zhong) re-ranks everything. This list is the **default commit** when I have no stronger read.

## Synergies — the [4] (and full small-cap) breakpoints that decide mid

This is the breakpoint phase. Ranked by how reliably the breakpoint flips fights *now*.

### S — the mid power spikes
- **Mage [4]** (Cecilion/Lylia/Julian + Kagura/Vexana) -> +80% Magic ATK to mages stacked on 700%+ nukes;
  the single hardest-hitting mid line, and every member is a real unit.
- **Exorcist [4]** (Granger/Saber/Phoveus + Kagura/Yu Zhong) -> **the sustain spike.** The Seal carrier's
  dying allies become Phantoms that recast *the carrier's skill* at **120%**. Put the Seal on **Kagura**
  and every Exorcist death fires another ~770% AoE nuke, so the board keeps dealing damage *as it dies* (a
  built-in second wave). This is why a mid Exorcist core "sustains the damage" even when it loses the front.
- **Dauntless [4]** (Silvanna/Balmond/Franco/Hilda) -> team Shield 10% Max HP + Dauntless +45%; an HP wall
  that wins attrition and protects a carry through the spike.
- **Marksman [4]** (Brody/Granger + Miya/Karrie/Irithel) -> team +10% / marksman +45% ATK speed; ramps a
  back-row auto-attacker into a shredder by the fight's second half.
- **Neobeasts [4]** (Gatotkaca/Lylia/Brody + Pharsa/Fredrinn) -> 30 points/round, the bank from early now
  paying real ATK/HP; econ and scaling at once, goes wide.

### A — strong commits, slightly gated
- **Defender [4]** (Minotaur/Gatotkaca + Tigreal/Khufra) -> +12 team / +30 defender Hybrid DEF; the safe
  front that frees items for the carry.
- **Astro Power [4]** (Minotaur/Martis/Hilda/Helcurt + Aurora) -> Sovereign +40% DMG + 10% lifesteal on the
  most-itemed unit; a real carry-amp if you funnel gear.
- **Emberlord [4]** (Cecilion/Hanzo/Masha/Tigreal + Irithel) -> 3 survivors inherit 25% ATK per death; snowballs
  in a grind.
- **Stargazer [4]** (Eudora/Selena/Luo Yi + Aurora/Pharsa/Lunox) -> 12 mana/s to stargazers; the enabler that
  makes the caster carries cast twice.
- **Bruiser [4]** (Dyrroth/Gatotkaca/Belerick/Masha + Yu Zhong) -> +35% basic DMG + 30% double-hit; a
  self-sufficient bruiser front-carry.
- **Enchanted Tales [4]** (Selena/Belerick + Vexana/Leomord) -> full cap: a Protagonist with enhanced skills +
  fragment scaling; a genuine power line once the two 4-costs land.

### B — full small-caps and slow burns
- **Phasewarper [3]** (Fanny + Clint/Julian/Lancelot) -> +15% DMG + a warp-heal at 40% HP; survivability on
  divers.
- **Scavenger [2]** (Angela + Phoveus) holds; **[3]** waits on Claude (a 5-cost) -> a *late* breakpoint, not mid.
- **Swiftblade [4]** (Saber/Helcurt/Hanzo/Joy + Gusion) -> penetration for an assassin dive; swingy.
- **Northern Vale [3]** waits on Bane (5-cost); **Mystic Meow [2]** is a go-wide splash that pays at high
  total-synergy count. **Kishin [4]** is gear-ramp, value over fight-flip.
- **Dragoncaller [4]** (Balmond/Clint/Eudora + Gusion/Terizla) -> the dragon + 15% DMG; the real payoff is the
  [10], so mid it is a placeholder.

## Heroes — the 3-cost workhorses and the first 4-cost carries

Ranked by **Breakpoint + Star-up reach + Commit-flex**. The 3-costs are the contested core (2-star these);
the 4-costs are the carries you fish.

### S — the mid carries and cores
- **Kagura** (4, Mage/Exorcist, back) — B:H R:M C:H. The mid keystone: a 770% AoE that shields allies, the
  ideal **Exorcist Seal carrier** (deaths recast it), and a full Mage member. Carry + sustain + protector.
- **Khufra** (3, Defender/Heartbond, front) — B:H R:H C:H. A 1s AoE stun + a nuke that scales to 970%; the
  best 3-cost front, pool-deep so easy to 2-star.
- **Vexana** (4, Mage/Enchanted Tales, back) — B:H R:M C:M. AoE knock-up + Protagonist; control and damage in
  one, anchors both Mage and the Tales line.
- **Franco** (3, Dauntless+Weapon Master/Kishin, front) — B:H R:H C:H. **Double class**, a hook+shield
  initiator; fills two breakpoints on a sticky body (9663 HP at 3-star).

### A — strong commits
- **Julian** (3, Mage+Phasewarper/Mystic Meow, front) — **double class**, a zone-of-damage + self-shield; a
  front-line caster that bridges Mage and Phasewarper.
- **Karrie** (4, Marksman/Kishin, back) — %-pen dual-wield, the anti-tank carry that melts the fat fronts mid
  comps lean on.
- **Aurora** (4, Stargazer/Astro, back) — an ice AoE with a huge 3-star ceiling (500%, 13795 HP); strong now,
  a win-con later.
- **Pharsa** (4, Stargazer/Neobeasts, back) — multi-bombardment AoE; the Neobeasts scaling carry.
- **Lunox** (4, Stargazer/Kishin, back) — Chaos Assault that gains casts on kills; snowballs a winning fight.
- **Yu Zhong** (4, Bruiser/Exorcist, front) — a dragon-soul bruiser-carry that doubles as an Exorcist body for
  the Kagura line.
- **Hilda** (3, Dauntless/Astro) / **Tigreal** (3, Defender/Emberlord) / **Masha** (3, Bruiser/Emberlord) —
  the tanky 3-cost cores: a wall, a stun-tank, an ATK-speed bruiser.
- **Miya** (3, Marksman/Heartbond, back) — split-arrows + ATK speed; the Marksman-line auto-attack carry.
- **Saber / Helcurt** (3, Swiftblade) — back-line assassins that delete a squishy mid carry.

### B — situational / role-dependent
- **Clint** (3, Phasewarper/Dragoncaller) / **Luo Yi** (3, Stargazer/Northern Vale) / **Fredrinn** (3, Weapon
  Master/Neobeasts) — fine cores in their line, weaker as standalone carries. Luo Yi rewards a high-skill
  pilot (ramping skill DMG).
- **Gusion** (4, Swiftblade/Dragoncaller, front) — a burst assassin; strong but item- and position-reliant.
- **Irithel / Leomord / Esmeralda / Terizla** (4) — real carries, but their nuclear 3-star ceilings make them
  read better as *late* win-cons; mid they are good 1-2 star bodies if you highroll the pull.

## The phase throughline (how to actually use this)

- **This is the commit window.** Read which line the pool left open in Stage I, then commit by mid Stage II
  and 2-star the core. Floating uncommitted into Stage III is how you fall behind.
- **Use the Blessing to buy the breakpoint a round early** (push [3]->[4], or seed a faction toward [6]). A
  breakpoint one round ahead of the lobby is free wins.
- **The Exorcist Seal goes on your biggest single nuke** (Kagura). The phantom recast scales with the
  Exorcist count, so it pays to run the [4] and carry the AoE.
- **Spend the auction (II-6) on the spike**, not on hoarding: a 2-star 4-cost carry or an enhanced item that
  flips your fight is worth emptying the bank.
- **Protect the carry.** A mid carry dies to assassins/CC; Dauntless shields, a Defender wall, or Kagura's
  shield (or a Kagura-commander) is the difference between a spike and a feed.
