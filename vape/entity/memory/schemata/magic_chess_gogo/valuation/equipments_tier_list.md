# Equipment Tier List — by type and by combo (MCGG S6)

A valuation of the items heroes carry (3 gear slots each; Magic Crystals sit separately and cost no slot).
Given two ways, because an item's worth lives in **who holds it and what it is paired with**: first **by type**
(physical / magic / defense / utility / the levers), then **by strategy combo** (the pairing that makes it
S). Part of the valuation folder beside [[commander_tier_list]], the phase lists, and [[gogo_cards_tier_list]].
Sources: [[../infos/equipments/regular]], [[../infos/equipments/index]],
[[../infos/equipments/commander_exclusive]], [[../infos/equipments/synergy_exclusive]],
[[../infos/equipments/magic_crystal]], [[../infos/equipments/special]].

## Disclaimer — read before trusting a tier

**An item is only as good as the carrier and the comp it lands on.** A tier here is a *default*; it inverts
constantly:
- **No item is universally S.** Golden Staff is S on an on-hit marksman and wasted on a one-cast mage. Holy
  Crystal is S on a burst mage and dead on a basic-attacker. The type-tiers below assume *the right carrier*.
- **A commander can re-rank the whole table.** Minotaur (the gear economy) and Aamon (mirror copies) change
  which items even matter; Ruby/Karina/Zilong bring their own exclusive items that outrank generics on-kit
  ([[commander_tier_list]]).
- **The meta sets the defensive value.** %-Max-HP and %-penetration items (Demon Hunter Sword, Sea Halberd,
  Karrie's kit) are S *when the lobby stacks fat fronts / [10]-HP boards*, ordinary otherwise.
- **Enhanced > Basic, always** — the Enhanced form adds a passive or magnitude; treat "S" as the Enhanced
  form unless noted.
- **Items are whole, not crafted** (no recipe tree) and **drop from creep rounds + the Go Go Box**, not a gold
  shop ([[../infos/equipment_and_economy]]) — so you take what the run offers, which is why the *combo* view
  matters more than the raw ranking.

Held **in pencil**, reasoned not lived ([[../disclaimer]]).

## View 1 — by type (assuming the right carrier)

### Physical ATK (for marksmen / physical carries)
- **S:** **Golden Staff** (+ATK speed that **stacks per Basic ATK**, x50 — snowballs any on-hit/multi-hit
  carry into a shredder).
- **A:** **Demon Hunter Sword** (+%-target-Max-HP on basics — the anti-tank), **Sea Halberd** (%-pen +
  anti-heal), **Haas' Claws** (lifesteal + crit — a self-sustaining carry), **Blade of Despair** (+25/50% DMG
  vs sub-50% targets — the executioner).
- **B:** **Berserker's Fury** (crit/crit-DMG — only S with crit support, i.e. Lesley), **War Axe** (spell-vamp
  bruiser stacks — for a bruiser-carry, not a marksman).

### Magic ATK (for mages / skill carries)
- **S:** **Holy Crystal** (+Magic ATK + flat +Skill DMG — the highest raw mage damage).
- **A:** **Divine Glaive** (Magic pen — vs magic-DEF stacking), **Glowing Wand** (skill-burn %-Max-HP +
  anti-heal — vs fat fronts and sustain).
- **B:** **Ice Queen Wand** (spell-vamp + an ATK-speed slow — sustain/utility over pure damage).

### Defense (for the front line / the tank)
- **S:** **Guardian Helmet** (+%Max-HP + regen — the universal front-survival piece).
- **A:** **Antique Cuirass** (+%HP + DMG-reduction + **lowers enemy Hybrid ATK** — vs a burst lobby), **Thunder
  Belt** (+%Max-HP + a %-Max-HP magic proc — tanky *and* deals), **Oracle** (gives the **whole row** DEF — a
  team multiplier on a stacked row), **Ancient Wrath** (taunt + DEF-per-attacker — a dedicated front-pull).
- **B:** **Blade Armor** (reflect Basic-ATK DMG — **S specifically vs an auto-attacker/marksman lobby**),
  **Dominance Ice** (mana + an ATK-speed slow aura — utility tank).

### Hybrid ATK / utility (force-multipliers, carrier-flexible)
- **S:** **Claude's Theft Device** (copies 2 random items at battle start = **effectively a 3rd and 4th
  piece** on the carrier — a force multiplier on your best-itemed unit), **Winter Crown** (invuln-freeze + heal
  at low HP / lethal — **saves a single carry** through a burst, buys a re-cast or a resurrect window).
- **A:** **Spellblade Talisman** (restore 55/80% Mana at battle start — the slow-mana caster casts
  *immediately*), **Purple Buff** (mana per skill — a caster keeps casting).
- **B:** **Feline Blade** (briefly turns the top-star enemy harmless — single-target soft CC).

### The levers (no gear slot, or a free body — the highest-leverage of all)
- **S:** **Synergy Magic Crystal** (a **bought +1 synergy count** = a breakpoint on demand, *and* it takes no
  gear slot — the cleanest way to hit a [4]/[6] or seed a [10]), **Go Go Magic Crystal** (+1 Hero Capacity),
  **Mirror Device** (copy a hero of a chosen cost = a free copy toward a star-up; the Low-Tier version is
  1-3 cost).
- **A:** **Blessing Shifter** (move/grant a Blessing = a +1 synergy count, repositionable), **Equipment
  Duplicator** (3 copies of a non-special item).

### Synergy-exclusive (only on that synergy)
- **S on-synergy:** **Exorcist's Seal** (the carrier becomes the Phantom source — the engine of the
  Exorcist+Kagura sustain line and the [10] Phantom Lord).
- **A on-synergy:** **Justice Sword** (periodic AoE True DMG, a huge Supreme tier), **Guren Chakram**
  (lifesteal + spell-vamp + scaling DMG), **Nether Claw** (CC + big %HP).

### Commander-exclusive (granted by a Power Card — S on-kit, see [[gogo_cards_tier_list]])
- Ruby's **Crimson/Raging Cloak + Hunting/Raging Scythe** (resurrect + interval DMG), Karina's
  **Shadow/Supreme Blades** (execute + stacking DMG), Zilong's **Great Dragon Spear** (extra-ATK chance on an
  auto-attacker), Chou's Sash, Miya's **Lunar Longbow** (ally heal on kill), Paquito's **Glory Gloves** /
  the **Boxing Gloves** (copy a hero by cost), Essence Core (Blessing-synergy transfer). On their commander
  these outrank generic items; off it, unavailable.

## View 2 — by strategy combo (the pairing that makes it S)

The way items are actually drafted: a carrier + the items that pop off on it.

- **Golden Staff (+ The Hunt bundle) on a multi-hit / on-hit marksman** (Karrie, Irithel, Miya, Brody, Clint)
  -> **S.** Stacking ATK speed compounds every on-hit and every passive proc.
- **Holy Crystal + Glowing Wand on a burst mage** (Kagura, Selena, Lylia, Aurora, Vexana) -> **S.** Flat skill
  DMG + skill-burn = the maximum nuke; Glowing Wand's anti-heal also guts sustain foes.
- **Spellblade Talisman on a slow-mana carry** (Kagura MP60, Vexana, Angela MP100, Irithel MP100) -> **S.**
  Battle-start mana means the big first cast lands *before* the carry dies — the single highest-leverage item
  on a high-mana unit.
- **Demon Hunter Sword / Sea Halberd / Glowing Wand vs the fat-front + [10]-HP meta** -> **S anti-tank.**
  %-Max-HP and %-pen scale with the enemy's HP, so they *grow* against exactly the boards that beat raw damage.
- **Winter Crown on your one hyper-carry** (Alice's carry, a single 3-star, the Astro Sovereign) -> **S
  survival.** The low-HP invuln buys the extra cast / the resurrect; a one-carry comp lives or dies on it.
- **Claude's Theft Device on your best-itemed unit** -> **S.** Copies its two best items at battle start =
  effectively a 4-item carry; a force multiplier on the Sovereign or the hyper-carry.
- **Exorcist's Seal on Kagura (Exorcist active)** -> **S.** Dying Exorcists recast Kagura's ~770%+ AoE; the
  mid-game sustain engine, and the [10] Phantom Lord at the top end (see [[middle_game_tier_list]],
  [[end_game_tier_list]]).
- **Berserker's Fury + Haas' Claws on Lesley** -> **S.** Lesley converts ATK speed to Crit DMG and has 45%
  base crit, so crit items go nuclear on her specifically.
- **Blade Armor (+ Antique Cuirass) on the front vs an auto-attacker lobby** -> **S counter.** Reflect +
  enemy-ATK-down hard-counters marksman/basic-attack boards.
- **Aegis / Guardian Helmet on the front + the carry behind** -> **S floor.** The safest stabilizer when you
  are bleeding HP and need to survive to the spike.
- **A Synergy Magic Crystal to complete a [10] (or a needed [4]/[6])** -> **S.** The +1 count with no slot
  cost is often the last piece of an ultra ([[end_game_tier_list]]).

## The throughline (how to actually equip)

- **Build the carrier first, then draft to it.** Decide your carry and damage type, then take the items that
  compound on it; a perfect item on the wrong unit is a B.
- **Funnel, do not spread.** Three items on one carry (or the Astro Sovereign) beats one item on three units;
  Claude's Theft Device and the Astro "most-itemed" rule both reward stacking.
- **Read the lobby for the defensive pick.** Fat fronts -> %-HP/%-pen; auto-attackers -> Blade Armor; burst
  -> Antique Cuirass / Winter Crown.
- **Crystals and capacity are items too** — a +1 synergy count or a +1 slot often beats a stat stick.
- **Take Enhanced whenever offered**, and prefer the bundle cards ([[gogo_cards_tier_list]]) that hand a
  carrier its whole kit at once (The Hunt, Box of Holy Objects).
