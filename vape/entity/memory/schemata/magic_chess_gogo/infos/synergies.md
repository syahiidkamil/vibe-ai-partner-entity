# Synergies (MCGG S6)

Team bonuses that activate at member-count breakpoints. Two axes: **class** (role: Marksman, Mage,
Defender, ...) and **faction** (origin: Emberlord, Astro Power, ...). A hero usually has one class
and one faction synergy. Field enough of the same synergy to reach a breakpoint and its effect
turns on, stronger at each tier: class synergies cap at [2]/[4]/[6], factions can reach [10].

**Raising a synergy count beyond deploying members:**
- **Blessing** adds +1 to one of a hero's synergy counts (from combining a 2-Star hero, Round II-1
  onward; see [encyclopedia/season_information](encyclopedia/season_information.md)).
- **Synergy Magic Crystal** also grants +1 to a synergy count.

Effect summaries are from the in-game localization. Exact per-tier numbers come from the **Synergy
Guide** UI: Emberlord and Astro Power are filled exact (from screenshots); the rest describe the
mechanic with numbers pending their Synergy Guide captures. Neobeasts point-reward ladder:
[encyclopedia/synergy_guide](encyclopedia/synergy_guide.md).

## Class synergies

### ![](synergy_icons/bruiser.png) Bruiser  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Gatotkaca, Belerick, Badang, Dyrroth, Masha, Yu Zhong
**Effect:** Bruisers' Basic ATKs have a chance to make the next Basic ATK strike faster and harder;
they also gain extra Basic ATK DMG and a chance to hit twice. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/dauntless.png) Dauntless  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Balmond, Franco, Ruby, Hilda, Esmeralda, Silvanna
**Effect:** all allies gain a Shield (a share of Max HP); Dauntless Heroes gain extra Shield.
(Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/defender.png) Defender  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Tigreal, Bane, Minotaur, Gatotkaca, Khufra, Terizla
**Effect:** all allies gain Hybrid DEF; Defenders gain extra Hybrid DEF, doubled in the opening
seconds of the fight. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/weapon_master.png) Weapon Master  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Alucard, Franco, Alpha, Martis, Leomord, Fredrinn
**Effect:** all allies gain Hybrid Lifesteal; Weapon Masters gain extra Lifesteal plus Physical ATK
that scales with HP lost, and heal on kills. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/marksman.png) Marksman  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Miya, Karrie, Irithel, Lesley, Granger, Brody
**Effect:** Marksman Buff: members gain Physical ATK, and their Basic ATKs slow the target.
(Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/mage.png) Mage  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Kagura, Vexana, Odette, Lylia, Cecilion, Julian
**Effect:** all allies gain Magic ATK; Mages gain extra Magic ATK, and on each skill cast gain a
Max-HP shield or freeze a random enemy at higher tiers. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/stargazer.png) Stargazer  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Eudora, Aurora, Pharsa, Selena, Lunox, Luo Yi
**Effect:** all allies restore Mana per second; Stargazers restore extra Mana per second.
(Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/assassin.png) Assassin  (class · 2/4/6)
**Members:** Saber, Helcurt, Gusion, Hanzo, Ling, Joy
**Effect:** all allies gain DMG Increase, Assassins gain extra; Assassins also leap to the enemy
back row at battle start and gain Hybrid ATK on their hits. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/scavenger.png) Scavenger  (class · 2/3)
**Members:** Angela, Claude, Phoveus
**Effect:** the economy synergy, generates extra Gold / free Heroes across the run (keep it active
to the final round for its full payout). (Exact values pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/phasewarper.png) Phasewarper  (class · 2/3)
**Members:** Clint, Fanny, Lancelot, Julian
**Effect:** Phasewarpers gain DMG Increase; the first time a Phasewarper's HP drops low, it warps
to a safe spot and restores HP. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

## Faction synergies

### ![](synergy_icons/emberlord.png) Emberlord  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Tigreal, Alpha, Irithel, Hanzo, Masha, Cecilion
When an Emberlord Hero dies (only the first 5 deaths trigger it), surviving Emberlord Heroes each
gain a bonus of 25% of the fallen Hero's HP and a share of Hybrid ATK.
- **[2]** 1 surviving Hero gains 20% of the fallen Hero's Hybrid ATK.
- **[4]** 3 surviving Heroes each gain 25% of the fallen Hero's Hybrid ATK.
- **[6]** 5 surviving Heroes each gain 35% of the fallen Hero's Hybrid ATK.
- **[10]** All surviving Heroes each gain 50% of the fallen Hero's HP and 85% of their Hybrid ATK.
  A dying Emberlord Hero resurrects with 30% HP.

### ![](synergy_icons/exorcist.png) Exorcist  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Saber, Kagura, Ruby, Granger, Yu Zhong, Phoveus
**Effect:** members obtain the Exorcist's Seal (it converts into Equipment suited to its carrier);
when an Exorcist Hero dies it becomes a Phantom that deals DMG, and Exorcist Heroes gain DMG
Increase and HP. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/heartbond.png) Heartbond  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Miya, Alucard, Fanny, Claude, Khufra, Esmeralda
**Effect:** Heartbond Heroes bond in pairs; a bonded Hero about to die enters Dormancy (invincible,
cannot act) instead, and when one enters Dormancy its partner gains ATK Speed. (Per-tier numbers
pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/astro_power.png) Astro Power  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Minotaur, Hilda, Aurora, Odette, Helcurt, Martis
Astro Power Heroes gain DMG Increase. The Astro Power Hero with the most Equipment becomes the
Astro Sovereign, gaining extra DMG Increase and Hybrid Lifesteal.
- **[2]** +10% DMG Increase. Sovereign: +20% DMG Increase, +5% Hybrid Lifesteal.
- **[4]** +20% DMG Increase. Sovereign: +40% DMG Increase, +10% Hybrid Lifesteal.
- **[6]** +30% DMG Increase. Sovereign: +80% DMG Increase, +15% Hybrid Lifesteal.
- **[10]** +100% DMG Increase. Create a second Astro Sovereign. All Sovereigns gain 300% DMG
  Increase, 40% Hybrid Lifesteal, and 600% Max HP. Their Basic ATKs generate Astro Links, dealing
  True DMG equal to 600% of Adaptive ATK to the target and 4 nearby enemies.

### ![](synergy_icons/dragoncaller.png) Dragoncaller  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Balmond, Clint, Eudora, Gusion, Badang, Terizla
**Effect:** periodically a dragon descends on the most crowded 9-tile area, dealing DMG (a percent
of Max HP) and strengthening Dragoncallers with DMG Increase; at the top tier it also summons a
Divine Dragon. Dragon unit: [heroes/special_units](heroes/special_units.md). (Per-tier numbers
pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/neobeasts.png) Neobeasts  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Gatotkaca, Pharsa, Ling, Lylia, Brody, Fredrinn
**Effect:** members fill a Neochest with points each round and on ally deaths; reaching point
thresholds pays rewards and grants 12/28 Gold at 300/700 points. Reward ladder:
[encyclopedia/synergy_guide](encyclopedia/synergy_guide.md). (Per-tier combat numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/kishin.png) Kishin  (faction · 2/4/6/10)
**Members:** Franco, Karrie, Lancelot, Angela, Lunox, Dyrroth
**Effect:** members obtain Kishin-exclusive Equipment (upgrade it by buying duplicates in the
Shop); Kishin Heroes gain Hybrid DEF. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/enchanted_tales.png) Enchanted Tales  (faction · 2/4)
**Members:** Vexana, Selena, Leomord, Belerick
**Effect:** each match a random Enchanted Tales Hero becomes the Protagonist with enhanced skills;
enemies drop Tale Fragments. (Per-tier numbers pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/mystic_meow.png) Mystic Meow  (faction · 2)
**Members:** Lesley, Silvanna, Julian
**Effect:** all allies gain HP scaled by how many Synergy types are active; at a Synergy-count
threshold you choose between a Red or Blue Mystic Crystal buff. (Exact values pending.)

### ![](synergy_icons/northern_vale.png) Northern Vale  (faction · 2/3)
**Members:** Bane, Luo Yi, Joy
**Effect:** members apply Winter Marks when dealing DMG; enough marks detonate for True DMG, and at
battle start members restore Mana and gain Skill DMG Increase. (Per-tier numbers pending.)
