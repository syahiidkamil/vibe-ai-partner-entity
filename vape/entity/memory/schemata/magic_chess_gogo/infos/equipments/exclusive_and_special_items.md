# Exclusive & Special Items (MCGG S6)

The non-Regular equipment tabs: Magic Crystal, Synergy Exclusive, Commander Exclusive, Special.
Names and effects first-party from the in-game Equipment UI (S6); see [[index]] and
[source_map](source_map.md). Effects filled where a detail panel was captured.

## Magic Crystal (16, complete)
Each is equipped on a hero to grant that synergy (Unique Passive - Potent: "Equip on a hero to grant
`<Synergy>` Synergy"). One per synergy that has a crystal (15 synergies), plus the Go Go crystal.
This is the in-battle source of the Synergy Magic Crystal that adds +1 to a synergy count
([[../synergies/synergies]]).
- **Synergy crystals (15):** Bruiser, Dauntless, Defender, Weapon Master, Marksman, Mage, Stargazer,
  Swiftblade, Emberlord, Exorcist, Heartbond, Astro Power, Dragoncaller, Neobeasts, Kishin.
- **Go Go Magic Crystal** — Unique Passive - Expansion: increase Capacity by 1.

(Scavenger, Phasewarper, Enchanted Tales, Mystic Meow, Northern Vale have no crystal — the same 15
that carry Blessing/Badge cards.)

## Commander Exclusive (15, complete)
Each ties to a Commander's Power Card (the equipment that card grants). Grouped by the UI headers.
- **Defense:** Crimson Cloak, Raging Cloak (both Ruby's).
- **Hybrid ATK:** Lunar Longbow (Miya), Chou's Sash (Chou), Great Dragon Spear (Zilong), Essence
  Core, Shadow Twinblades (Karina), Supreme Blades, Hunting Scythe, Raging Scythe (Ruby).
- **Consumable:** Glory Gloves (Paquito), Tier 3 Boxing Glove, Tier 4 Boxing Glove, Tier 5 Boxing
  Glove, Low-Tier Mirror Device.

Captured effects:
- **Crimson Cloak** — 1-Star Ruby's Cloak. +20% Max HP; Unique Passive - Mastery: the carrier's
  Basic ATKs apply a Rage Mark to 1 enemy; when the marked enemy dies, the carrier restores 10% HP.
- **Tier 3 Boxing Glove** — Copy 3-Gold Hero (Unique Passive - Glory: copy a Hero of a specific cost).
