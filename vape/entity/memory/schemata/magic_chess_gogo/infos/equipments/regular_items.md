# Regular Items (MCGG S6) — the Regular tab

The standard pool: **28 items**, grouped by the UI's type headers. Names and effects are first-party
from the in-game Equipment UI (S6); see [[index]] and [source_map](source_map.md). Each item has a
**Basic** and an **Enhanced** form (the Enhanced toggle raises stats and/or adds a Unique Passive).
Effects are filled where a detail panel was captured; the rest are name-confirmed, effect pending a
per-item capture.

## Basic (creep-round actives)
- **Inspire** — Increase ATK Speed. Unique Passive - Inspire: provide 20/25/30/40% ATK Speed (rises
  with Stage). Enhanced adds Unique Passive - Swift: +20% ATK Speed.
- **Revitalize** — healing/sustain.
- **Purify** — cleanse / anti-CC.
- **Aegis** — shield.
- **Retribution** — jungle/economy active.

## Physical
- **Demon Hunter Sword** — Tank Killer. +10% ATK Speed; Unique Passive - Engulf: Basic ATKs deal
  extra Physical DMG equal to 2% of the target's max HP.
- **Berserker's Fury** — Increase Crit. +10% Physical ATK, +20% Crit Chance, +40% Crit DMG.
- **Golden Staff**
- **War Axe**
- **Sea Halberd**
- **Blade of Despair**
- **Haas' Claws**

## Magic
- **Glowing Wand** — Skill Burn. +15% Magic ATK; Unique Passive - Scorch: Skill DMG burns the target
  for 3s (Magic DMG = 2.5% of max HP, min 10, per second) and reduces Healing Received by 30% for 3s.
- **Divine Glaive**
- **Ice Queen Wand**
- **Holy Crystal**
