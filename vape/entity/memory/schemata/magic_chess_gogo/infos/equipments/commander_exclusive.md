# Commander Exclusive (MCGG S6) — the Commander Exclusive tab

15 items, each tied to a Commander's Power Card ([[../gogo_cards/power_cards]]) — the equipment
that card grants. Basic→Enhanced raises the stat block; the Cloaks/Scythes gain a 2nd passive at
2-Star. Names, stats, and passive values first-party from the in-game UI (Basic + Enhanced panels).

## Defense (Ruby)
- **Crimson Cloak** — *1-Star Ruby's Cloak.* +20% Max HP *(Enh: +40%)*. Mastery: Basic ATKs mark 1
  enemy with a Rage Mark; when it dies, restore 10% HP.
- **Raging Cloak** — *2-Star Ruby's Cloak.* +20% Max HP *(Enh: +40%)*. Mastery (as above) + Life
  from Death: on death, resurrect with 30% HP (once per round).

## Hybrid ATK
- **Lunar Longbow** (Miya) — *Ally Heal on Kill.* +20% ATK Speed. Moonlight: on kill, heal the
  lowest-HP% ally for 20% HP *(Enh: 30%)*.
- **Chou's Sash** (Chou) — *Hybrid ATK + Kill Bonus.* +60% Hybrid ATK *(Enh: +60% Hybrid ATK, +20%
  ATK Speed)*. Martial Arts: after killing 3 enemies, gain 1 Equipment Chest (max 4).
- **Great Dragon Spear** (Zilong) — *Enhanceable Heavenly Artifact.* no base stat *(Enh: +20%
  Hybrid ATK)*. Dragon Flurry: each Basic ATK has 20% chance to add 2 extra ATKs.
- **Essence Core** — *Hero's Power Inherited.* +10% Hybrid ATK, +10% Max HP *(Enh: +20% / +20%)*.
  Duality Transformation: transforming a Blessed Hero's power counts the Blessing's Synergy without
  the carrier gaining the Blessing.
- **Shadow Twinblades** (Karina, 1-Star) — +35% Hybrid ATK, +25% Max HP *(Enh: +55% / +30%)*.
  Execute: Karina executes adjacent enemies below 18% HP.
- **Supreme Blades** (Karina, 2-Star) — +35% Hybrid ATK, +25% Max HP *(Enh: +55% / +30%)*. Gift:
  Execute (as above); each execution grants +2% DMG, up to +36%.
- **Hunting Scythe** (Ruby, 1-Star) — +20% Hybrid ATK *(Enh: +40%)*. Hunt: every 2s deal 280%
  Adaptive Physical DMG to the Rage-Marked enemy.
- **Raging Scythe** (Ruby, 2-Star) — +20% Hybrid ATK *(Enh: +40%)*. Hunt (as above) + Life from
  Death: after the Cloak carrier resurrects, the Scythe carrier gains 55% Hybrid ATK.

## Consumable (copy-a-Hero)
- **Glory Gloves** (Paquito) — Glory: copy a 1- to 3-Gold Hero and gain Gold.
- **Low-Tier Mirror Device** — Mirror: copy a 1- to 3-Gold Hero.
- **Tier 3 / Tier 4 / Tier 5 Boxing Glove** — Glory: copy a 3- / 4- / 5-Gold Hero respectively.
