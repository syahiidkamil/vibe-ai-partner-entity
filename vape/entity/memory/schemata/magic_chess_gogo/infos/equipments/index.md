# Equipment (MCGG S6)

Items heroes carry, up to **3 per hero** (3 gear slots). Each item has a **Basic** and an
**Enhanced** form; enhancing raises the stats and/or adds a Unique Passive. Items drop from creep
(PvE) rounds and the Go Go Box, not a gold shop. Drops and economy: [[equipment_and_economy]].

## Type groups
Items sort under **Basic / Physical / Magic / Defense / Hybrid ATK** (plus **Consumable** on the
exclusive tabs) — the stat or role an item is built around.

## Two key cross-links
- **Magic Crystals** are equippable items that grant a synergy (one per synergy that has one); this
  is the in-battle source of the +1 synergy-count Magic Crystal ([[../synergies/synergies]]).
- **Commander Exclusive** items are the equipment granted by commander Power Cards
  ([[../gogo_cards/power_cards]]).

Names and effects are first-party from the in-game UI; exact per-id stat rows and the names/stats
provenance live in [source_map](source_map.md). Governed by [[disclaimer]]; world-model: [[schemata]].
