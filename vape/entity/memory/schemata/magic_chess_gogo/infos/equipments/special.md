# Special (MCGG S6) — the Special tab

The Special tab: utility / copy-a-Hero consumables. ~4 items in the tab; **2 captured** (first-party
from the in-game UI).

- **Mirror Device** — *Copy 1- to 5-Gold Hero.* Mirror: copy a Hero of a chosen cost (the full-range
  version of the Commander-Exclusive Low-Tier Mirror Device, which is 1-3 Gold only).
- **Blessing Shifter** — *Shift Blessing.* Blessing: grant a Blessing to a Hero, removing 1 existing
  Blessing; a Blessing adds 1 count to a random Synergy of that Hero.
