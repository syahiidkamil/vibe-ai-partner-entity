# Magic Crystal (MCGG S6) — the Magic Crystal tab

16 crystals. Each grants a synergy when equipped (Unique Passive - Potent: "Equip on a hero to
grant `<Synergy>` Synergy") — the in-battle source of the +1 synergy-count Magic Crystal
([[../synergies/synergies]]). **Magic Crystals do not occupy an equipment slot** (they sit in a
separate table, not the 97-row base). Names first-party from the in-game UI.

## Synergy crystals (15)
Bruiser, Dauntless, Defender, Weapon Master, Marksman, Mage, Stargazer, Swiftblade, Emberlord,
Exorcist, Heartbond, Astro Power, Dragoncaller, Neobeasts, Kishin.
- Captured panel — **Bruiser Magic Crystal:** Unique Passive - Potent: equip on a hero to grant
  Bruiser Synergy. (Every synergy crystal follows this pattern for its own synergy.)

These are the same 15 synergies that carry Blessing/Badge cards; Scavenger, Phasewarper, Enchanted
Tales, Mystic Meow, and Northern Vale have no crystal.

## Go Go Magic Crystal
- Unique Passive - Expansion: increase Capacity by 1 (one more deploy slot).
