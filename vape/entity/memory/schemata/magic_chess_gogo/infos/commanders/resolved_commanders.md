# Commander kits (MCGG S6) — all 37, VERBATIM skill text

Every commander carries 2 signature run-level skills (an Active and/or a Passive) that reshape gold,
the shop, equipment, synergies, rewards, or combat, never normal in-combat hero skills. The second
skill is usually the star-up/upgrade of the first (it raises a magnitude or adds an effect), though a
few are a distinct Active + Passive pair (e.g. Minotaur). Grouped by strategy.

**Provenance and honesty:** every skill description below is transcribed **verbatim** from the
per-commander screenshots in `vape/entity/storage/magic-chess-gogo/commanders/<name>.png` (no
paraphrase, no trimming). Two caveats travel with it: (1) the displayed numbers are at the **star
level shown** in each screenshot (mostly 2 or 3 stars, noted per entry as needed), so a magnitude may
be the leveled display rather than the base; (2) some skin labels were cut off at the frame edge and
are marked `[not legible]`. Synergy rosters and per-tier effects live in [[../synergies/synergies]],
not here. System: [[index]]; skins + provenance: [[source_map]]; governed by [[disclaimer]];
world-model: [[schemata]].
