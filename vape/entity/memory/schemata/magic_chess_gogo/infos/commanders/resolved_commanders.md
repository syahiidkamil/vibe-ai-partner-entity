# Commander kits (MCGG S6) — all 37, with exact values

Every commander carries 2 signature run-level skills (Passive/Active) that reshape gold, the shop,
equipment, synergies, rewards, or combat — never normal combat skills. The second skill is usually
the star-up/upgrade of the first. Grouped by strategy. System: [[index]]; skins + provenance: [[source_map]].

## Economy / Gold
- **Lancelot** — *Blade of Roses.* Golden Legacy (P): no Interest; from stage I/II/III gain 2/3/3
  bonus Gold/round, and each round all Gold is consumed to upgrade Lancelot. Golden Blade (P): from
  stage I/II/III, bonus Gold rises to 4/5/5.
- **Chou** — *Kung Fu Boy.* Warrior's Honor (P): +2 Gold after winning a round. Win or Lose (P):
  also +3 Gold after losing a round.
- **Nana** — *Sweet Leonin.* Molina's Blessing (P): survive first death at 1 HP. Molina's Gift (P):
  +8 Gold per 10 DMG taken (up to 3×); the 3rd trigger grants an Equipment Chest.

## Duplicate / copy / star-up heroes
- **Aamon** — *Duke of Shards.* Blade of Resonance (P): start with 1 Mirror Device; 2-Star heroes
  give 1 Shard, 7 Shards auto-convert to a Mirror Device (max 2). Skybreaker Blade (P): +12 Gold per
  Mirror Device made.
- **Alice** — *Queen of Blood.* Abyssal Gaze (A, Rounds I-1/II-1/III-1): a chosen hero stars up by
  attribute boost instead of 3 copies (up to 8×; 1-Star +50% of 2-Star stats per copy, 2-Star +8%
  of 3-Star). Crimson Grace (P): the chosen hero gets 4 more boosts of +10% Hybrid ATK & +10% HP.
- **Dyrroth** — *Prince of the Abyss.* Abyssal Devourer (A, from I-4): every 3 rounds devour a
  1-Star hero, return one +1 Gold cost (max 5). Abyssal Favor (P): +1 Commander EXP per devour;
  every 2nd devour returns 2 heroes sharing the devoured one's synergies.
- **Harley** — *Mage Genius.* Midas Touch! (A, from II-2): refresh the shop to 3× 5-Gold heroes +
  1 free buy. Gold! Gold! Gold! (P): that refresh now fills 5× 5-Gold heroes.
- **Lylia** — *Lovable Witch.* Go, Tharz! (A, at Capacity 8): shop disabled; immediately gain a
  copy of your highest-cost deployed hero, +1 more on each win. Party Time! (P): on a loss, grant
  +15% Hybrid ATK to the highest-cost deployed hero.
- **Moskov** — *Spear of Quiescence.* Power of Shadow (P): place 1/2/3/4-Gold heroes in a Shadow
  Field (+5 Gold each); then a placed hero gains +15% Hybrid ATK & +15% HP. Dark Star (P): also
  spawn two 1-Star copies of the enhanced hero.
- **Paquito** — *The Heavenly Fist.* Fist of Glory (P): in II-1 / III-1 gain Boxing Gloves for 3- /
  4-Gold heroes; equip to get that hero's 1-Star copy ×2. Champ Stance (P): in IV-1, the 5-Gold
  Boxing Glove (copy ×2).
- **Popol and Kupa** — *Icefield Companions.* Copy Trap (P): a movable trap copies an enemy hero
  that steps in (1-Star, once/round). Hunter's Mark (P): +1 Gold per copy, or +1 Gold if none.
- **Vale** — *Windtalker.* Updraft (A): refresh the shop to heroes sharing a synergy with a chosen
  hero + 1 free buy; +1 charge at Commander Levels 4/6/8/10. Shifting Wind (P): +1 more charge at
  Levels 5/7/9.

## Equipment
- **Karina** — *Shadow Blade.* Shadow Twinblades (P): start with the blades — execute adjacent
  enemies below 18% HP. Twinblades Evolution (P): each execute grants +2% DMG (up to +36%).
- **Luo Yi** — *Mistveil Mage.* Duality Transformation (P): transform a hero into an Essence Core
  equipment inheriting 40% of its Hybrid ATK/HP and 40% of its equipment's base stats; reset to
  revert. Harmony of Essence (P): the Core gains extra effects by the hero's Role Synergy.
- **Minotaur** — *Son of Minos.* Molten Decree (A, 3×): enhance a chosen Equipment (not Special/
  already-Enhanced). Bloodline Resonance (P): start with 1 Equipment Chest; +2 Gold per enhance.
- **Ruby** — *Little Red Hood.* Red Hood's Rage (P): start with Cloak (Rage Mark heal 10% HP on
  marked kill) + Scythe (280% Adaptive Physical DMG to the marked enemy every 2s). Life from Death
  (P): Cloak resurrects carrier at 30% HP once/round; after that, Scythe carrier gains +55% Hybrid ATK.
- **Zilong** — *Spear of the Dragon.* Great Dragon Spear (P): start with the Spear (20% chance per
  Basic ATK to add 2 extra ATKs); buy 1 trait to enhance it (Control Immunity & Lifesteal / Gold
  Bandit 5% per ATK / Low-HP Shield 50% Max HP). Dragon's Roar (P): buy 2 more traits.

## Synergy grants
- **Gusion** — *Holy Blade.* Light Sigil (P): a Sigil shows a random Role Synergy; drag a hero onto
  it to grant the synergy (+7 Gold; 3 refreshes). Sigil Resonance (P): after Stage II, +1 use.
- **Guinevere** — *Ms. Violet.* Super Magic (P): start with 1 Equipment; 14% chance a free hero
  appears after auto-refresh. Mystic Evolution (P): free-hero chance rises to 24%.
- **Lunox** — *Twilight Goddess.* Power of Chaos (A, after I-2): grant a hero a random Blessing
  (+1 Role/Faction synergy count; not Shifter-transferable). Power of Order (P): after casting, gain
  3 heroes from that synergy + 6 Gold.

## Reward / value engines
- **Alucard** — *Demon Hunter.* Demonic Altar (A, Rounds I-3 & II-3): open an Altar of 6 rewards —
  discard 1, take 1 random. Plunder Expansion (P): +1 activation in III-3.
- **Aurora** — *Maiden of the Glacier.* Frost Treasures (P): shop freezes each round; buy nothing →
  +2 Frost Energy; at 6, a reward worth ≥7 Gold. Icy Blessing (P): Frost Energy gain rises to 3.
- **Cyclops** — *Asta.* Astral Blessing (P): when another Commander is eliminated, gain Gold = 14%
  of their lineup value + 1 random hero from their lineup at its star. Astral Resonance (P): when
  your HP first hits ≤80, gain 3 heroes of your highest-count synergy + 1 Equipment Chest.
- **Diggie** — *Timekeeper.* Alarm Chest (P): a chest gains 15 Time Points/round; at 100 it opens
  for rewards then upgrades rarity (max 3 opens). Time Acceleration (P): win +10 points; loss
  permanently +300 chest Max HP.
- **Kalea** — *Surging Wave.* Serpent's Favor (P): from I-4 roll a Lucky Dice (1-4 pts; >8 resets);
  activate the Treasure Trove for rewards by points (max 2/match). Wave of Fortune (P): max
  activations → 3.
- **Vexana** — *Shimmer of Hope.* I Want All of Them! (P): for the first 3 Go Go Box rounds, take 1
  extra reward after others pick (+13 Gold in the Go Go Auction round). Unrestricted (P): in the
  first Go Go Box round, copy your first pick and gain 13 Gold.

## Go Go cards / Commander EXP
- **Valir** — *Son of Flames.* Blazing Inferno (P): choose Go Go Cards twice in the II-3 stage.
  Incinerate (P): extra Commander EXP by card quality in II-3 (16 purple / 26 blue). *(Magic
  Crystal "Rune" mode: +15 Commander EXP at the start of II-3.)*
- **Yu Zhong** — *Black Dragon.* Soul Grip (P): Go Go Cards at the start of II-3 upgrade 1 quality
  tier (max → random Black Dragon Treasure). Cursing Touch (P): also upgrades III-3 cards; +1 Black
  Dragon Treasure during the I-2 Go Go Card pick.

## Combat & board control
- **Angela** — *Bunnylove.* Love's Protection (A): a chosen ally gets 180% Mana Regen + a shield =
  25% Max HP. Ripples of Love (P): extra protection to the highest-Power hero (prefers Mana users).
- **Fanny** — *Blade Dancer.* Blade Dancer (P): build a Hero Launcher (10 rounds; +4 Gold on
  finish); drag a hero on to summon a Shadow that joins battle. Heart of the Blade (P): build takes
  only 2 rounds, launched hero +20% Hybrid ATK & HP.
- **Johnson** — *Wild Engine.* Johnny, Go! (A): open a shop of free 3-Gold Johnnies (2 random
  synergies each; "Spanner Spin" stuns 0.5/0.7/1s). Transform! (P): at battle start, Johnny charges
  the densest area for Magic DMG = 10% of his Max HP, CC-immune while transformed.
- **Kagura** — *Onmyouji Master.* Shield Umbrella (A): a chosen hero, when first below 50% HP, gains
  a shield = 60% Max HP. Yin Yang Gathering (P): shield rises to 135% Max HP.
- **Layla** — *Blue Specter.* Hologram: Destruction Rush (P): a hologram lasers enemies for 340
  Physical DMG, +3% ATK Speed each round. Hologram: Synergy Freedom (P): per 8000 DMG dealt, gain a
  hero from your highest synergy next round.
- **Ling** — *Cyan Finch.* Showdown (P): a Dueling Ring (25s) drags the farthest enemy in for a
  Basic-ATK-only duel; the ally inside gets +100% ATK Speed. Transcendent Stance (P): after winning
  a duel, +6% Hybrid ATK permanently (up to 11 stacks).
- **Lukas** — *Beast of Light.* Unleash the Beast (P): a statue's Shockwave Blast deals 20% target
  Max HP as Physical DMG + 1s stun. Frenzy (P): rises to 45% + 1.5s stun; front-row placement adds
  Wild Power (45% Max HP DMG, -65 Hybrid DEF).
- **Miya** — *Greenwood Archer.* Lunar Longbow (A): after every 6 Basic ATKs of a chosen hero, Miya
  fires 5 arrows for 7% target Max HP each. Moonshade Arrow (P): +3 split arrows (4% Max HP each);
  the chosen hero +50% ATK Speed.
- **Wanwan** — *Agile Tiger.* Tiger Pounce (P): when an enemy first drops below 30% HP, strike the
  lowest-HP enemy 20× for 180/310/430/550 Physical DMG by stage. Reinforcement (P): +1 Gold per
  enemy hero killed (up to 3).
