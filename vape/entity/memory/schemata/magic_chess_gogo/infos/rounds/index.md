# Rounds: the match calendar (special events by stage and round position)

What happens *when* in a match, beyond the every-round loop. The stage->round map, the per-round
mechanics (Preparation/Battle phase, Battle Acceleration, Commander DMG), and the generic
Creep / Go Go Box / Power Card round *types* live in [[encyclopedia/round_and_battle]] and
[[game_rules_and_round_flow]]. This file is the **schedule**: which special event lands at which
exact round position. Confirmed end-to-end against a full custom-vs-computer match,
[the replay](../../experience/custom_with_computer/replay.md) (2026-06-28). Governed by [[disclaimer]].

## The stage frame (recap)

Four stages, each several rounds, each ending in a **Go Go Box** reward stage (detail + the
per-round Commander-DMG base: [[encyclopedia/round_and_battle]]).

| Stage | Rounds |
|------|--------|
| I    | 1-4    |
| II   | 5-10   |
| III  | 11-16  |
| IV   | 17-99 (final, open-ended) |

## The calendar (special events by position)

### Stage I (1-4)
- **I-1, Creep round.** Kill the creeps, then choose **1 of 3** regular **basic** equipment; the
  other two vanish. (Replay: the three on offer were Aegis, Inspire, Retribution.)
- **I-2, Go Go Card selection** (Commander Power Card). Pick one run-shaping Power Card; each option
  is refreshable. (Replay: Kagura's Power vs Lukas's Power.) Cards: [[gogo_cards/index]].
- **End of Stage I, Reward Selection** (outside the round, a Go Go Box). Sequential pick: the
  **lowest-HP** commander chooses first (catch-up). **8 equipment + 3 heroes** on offer, one taken.

### Stage II (5-10)
- **II-1 (start), Blessing Shifter.** Receive a Blessing Shifter: **generate** a hero's Blessing, or
  **shift** a Blessing from one hero to another. (The Blessing system itself, the free +1 synergy
  count from combining a 2-star, also opens here: [[encyclopedia/hero]].) Item: [[equipments/special]].
- **II-3, Go Go Card selection + Creep round.** Pick a Go Go Card, and clear a creep round for a
  regular equipment reward (**1 of 3** random). (Replay: cards Bright Sunshine vs Reinforcement; the
  round-preview track marks a creep with a **purple diamond** when its card reward is purple-rarity.)
- **End of Stage II, Auction** (outside the round, a Go Go Box variant). Commanders **bid Gold** on
  the lots. (Replay: 8 lots incl. Enhanced Sea Halberd, Enhanced Divine Glaive, Random Magic Crystal,
  Go Go Magic Crystal, Mirror Device, a 4-Gold-Hero x2, Enhanced Berserker's Fury, Enhanced Oracle.)

### Stage III (11-16)
- **III-3, Go Go Card selection + Creep round + Blessing Shifter.** All three at once: a Go Go Card
  pick, a creep equipment reward (**1 of 3** random), and a *second* **Blessing Shifter**. (Replay:
  cards seen incl. Healing Fountain, Solid Alliance I, Purest Power I, Breath of Berserk I.)
- **End of Stage III, Reward Selection** (outside the round, a Go Go Box). Same sequential
  lowest-HP-first pick as Stage I. (Confirmed by the replay; the original schedule note omitted it.)

### Stage IV (17-99, final)
- **IV-3, Creep round.** A regular equipment reward (**1 of 3** random). No Go Go Card or Blessing
  Shifter this stage.
- **End of Stage IV, Reward Selection(s).** The final Go Go Box. (In the replay the match was already
  decided by elimination before this resolved; last commander standing wins.)

## The per-round loop (every round, all stages)

Shop auto-refreshes, +2 Commander EXP, gold at round end (salary + interest + streak + win). Detail:
[[game_rules_and_round_flow]], [[encyclopedia/gold_economy]].
