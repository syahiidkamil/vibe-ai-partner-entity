## Notes / soft spots
- The original schedule note omitted an **end-of-Stage-III Reward Selection**; the replay shows one
  right after the III-6 victory (image 26), so it is included. Read every stage end as a **Go Go
  Box** reward stage: Stage I and III are a sequential reward selection, Stage II is an auction,
  Stage IV is the final reward selection.
- The replay is a **Custom** match ("no rewards in Custom Mode"), so the *schedule* and *event types*
  are authoritative, but the opponents' skill is not representative (the AI is weak; the player held
  100 HP all game). Round positions transfer to ranked; the *contents* of each reward are randomized
  per match.
- Reward and auction *contents* listed in the index are this match's roll (examples), not a fixed
  table. The Go Go Card pools are in [[gogo_cards/index]]; equipment in [[equipments/index]].
