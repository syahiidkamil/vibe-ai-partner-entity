# Generic Go Go Cards (Blue / Purple / Orange) — MCGG (concrete rung)

The non-commander Go Go Cards: the generic run-shaping effects under the **Blue / Purple / Orange**
tabs (the commander cards are in [[power_cards]]). See [[index]]; source and method:
[source_map](source_map.md).

## The rarity system (resolved from the UI)
- Three tiers, ascending: **Blue < Purple < Orange**. Higher rarity is a stronger roll of the same
  effect, shown two ways:
  - **Tiered families** carry a `I / II / III` suffix that tracks rarity: e.g. Barrier's Seal
    I/II/III = +650 / +950 / +1900 front-row HP; Breath of Berserk I/II/III = +8% / +16% / +28%
    Lifesteal; Thickened Blood, Magic Immersion, Customize Recruitment, Solid Alliance, Purest
    Power, Dimensional Shot, Attack & Defend, Premium Client, Glittering Jewels all scale the same way.
  - A `+` suffix marks an upgraded variant (e.g. Reinforcement+ , Golden Era+).
- **Synergy reward cards** come at two rarities: **Blessing** (orange) gives a Magic Crystal + an
  item + a hero; **Badge** (purple) gives the Magic Crystal + a hero (no item). One per synergy.
  These are how a [[../synergies/synergies]] Magic Crystal (the +1 synergy-count source) is drawn.
- **Quality upgrade / Black Dragon Treasure.** Effects bump available/subsequent cards by 1 quality
  tier; at max quality the bump pays a random Black Dragon Treasure (see Yu Zhong in [[power_cards]]).

## Reading note
The families cluster into a few levers: economy (Gold, refreshes, interest), recruitment (specific
Hero pulls, shop odds), combat buffs (ATK Speed, Hybrid ATK, Lifesteal, Shield/HP, DMG windows),
equipment bundles, and synergy payoffs (Blessing/Badge, Demon Commander, Astro Judgment, etc.).
Higher rarity = bigger numbers or an added clause. Full power cards: [[power_cards]].
