# Power Cards — MCGG Go Go Cards (Commander Power Cards)

The commander-themed Go Go Cards (the "Power Cards" tab, pink frame). Each is tied to a Commander
and its effect keys off that Commander's kit. `(Exclusive)` marks a card only its own Commander may
pick; the rest can be borrowed by any Commander. See [[index]]; source and method:
[source_map](source_map.md).

**46 power cards**, listed by Commander. Some Commanders have two distinct cards (an exclusive
economy/buff card and a borrowable equipment/utility card).

- **Aamon's Power** — Gain 4 Gold each time a Hero reaches 2-Star (up to 20 Gold in total).
- **Alice's Power** — Gain 5 EXP. Heroes selected by Alice's skill gain 45% extra Hybrid ATK.
  **(Exclusive)**
- **Alice's Power** — Obtain a 1-Star version of a selected Hero. Additional copies auto-combine,
  granting partial Star-Up bonuses. Select 3 time(s).
- **Alucard's Power** — Obtain Demonic Altar (6 Rewards). In Rounds I-2, II-2, III-2, discard 1
  reward and receive 1 from the remaining.
- **Angela's Power** — Gain a movable doll. At battle start it increases Mana Regen by 70% for
  allies within 2 tiles to its left and right on the same row.
- **Aurora's Power** — Up to 6 times/match: if you buy no Heroes in a round, get a random reward
  worth at least 4 Gold next round.
- **Chou's Power** — Obtain Chou's Sash. After killing 3 enemies, obtain 1 Equipment Chest (max 4).
- **Cyclops' Power** — Gain 8 Gold. When any Commander is eliminated, gain 15% of their deployed
  Heroes' total cost as Gold.
- **Diggie's Power** — Summon an Alarm Chest. Gain 15 Time Points/round, +10 on victory. At Point
  thresholds it grants rewards then upgrades rarity.
- **Diggie's Power** — Gain 15 extra Time Points each round. The Alarm Chest opens 1 extra time for
  rewards worth 25 Gold. **(Exclusive)**
- **Dyrroth's Power** — Every 3 rounds, devour a 1-Star Hero from your slot for a random Hero of +1
  Gold cost (up to 5-Gold). Each devour grants 2 EXP.
- **Fanny's Power** — After Commander reaches Lv.5, Hero Capacity +1.
- **Guinevere's Power** — For the next 13 rounds, 1 free Hero appears in Shop after auto-refresh at
  each round start.
- **Gusion's Power** — Gain 1 Role Magic Crystal Chest and 3 Gold.
- **Harley's Power** — Gain 6 Gold. The Shop auto-refreshes once to show at least 4-Gold Hero ×2.
- **Johnson's Power** — Increases Johnnies in the Hero Pool by 5. Johnny gains 25% Hybrid ATK and
  20% Max HP. **(Exclusive)**
- **Johnson's Power** — Open Johnson's Skill Shop to pick a Johnny (3-Gold) with 2 random Synergies;
  all future Johnnies share those Synergies.
- **Kagura's Power** — Select a Hero. At battle start they gain 20% Hybrid Lifesteal and 30% Hybrid
  ATK. Gain 9 Gold.
- **Kalea's Power** — Roll the Lucky Dice now and again at Stage III start for rewards worth 21-35
  Gold total based on the roll.
- **Karina's Power** — Gain 6 Gold. Twinblades gain 40% ATK Speed. **(Exclusive)**
- **Karina's Power** — Obtain Twinblades: executes nearby enemies below 18% HP; each execution
  grants 2% extra DMG (up to 36%).
- **Lancelot's Power** — Gain 2 Gold per round upon reaching Commander Lv.5.
- **Layla's Power** — For every 50000 DMG dealt by all allies, obtain 1 Hero from your most
  activated Synergy.
- **Ling's Power** — At Commander Lv.4, 5, and 7, obtain the same random 4-Gold Hero.
- **Lukas's Power** — Gain 12 Gold. Sacred Beast gains 50% extra DMG. **(Exclusive)**
- **Lukas's Power** — Gain 1 statue. At battle start it deals DMG and applies debuffs, then leaves.
- **Lunox's Power** — Gain 4 Gold. When you first activate a Synergy to 6, gain 24 more Gold.
  **(Exclusive)**
- **Lunox's Power** — Gain 6 Gold. Select one of your Heroes without a Blessing to grant a random
  Blessing.
- **Luo Yi's Power** — Obtain random 3-Gold Hero ×1 now, then the same one at each round start, up
  to 6 total.
- **Lylia's Power** — At Commander Lv.7, receive a 5-Gold Hero with a matching Equipment.
- **Minotaur's Power** — Select an Equipment to enhance. Can be used 2 time(s). Obtain random
  Equipment ×2.
- **Miya's Power** — Gain 12 Gold. Obtain Lunar Longbow (+20% ATK Speed; on kill restores 20% Max HP
  to an ally).
- **Moskov's Power** — Obtain a random 1-Gold Hero now; in following rounds obtain a random 2-, 3-,
  4-, and 5-Gold Hero in sequence.
- **Nana's Power** — Gain 1 Equipment Chest and 8 Gold. Commander becomes immune to fatal DMG once.
- **Paquito's Power** — Obtain Glory Gloves. Equip to a 3-Gold-or-lower Hero for their 1-Star copy
  ×2 and 4 Gold.
- **Popol and Kupa's Power** — From Round II-1, generate a Copy Trap. Once/round, if an enemy steps
  in, obtain their 1-Star version and 1 Gold; otherwise gain 1 Gold.
- **Popol and Kupa's Power** — Gain 2 Gold when capturing a Hero. Gain 2 Equipment Chest(s) after
  capturing 10 Hero(es) for the first time. **(Exclusive)**
- **Ruby's Power** — Obtain Raging Scythe (deals DMG at intervals) and Raging Cloak (restore HP &
  resurrect).
- **Ruby's Power** — Gain 2 Gold for each survived Cloak or Scythe carrier when battle ends. Max
  Gold: 35. **(Exclusive)**
- **Vale's Power** — Gain 1 free Hero purchase and 1 Gold each time Commander Level increases.
- **Valir's Power** — Gain 4 Gold. Obtain a random Commander Power Card.
- **Vexana's Power** — Select one extra reward during the first 4 Go Go Box rounds. Gain 13 Gold in
  Go Go Auction.
- **Wanwan's Power** — 50% chance to gain 1 Gold each time you kill an enemy (Max Gold: 35).
- **Yu Zhong's Power** — The subsequent Go Go Cards are upgraded by 1 quality tier; if already at
  max quality, receive a random Black Dragon Treasure instead.
- **Zilong's Power** — Great Dragon Spear + 30% Hybrid ATK. The Shop auto-refreshes to fill all
  slots with its traits. Select 1 trait free. **(Exclusive)**
- **Zilong's Power** — Obtain Great Dragon Spear (20% chance for extra ATKs on each Basic ATK); get
  traits from the Shop to enhance it.

## Reading note

Power Cards span every lever: equipment (Minotaur, Zilong, Miya, Paquito), carry buffs (Kagura,
Alice, Johnson), flat/scaling economy (Lancelot, Lunox, Harley, Aamon, Cyclops, Wanwan), board
manipulation (Popol and Kupa, Dyrroth, Moskov, Luo Yi), tempo/shop control (Guinevere, Aurora,
Diggie), and card-quality upgrades (Yu Zhong). Generic rarity cards: [[generic_cards]].
