# Encyclopedia: Game Rules

Transcribed from the in-app Encyclopedia screenshot (game_rules.png), 2026-06-28, live client
v1.2.88.302.2.

Verbatim, in-app Encyclopedia > **Game Rules** tab. Source: localization id `3003306606`.

> 1. All players purchase to recruit Heroes from the **Shared Hero Pool** to build their battle
>    lineups.
> 2. The number of each Hero in the Hero Pool is fixed. The higher their quality, the rarer they
>    are. When other players purchase to recruit a Hero, it becomes harder for you to obtain that
>    same Hero.
> 3. The game proceeds in rounds where players take turns battling **Creeps** or **other
>    players**.
> 4. The last player standing becomes the **Champion** of the match.

What it pins down: the shared, contested pool (the scarcity engine), fixed per-hero copy counts by
quality tier, the turn-based round structure (PvE creeps + PvP), and the last-one-standing win
condition. The pool mechanics live in [[equipment_and_economy]]; the full loop in
[[game_rules_and_round_flow]].
