# Power Cards — MCGG Go Go Cards (captured from UI)

Commander/hero-themed power cards (the pink-framed cards in the "Power Cards" tab). Captured exact
from the in-game UI screenshot (2026-06-27); names and effect text verbatim.

- **Lancelot's Power**: Gain 2 Gold per round upon reaching Commander Lv.5.
- **Alice's Power**: Gain 5 EXP. Heroes selected by Alice's skill gain 45% extra Hybrid ATK.
  (Exclusive to Commander Alice.)
- **Harley's Power**: Gain 6 Gold. The Shop will auto refresh once to show at least 4-Gold Hero x2.
- **Popol and Kupa's Power**: Starting from Round II-1, generate a Copy Trap. Once per round, if an
  enemy steps into it, obtain their 1-Star version and 1 Gold. Otherwise, gain 1 Gold.

## Reading note
Each Power Card is tied to a specific hero/commander, and some are Commander-exclusive (Alice's,
only usable when your Commander is Alice). The effects span the run's levers: flat economy
(Lancelot, Harley), EXP and a carry buff (Alice), and a board-stealing trap (Popol and Kupa).
