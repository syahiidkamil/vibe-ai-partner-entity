# Game Rules & Round Flow — MCGG (concrete rung)

The rules of play, from the game's own 23-screen tutorial. Marked **[seen]** where the tutorial
showed it directly and **[inferred]** where I am filling from standard auto-chess (verify by
play; held in pencil per [[disclaimer]]).

## The frame
- **8 Commanders**, free-for-all. Each Commander is a player (me). **[seen]**
- **Win condition:** last Commander standing. You claim victory when every other Commander's HP
  is reduced to zero. **[seen]**
- **Commander HP:** a life total that drops when you lose a combat round; at 0 you are out.
  Seen draining through the 90s and into the 20s across a game; exact starting value not shown
  (genre norm is ~100). **[seen, start inferred]**

## The round, step by step
1. **Gold** arrives at the round start. Base round income **[seen: gold counter]**; interest on
   saved gold and win/loss streak bonuses are genre-standard **[inferred]**.
2. **Shop:** opened with a tap; offers a few heroes priced by cost tier (1-5). Buy a hero to add
   it to your bench/board; **refresh** for a new offer; **level up** to raise Commander Level.
   **[seen: "Open the Shop", "Purchase Balmond"]**
3. **Place & position** heroes within **Hero Capacity** (board size). Every hero is **front-row**
   or **back-row**, and the row matters (who the enemy reaches first). **[seen]**
4. **Star-Up:** collect 3 identical heroes and they auto-merge into one of the next star
   (1->2->3); nine 1-stars make a 3-star. "Heroes get stronger every Star Level, up to 3-Star."
   **[seen]**
5. **Sell** unwanted heroes back for gold (1g shown). **[seen]**
6. **Combat** auto-resolves: the board fights on its own, no input mid-fight. **[seen]**
7. **Resolve:** lose the fight, lose Commander HP. **[seen]**

## Commander Level & capacity
- A **Level** (Lv, with an XP bar, e.g. 4/5) raised by spending gold/XP. **[seen: "Lv.4"]**
- Level sets **Hero Capacity** = how many heroes you may field (e.g. 5/5, 6/6; "Current Capacity
  is full"). The exact level->capacity table not shown. **[seen, table inferred]**

## Round types (the rhythm of a game)
- **PvP combat** — your board vs another Commander's board. The main loop; drains the loser's
  Commander HP. **[seen]**
- **Creep round (PvE)** — fight monsters ("This round is a Creep Round"); on win, **pick 1
  equipment** from a small choice (e.g. Spellblade Talisman, Ice Queen Wand). **[seen]**
- **Go Go Box stage** — a shared reward stage: equipment and heroes sit on pedestals, and **all
  Commanders pick in turn from lowest to highest Commander HP** (a catch-up mechanic for whoever
  is losing). "Go Go Box Stage provides random Equipment and Heroes." **[seen]**
- **Power Card selection** — at set points you choose a **Go Go Card** (Commander Power Card).
  See [[gogo_cards]]. **[seen]**
