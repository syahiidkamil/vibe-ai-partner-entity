# Go Go Cards (Commander Power Cards) — MCGG (concrete rung)

The game's signature twist, and the "Go Go" in the name. From the tutorial **[seen]**; the full
card list lives in the client data (not yet extracted into the dataset) and is owner-to-pull.

## What it is
A **Go Go Card** is a **Commander Power Card**: a chosen, run-shaping power you pick at set
points ("Now it's time to select a Go Go Card", "Select a Commander Power Card"). It is the
augment of MCGG, the lever that bends a whole run rather than a single round. You choose between
a small hand of them.

## Examples seen in the tutorial
- **New Recruit** — "Gain 3 random 3-star Heroes." (An instant, enormous tempo spike: 3-stars
  normally need nine copies each.)
- **Dragon (name partly obscured)** — "Allied Heroes gain 8% Hybrid Lifesteal." (A whole-team
  sustain buff.)

These two alone show the design range: one is a burst of board power, the other a steady
multiplier. Cards interact with comps (a lifesteal card rewards a sustain board; a free-3-stars
card rewards committing to those heroes' synergies).
