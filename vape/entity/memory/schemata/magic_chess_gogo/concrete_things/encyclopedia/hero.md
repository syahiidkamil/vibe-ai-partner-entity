# Encyclopedia: Hero (Star & Quality, Faction & Role, Summoned, Shop Refresh)

Verbatim, in-app Encyclopedia > **Hero** tab and its sub-panels.

Transcribed from the in-app Encyclopedia screenshots (hero_01/02.png), 2026-06-28, live client
v1.2.88.302.2.

> Transcriber note: in-app, the two headings "Star & Quality" and "Faction & Role" are shown
> with their bodies SWAPPED. The text under "Star & Quality" describes Faction and Role; the
> text under "Faction & Role" describes Star levels and quality tiers. Transcribed exactly as
> displayed; the apparent swap looks like a localization bug, not a transcription error.

## Star & Quality
> 1. Each Hero belongs to a specific **Faction** and **Role**.
> 2. When different Heroes of the same Faction or Role are deployed on the board, corresponding
>    **Synergy** effects will be activated.

## Faction & Role
> 1. Each Hero has 3 Star levels, starting at 1-Star.
> 2. Every **3** of the same Hero will automatically combine to reach the next Star level. Higher
>    stars mean more power.
> 3. There are five tiers of Hero quality, each costing a different amount of Gold. The more a
>    Hero costs, the rarer and stronger they are.

Five quality tiers (1-5 cost), per-star stats, and the 20 synergies: [[heroes/index]],
[[synergies]].

## Summoned Heroes
> Certain Synergies summon Heroes in the Battle Area. Summoned Heroes do not take up Capacity and
> have the following traits:
> 1. Cannot be purchased, sold in the Shop, or placed in Hero Slots.
> 2. Have their Star level and skills adjusted based on Synergy count.
> 3. Will disappear immediately if the Synergy that summoned them becomes inactive.

(Summoned units are free board presence, so synergies that summon are positioning value the
Capacity cap does not charge for.)

## Shop Refresh Probability
> 1. The probability of finding Heroes of different quality tiers in the Shop varies depending on
>    the Commander Level. Well-timed Shop refreshes can effectively increase your chances of
>    getting your desired Hero.
> 2. Below **Epic** Rank, Ranked, Classic, and Custom modes switch to the Novice Hero Library,
>    with Hero refresh probability doubled.

Count (Per Hero) is how many copies of each Hero of that tier sit in the shared pool: 25, 21,
18, 14, 9 for 1-Gold to 5-Gold. A "/" means that tier cannot appear at that Commander Level.

| Commander Lv. | 1-Gold | 2-Gold | 3-Gold | 4-Gold | 5-Gold |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Count (Per Hero) | 25 | 21 | 18 | 14 | 9 |
| Lv.3 | 60% | 30% | 10% | / | / |
| Lv.4 | 50% | 35% | 15% | / | / |
| Lv.5 | 34% | 35% | 30% | 1% | / |
| Lv.6 | 25% | 30% | 40% | 5% | / |
| Lv.7 | 19% | 28% | 42% | 10% | 1% |
| Lv.8 | 16% | 26% | 35% | 18% | 5% |
| Lv.9 | 13% | 22% | 25% | 26% | 14% |
| Lv.10 | 6% | 12% | 22% | 35% | 25% |

Each row sums to 100%. Lv.1 and Lv.2 are not shown; Lv.9 and Lv.10 come from round_03.png (the
same table scrolled down). The table runs Lv.3 to Lv.10 (the cap).

The curve is the economy clock: cheap tiers dominate early (1-Gold is 60% at Lv.3), and high
tiers only open as Commander Level climbs (5-Gold first appears at Lv.7, at 1%). Leveling to
reach a tier, then refreshing, is how you fish for a specific Hero.

## Blessing System

The Blessing System is on the Season Information tab, not the Hero tab, so it lives there:
[[season_information]] (screenshot-verified). It increases one of a Hero's Synergy counts by 1.
