# Encyclopedia: Hero (Star & Quality, Faction & Role, Summoned, Blessing)

Verbatim, in-app Encyclopedia > **Hero** tab and its sub-panels. Sources noted per panel.

## Star & Quality (id `3003307283`)
> 1. Each Hero has 3 Star levels, starting at 1-Star.
> 2. Every **3** of the same Hero will automatically combine to reach the next Star level. Higher
>    stars mean more power.
> 3. There are five tiers of Hero quality, each costing a different amount of Gold. The more a Hero
>    costs, the rarer and stronger they are.

## Faction & Role (id `3003307284`)
> 1. Each Hero belongs to a specific **Faction** and **Role**.
> 2. When different Heroes of the same Faction or Role are deployed on the board, corresponding
>    **Synergy** effects will be activated.

Five quality tiers (1-5 cost), per-star stats, and the 20 synergies: [[heroes/index]],
[[synergies]].

## Summoned Heroes (id `2912711922`)
> Certain Synergies summon Heroes in the Battle Area. Summoned Heroes do not take up Capacity and
> have the following traits:
> 1. Cannot be purchased, sold in the Shop, or placed in Hero Slots.
> 2. Have their Star level and skills adjusted based on Synergy count.
> 3. Will disappear immediately if the Synergy that summoned them becomes inactive.

(Summoned units are free board presence, so synergies that summon are positioning value the
Capacity cap does not charge for.)

## Blessing System (id `2912711175`)
> **Blessing Effect:** Increases one of the Hero's Synergy counts by **1**.
> How to Trigger:
> 1. Starting from Round **II-1**, if no Heroes have a Blessing Effect (excluding those granted by
>    Commander Skills or Synergies), combining a **2-Star** Hero grants a Blessing Effect to one of
>    their random Synergies.
> 2. Certain Commander Skills or Synergies can grant an additional Blessing Effect to a Hero.

The Blessing is the cheap path to an extra synergy count: a 2-star you build anyway can tip a
synergy over its next threshold for free. Synergy thresholds: [[synergies]].
