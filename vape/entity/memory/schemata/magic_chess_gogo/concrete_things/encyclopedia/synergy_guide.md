# Encyclopedia: Synergy Guide (Neobeasts Point Rewards)

The in-app **Synergy Guide** tab. The three screenshots all show one section, the **Neobeasts
Point Rewards** ladder, captured at three overlapping scroll positions, so the bands repeat
across the images and together resolve every line (nothing was cut off unreadably). This file is
the in-game GUIDE text only; the synergy rosters, the 20 synergy names, and their activation
tiers live in [[synergies]] and are not duplicated here.

Transcribed from the in-app Encyclopedia screenshots (synergy_guide_01/02/03.png), 2026-06-28,
live client v1.2.88.302.2.

## Where it sits (UI)

- Header bar: "Dawnlight Celebration | Arena".
- Left rail: Hero / Equipment / **Encyclopedia** (selected) / Go Go Cards.
- Top tabs: Game Rules / Round / Hero / Commander / Gold / Advanced Tutorial / **Synergy Guide**
  (selected) / Season Information.

## Neobeasts Point Rewards (verbatim)

The section heading reads "Neobeasts Point Rewards", followed by a one-line summary:

> Neobeasts Synergy grants 12 / 28 Gold at 300 / 700 points, respectively.

Below it is a point-threshold ladder. Each band (point range) lists the rewards granted on
reaching it:

| Points  | Rewards |
|---------|---------|
| 1-39    | 1-Gold Hero ×1 |
| 40-99   | 2-Gold Hero ×1; 2 EXP |
| 100-199 | 3-Gold Hero ×1; 6 EXP |
| 200-299 | 4-Gold Hero ×1; 12 EXP; Equipment ×1 |
| 300-499 | 5-Gold Hero ×1; 18 EXP; Magic Crystal Chest ×1 |
| 500-699 | 5-Gold Hero ×1; 30 EXP; Equipment ×3; Magic Crystal Chest ×1 |
| 700-899 | 5-Gold 2-Star Hero ×1; Mirror Device ×2; Magic Crystal Chest ×2 |
| 900-998 | 4-Gold 3-Star Hero ×1; Mirror Device ×3; Magic Crystal Chest ×3 |

The final **999** band lists three reward entries (shown as three lines, verbatim):

> Mirror Device ×8, 20 Gold
> 5-Gold 3-Star Hero ×1, 20 Gold
> 4-Gold 3-Star Hero ×3, 20 Gold, and Random Identical Magic Crystal ×3.

## Reading notes

- As captured, the Synergy Guide tab shows only the Neobeasts point-reward economy. It does not, in
  these screenshots, restate the general mechanics of how synergies activate or stack. For the
  rosters, the activation breakpoints, and the per-synergy effects, see [[synergies]].
- Neobeasts (r55 in the datamine) is the faction synergy whose members accrue points on enemy
  deaths; the ladder above is what those accrued points buy. Its membership and tiers
  (2 / 4 / 6 / 10): [[synergies]].
- The "×" multiplier glyph is reproduced as shown in the client (e.g. "×1", "×2", "×8").
- The 999 entry lists three reward lines without an explicit "or" / "all" connector in the UI, so
  whether they are alternatives or a combined grant is not stated here; transcribed as the three
  lines appear, no relationship inferred.
