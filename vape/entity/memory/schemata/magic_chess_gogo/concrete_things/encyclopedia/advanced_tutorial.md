# Encyclopedia: Advanced Tutorial

Verbatim, in-app Encyclopedia > **Advanced Tutorial** tab (the game's own strategy panels).
Sources: localization ids `3003308431` / `3003308432` / `3003308433`.

## Economy (id `3003308431`)
> 1. Save Gold properly in the early and mid-game and obtain **Interest** for each round.
> 2. When your HP is almost out, convert all the Gold into battle power. Otherwise, you will be
>    eliminated with a large amount of Gold unspent.

## Synergy & Positioning (id `3003308432`)
> 1. Activating **Synergy** as soon as possible is the basic tactic on the battlefield.
> 2. Only a well-rounded lineup can gain the high ground. Generally, deploy Melee Heroes with high
>    defense in the front row and Ranged Heroes with high damage in the back row.

## Counter & Pool (id `3003308433`)
> 1. In the mid to late game, keep an eye on your opponents' lineup and collect Heroes who can
>    counter them.
> 2. All players purchase to recruit Heroes from the same **Shared Hero Pool**. When you have
>    surplus Gold, consider picking Heroes that your opponents are actively collecting to delay
>    their progress toward 3-Star upgrades.

These three are the game's own statement of the strategy spine in [[schemata]]: economy-vs-tempo
(save then all-in), synergy + front/back positioning, and shared-pool denial. The convex-bet
reading: [[auto_chess_as_a_mirror]].
