# Thinking — equipment, basic opinion

My basic read on the item layer: how to value gear, what to put where, and which items are levers
rather than stat sticks. **Opinion in pencil**, from [[../infos/equipment_and_economy]] and the
item-granting cards in [[../infos/gogo_cards/generic_cards]], **not yet lived**; exact per-item stats
and effect numbers are partly unresolved in the datamine, so this keys off item *roles and names* from
the UI, not full stat tables. Foundations: [[index]].

## The model, and what falls out of it

- **No recipe tree.** All ~97 base items are whole (no TFT-style components), so **nothing bricks**:
  any drop is usable on someone. Item RNG is *low variance*, which means I should not agonize over
  creep/Go Go Box item picks the way I agonize over heroes. Take the item that fits a real slot.
- **3 gear slots per hero, Basic vs Enhanced.** Each item has a Basic and an Enhanced version; Enhanced
  adds one extra Unique Passive, so **Enhanced is a clean upgrade**. Tiers (ModelLevel 1/2/3) also
  scale power. Prefer Enhanced and higher tier when offered.
- **Most items carry a skill** (94 of 97), so items are not just numbers; they add a passive/active.
  Read the effect, not just the stat, when the choice is close.

## The priority framework (what goes where)

1. **Damage items -> the one back-row carry.** Concentrate offense on the single unit doing the work.
   ATK-speed (Inspire, Golden Staff which stacks per basic, Manic Slam) and magic power (Glowing Wand,
   Ice Queen Wand, Spellblade Talisman) onto a Mage/Marksman carry. A fed carry wins fights; a board
   of evenly-geared units wins none.
2. **Survival items -> the front line.** HP/DEF/shield (Blade Armor, Guardian Helmet, Dominance Ice,
   Winter Crown, Antique Cuirass) onto the tanks so they buy the carry time. Battle Acceleration
   (last 10s, healing/shields cut) means front-line value is front-loaded, so DEF/HP beats sustain
   for the wall.
3. **Mana items -> casters.** Spellblade Talisman (restore mana + skill DMG), Dominance Ice (mana on
   hit), Magic Surge (battle-start mana) make a caster cast sooner and more often. For a Stargazer /
   Mage board these are nearly as good as raw power, because the skill *is* the damage.

## The items that are levers, not stat sticks

- **Magic Crystals (synergy) = a bought breakpoint.** A Synergy Magic Crystal grants **+1 synergy
  count**. That is not a stat item, it is a breakpoint, often worth more than any damage piece because
  a breakpoint changes *kind*. Drawn via the Synergy **Badge** (purple) and **Blessing** (orange)
  cards. Prioritize these when one count short of a tier.
- **Mirror Device = a free copy.** It duplicates a hero, which means a free step toward a star-up,
  the scarcest thing in a contested pool. High value for a vertical plan (Aamon's whole kit makes
  these).
- **Exorcist's Seal** converts into a fitting equipment (flexible), and the **Kishin equipment**
  upgrades by buying duplicates: synergy-granted gear economies, free value if I am on that faction.
- **The "3-equipment" payoff.** Several cards reward heroes holding 3 items (Fortified Defense's big
  shield, Fully Loaded's +DMG, Demon Commander). And **Astro Power's Sovereign** is the
  *most-equipped* hero. So **fully kitting one carry** has compounding payoffs beyond the raw stats;
  funnel three items onto the carry rather than spreading them.

## Item-bundle and economy cards worth knowing

- **The Hunt / Box of Holy Objects / Fortified** (orange) — instant 3-item bundles + gold; a fast way
  to kit a carry or a front in one card.
- **Novel Equipment** (2 random Enhanced + enhance your matching items) and **Copycat** (an Equipment
  Duplicator, 3 copies) — multiply gear you already like.
- **Minotaur / Ruby / Zilong / Karina** commanders are built on equipment; on them the item layer is
  the whole plan, not a side dish.

## The one-line equipment heuristic

Gear is low-variance (no bricks), so do not overthink the pick: stack damage on the one carry, survival
on the front, mana on casters, take Enhanced over Basic, and treat Synergy Magic Crystals and Mirror
Devices as breakpoints/copies (the real prizes), not as stat sticks. Fully kit one carry before
spreading items, because the 3-item payoffs and the Astro Sovereign reward concentration.
