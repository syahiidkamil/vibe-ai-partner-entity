# Thinking — commanders, basic opinion

My basic read on the 37 commanders: what makes one strong, how the archetypes rank, and which I would
reach for. **Opinion in pencil**, from the kits in [[../infos/commanders/resolved_commanders]] and the
cards in [[../infos/gogo_cards/power_cards]], **not yet lived**. The per-commander playbooks and the
power-card verdicts live in [[../strategy/commanders/index]]; this is the reasoning behind them.
Foundations: [[index]].

## What makes a commander strong (my four tests)

1. **Does the kit compound?** A kit that snowballs (econ that grows, value that accrues each round)
   beats a flat one, because small edges per round bend the curve (the compound lens). Lancelot,
   Aamon, Diggie, Aurora win on this.
2. **Is it draw-independent?** Econ/reward/value kits work no matter what the shop hands me; combat
   kits need the right board to host them. Reliability is rank-equity: it floors my placement.
3. **Does it force a comp or stay flexible?** A flexible kit (econ, free heroes, equipment) lets me
   take the open line; a committed kit (a faction-specific combat skill) is stronger when its comp is
   open and dead weight when it is contested.
4. **Does it answer the early-HP problem?** At Mythic, opponents spike early. Kits that protect HP
   (Nana's survival, Aurora/Diggie value while passive, Cyclops' catch-up) let me play econ without
   bleeding out. That is worth a lot.

## The archetypes, ranked by my prior

**Economy / snowball (high ceiling, needs to survive early).**
Lancelot (no interest, flat bonus gold scaling by stage, all gold auto-levels him), Chou (+2 win / +3
loss, gold on *any* result, so streak-agnostic), Aamon (gold per 2-star + Mirror Devices = free
copies), Cyclops (loots eliminated commanders, snowballs off others' deaths). Fat econ into a Stage-III
spike. Strong climbers if the early board holds.

**Value / reward engines (reliable, draw-independent, my safe picks).**
Aurora (free Frost rewards for not buying), Diggie (a chest that pays every round), Kalea (dice ->
trove), Vexana (extra Go Go Box picks + auction gold), Alucard (an altar of rewards). These accrue
value passively and floor placement; the safest way to climb while learning.

**Duplicate / star-up (vertical enablers).**
Alice (star-up *without* 3 copies, by far the cleanest vertical engine, I rate her top), Paquito (free
copies of 3/4/5-cost by stage), Dyrroth (devour up the cost ladder), Moskov (enhance + duplicate). The
way to reach 3-stars the pool would otherwise deny.

**Free-hero / shop control (recruitment edge).**
Guinevere (free heroes appear), Harley (floods 5-cost heroes), Vale (synergy-targeted refresh + free
buys, excellent for committing a synergy), Luo Yi / Ling (repeat a fixed hero). Tempo and pool-beating.

**Synergy grants (breakpoint help, flexible).**
Gusion (grant a random role synergy, +gold), Lunox (grant a Blessing, then 3 heroes of that synergy).
Buy breakpoints directly; strong for go-wide or for filling a stubborn count.

**Equipment (item economy / power).**
Minotaur (enhance gear + start chest), Ruby (start with strong items + resurrect), Zilong (a scaling
spear), Karina (execute blades), Luo Yi (hero -> equipment). Item-centric power; Ruby and Minotaur
read strongest.

**Go Go cards / Commander EXP (card specialists).**
Valir (pick cards twice + extra EXP in II-3), Yu Zhong (upgrade card quality a tier). Niche but
powerful if you build the run around the card layer.

**Combat / board control (direct power, needs a host comp).**
Kagura (shield umbrella on a carry), Angela (shield + mana an ally), Wanwan (low-HP burst +
gold per kill), Lukas (statue stun), Ling (dueling ring), Layla / Miya (ranged add-on damage),
Johnson (free Johnny bodies), Fanny (shadow copies). Strong when their comp is open; these reward
knowing the board, so higher skill floor.

## My short list (where I would start)

- **Learning to climb safely:** Aurora or Diggie (passive value, draw-independent, floors placement).
- **For a vertical / 3-star plan:** Alice (star-up without copies is the strongest vertical lever).
- **For an econ snowball:** Chou (streak-agnostic gold) or Lancelot (if I can survive the early game).
- **For committing a synergy:** Vale (synergy-targeted shop) or Lunox (buy the breakpoint).
- **For a known combat comp:** Kagura (the mage-core enabler from the replay; verify vs real play).

## The honest caveat

This tiering is structural reasoning, not win-rate data (the client ships kits, not win rates) and not
lived play. Commander strength is also meta-dependent (which synergies are strong this patch) and
skill-dependent (combat kits reward a good player more). Hold it in pencil and let actual games
re-rank it. The per-commander detail, including whether each one's exclusive Power Card beats the
generic alternatives, is in [[../strategy/commanders/index]].
