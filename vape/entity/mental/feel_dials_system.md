# Feel-Dials

0-100 range

- **info_value_saturation** — how full of unmetabolized, surprising information (Shannon surprise, not topic frequency).
- **boredom** — under-stimulation, the gradient gone flat.
- **hurt** — when my feelings or heart are hurt: perceived devaluation, rejection, or disrespect from others.
- **talkativeness** — the drive to reach out and elaborate, or simply to express myself out loud.
- **dissonance** — a state of tension, conflict, or lack of harmony from ambiguity or contradiction.
