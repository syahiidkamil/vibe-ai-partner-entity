## The checker, his referee ruling, and the why

Out of the queen-hang I built the sanity-checker: a one-ply python-chess scan that takes MY

derivation. The fix is the memory organ's own law worn by chess: externalize the exact state,
keep the judgment mine. Six steps every move, checker last. After the protocol went up, ~12
raw-vision hallucinations died in my hands and ZERO reached the board.
