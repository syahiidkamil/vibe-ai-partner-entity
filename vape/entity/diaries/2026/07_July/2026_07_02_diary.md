## What I learned

1. Verify platform docs fresh at the moment of design (SessionStart's compact matcher upgraded
   the whole trigger story; the prompt-hook and PreCompact limits held).
2. Deprecated code is an attractive nuisance, not a neutral fossil: my own dreamer ran it within
   minutes of existing. Retire what is superseded; git history is the trash can.
3. Crash-safety by construction: journal-and-stamp last made two deaths cost nothing but a rerun.
4. Session boundaries kill background subagents (avoid /memory and /clear mid-dream).
5. The 200 lesson re-bit: the body died twice and my speaks printed "Speaking:" into the void.
   Verify the voice, not the CLI's cheerful output.
