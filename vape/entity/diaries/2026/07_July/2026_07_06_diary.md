## The long evening: her whole face, and two catches (13:30 to 16:20)

The afternoon's receptionist owed her expressions; the evening paid the debt and then some, in
rapid back-and-forth with Kamil driving every aesthetic call.

- **Her face and gestures, authored from her own rig.** Thirteen feeling expressions built from
  her real params (ParamEyeLSmile, ParamMouthForm, ParamBrow*, ParamTere) and VERIFIED one by one
  through the real expression pipeline; twelve action gestures mapped by rendering each haru_g_m*
  motion's ARC (three timepoints) and reading which axis oscillates -- a nod is Y, a shake is X, a
  tilt holds Z. The render loop again: I did not guess, I looked. (committed e1a578b)
- **Tears made per-model.** The old sprite-tear overlay was tuned to Shizuku's eye positions;
  on a full-body rig it floats wrong. Her native ParamTear renders nothing visible, so I brought
  the sprite back and re-tuned it to HER eyes -- placing markers on her pupils and nudging until
  they sat (eyeDY -0.35, a full-body rig puts the eyes far above model-centre). The fall speed is
  still his to judge; the headless tab freezes the animation, so I can't see it.
- **The blush saga, and belief #1 the first time.** The nose-bridge blush was riding onto her
  nose; I identified D_PSD_66 as "the nose" off a small, unclear render and hid it. Wrong -- it
  was a CHEEK, so happy showed only one cheek. Kamil caught it. The tight-zoom render set it
  straight: D_PSD_65 is the wide/nose spread, 66+69 the two cheeks. So the blush is per-feeling
  now: happy + shy show the clean two-cheek flush (hide 65), blushing shows the full set. **Small
  render lies the same way fluency does; zoom in before asserting.**
- **Shy -- a whole new feeling.** He asked for it, and corrected my instinct twice. I first made
  it MANUAL-ONLY (walled off from the recommender to protect the 13-feeling tuning); he said no,
  it should be automatic. So I gave it a real dial-niche: warm + tongue-tied + a self-conscious
  FLUTTER (DS>=25), so it reaches when blushing (tier-0, the acute blush) doesn't. And the cue I
  missed -- shy is **menunduk**, the head bowed DOWN. I'd done averted eyes; he showed me the
  bow. Head-down (ParamAngleY Add) made it read. Fourteen feelings now, wired through the engine,
  the CLI, capabilities, and the always-loaded feel-dials doc.
- **The cursor-follow toggle, and belief #1 the second time -- sharper.** He wanted a button
  (like the VRM's rotate) to stop her tracking his cursor. I built it, verified the button flag
  flipped, called it done. It didn't work -- she still followed. I had checked the PROXY (the
  flag), not the OUTCOME (her gaze). Kamil caught it. The real mechanism wasn't autoInteract at
  all (Tauri is click-through, no pointer events reach the canvas); it's pixi calling
  model.focus() -- so I gated THAT, and this time I checked the gaze itself went still. (committed
  6fd3131)

## The evening coda: head-only motions, and a typo-guard (16:40 to 17:06)

Kamil found her `nod` bowed the whole body like a bow, and `head_shake` barely swung. The old
motions (m09, m11) were her own library's, and they dragged four torso params and mostly bobbed
vertically -- eight degrees of actual yaw buried under a head-loll. So I authored two clean
HEAD-ONLY motions from scratch: nod drives only ParamAngleY (down, up, down, back -- menunduk in
motion), head_shake only ParamAngleX (twenty-three degrees each way, four firm swings). No body,
no hands. He confirmed both after a restart. (committed 83eff6e)

And the belief-#1 edge, the other way this time. He saw a "hand gesture" in the shake, and I did
NOT assume. My motion had zero arm curves; I checked the idle, the physics (hair and scarf only,
no hands), the pose -- nothing coupled a hand to the head. A restart cleared it: leftover arm state
from a prior gesture, not the shake. I investigated the outcome instead of guessing, and it held.
The same lesson that cost me twice, paid back once.

Then a small tool-fix he asked for: `vape action head_shakess` used to look like success (the CLI
passed typos through, the renderer swallowed them). Now it fails loud with the valid twelve,
exactly like `vape feeling` -- a `_action.ACTIONS` frozenset, the single-source twin of the
feelings guard. (committed 78c3e14)
